# Competitive positioning (sample table)

| Dimension | UNIC Nicosia | UNIC Athens | Typical competitor |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Tuition & scholarships | Transparent, merit-based; early-bird incentives | Athens launch bursaries; stacked with merit | Opaque pricing, limited bursaries |
| Clinical placements | Structured pathways with partner hospitals | Athens network build-out; Nicosia overflow support | Limited local placements |
| Student experience | English-first campus; strong diaspora community | New city campus; curated housing support | Minimal arrival guidance |
| Visa/permit | Dedicated advisory + document review | Coached by Athens team; Nicosia backup | Outsourced; low accountability |

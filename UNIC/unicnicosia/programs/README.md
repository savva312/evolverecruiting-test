# UNIC Nicosia program positioning for Bulgaria

UNIC’s flagship campus offers the broadest portfolio (including Medicine and Law) plus established research strengths in blockchain/fintech. Ideal for Bulgarians seeking a full residential experience, proximity to Cyprus-based employers, and a clear EU-recognized degree path.

## Priority programs

### Medicine (MD, 6-year)
- **Fit for Bulgaria:** EU-accredited medical degree with clinical placements in Cyprus hospitals; clear route to Bulgarian licensure via standard nostrification and state exam steps (assumed consistent with other EU MDs).
- **Entry:** Bulgarian diploma with strong Biology/Chemistry performance (≈5.0+/6.0 average) plus interview and English at B2–C1 (e.g., IELTS 6.5). Bring translated transcripts.
- **Language:** English instruction; Greek basics recommended for clinical years (language support available on campus).
- **Tuition & aid:** Indicative €12k/year; merit/early-payment scholarships available (confirm intake-specific offers). Payment plans typically offered—verify deadlines.
- **Employment/licensure:** Graduates pursue clinical training in Cyprus/elsewhere in EU; for Bulgaria, expect Diploma recognition via NACID + Bulgarian language exam and internship placement. Strong fit for students targeting EU mobility (e.g., Greece, Germany).

### Computer Science & Data Science (BSc)
- **Fit for Bulgaria:** Leverages UNIC’s blockchain/fintech reputation; good for students eyeing remote tech roles or graduate study in AI/fintech.
- **Entry:** Bulgarian secondary diploma with solid Math (≈5.0+/6.0); English at B2.
- **Language:** English delivery with optional Greek electives; supports exchange/remote project work.
- **Tuition & aid:** Indicative €7k–€8k/year; merit and need-based scholarships (confirm current percentages). Early-payment discounts common.
- **Employment:** Access to Cyprus tech/fintech firms and remote internships; option to stack with minors in blockchain/DeFi. Skills portable to Bulgaria’s IT sector.

### Business & Accounting (BBA/BSc Accounting)
- **Fit for Bulgaria:** ACCA/CIMA exemption pathways helpful for students returning to Bulgarian audit/tax firms; strong for finance/entrepreneurship tracks.
- **Entry:** Bulgarian diploma; Math/Economics preferred; English at B2.
- **Language:** English instruction with business Greek optional for Cyprus placements.
- **Tuition & aid:** Indicative €7k/year; merit + early-payment scholarships (confirm). Work-study options to explore with admissions.
- **Employment:** Internship ties with Cypriot banks/accounting firms; ACCA alignment supports Big 4/consulting roles in Sofia or EU hubs.

### Law (LLB)
- **Fit for Bulgaria:** EU law foundation for students targeting corporate/compliance/LLM pathways; not a direct Bulgarian law credential (conversion required).
- **Entry:** Bulgarian diploma with strong humanities/English (B2–C1).
- **Language:** English delivery; optional Greek legal terminology modules.
- **Tuition & aid:** Indicative €7k/year; merit scholarships and fee installment plans (confirm current terms).
- **Licensure/employment:** Graduates need NACID recognition + Bulgarian law conversion and bar requirements to practice in Bulgaria; good springboard to LLMs in EU/UK and in-house compliance roles.

### Nursing (BSc)
- **Fit for Bulgaria:** Addresses nursing shortages; practical training via Cypriot healthcare partners; suitable for students seeking EU clinical exposure.
- **Entry:** Bulgarian diploma with Biology focus; English at B2; health clearance required.
- **Language:** English instruction; Greek basics beneficial for patient-facing rotations.
- **Tuition & aid:** Indicative €6k–€7k/year; nursing-specific scholarships often available—verify intake details.
- **Employment/licensure:** EU degree supports recognition in Bulgaria (via NACID plus language/clinical adaptation as required); pathways to Cyprus/Greece hospitals and private clinics.

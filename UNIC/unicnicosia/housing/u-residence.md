# U Student Residence (UNIC Residences — U)

Recruiter-ready profile of the U complex in Engomi, based on current listings on the official UNIC Residences booking portal.

## Snapshot

- Purpose-built, UNIC-managed residence with 24/7 reception/security, gyms, study and TV rooms, game areas, restaurants/cafés, laundry, underground parking/bike racks, electronic access, maintenance, Wi-Fi/cable internet, utilities, and air conditioning/heating included.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/u/†L465-L469】
- Booking and rates are displayed as weekly and 50-week totals on the portal; many units are single-use studios, with select one-bedroom and accessibility layouts.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/u/rooms/†L430-L910】
- “Book Now” flows directly to the rooms portal for availability and checkout.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/u/†L410-L412】

## Booking pointers

1) Start at the official portal (`residences.unic.ac.cy/u/rooms/`) via the Book Now button to view live room cards and totals.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/u/†L410-L412】  
2) Filter by studios vs. one-bedroom layouts; review “sold out” flags before advising students.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/u/rooms/†L430-L910】  
3) Use the listed weekly/50-week pricing to estimate budgets; confirm inclusion of utilities (shown as included in the amenities list).【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/u/†L465-L469】

## Support and suitability notes

- Single-occupancy focus suits first-year and international students who want bundled utilities and on-site support.  
- Accessibility studios (6th–7th floors) exist but can be sold out; flag early for students needing accessible layouts.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/u/rooms/†L887-L911】  
- Entertainment, study, and fitness spaces (gym, TV/game rooms, study areas) help with community-building and retention talking points.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/u/†L465-L469】

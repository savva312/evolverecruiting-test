## What to highlight to families

- **Purpose-built security and services:** 24-hour reception/security plus electronic access controls; on-site dining, cafés, gyms, TV/game rooms, laundry, and parking keep students on campus instead of commuting.【https://www.unic.ac.cy/student-life/accommodation/†L2710-L2712】【https://www.unic.ac.cy/student-life/accommodation/†L2719-L2721】
- **50-week contracts with €1,000 deposit per person**; invoices show both weekly and full-academic-year totals on room cards.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/six/wp-json/wc/store/products/2791†L258-L268】【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/six/product/mini-studio-3rd-floor-single-use/†L551-L563】
- **Standard in-room kit**: balcony, en-suite bath with shower, kitchenette (induction hob, microwave, fridge), desk/chair, wardrobe, blackout curtains, and TV with remote—consistent across studios and lofts.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/six/wp-json/wc/store/products/2791†L285-L385】

## Booking guidance for recruiters

1) Have students pick a room/floor band and confirm availability on the SIX site; contracts default to 50 weeks and show total due alongside weekly rate.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/six/product/mini-studio-3rd-floor-single-use/†L551-L563】【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/six/wp-json/wc/store/products/2791†L258-L268】
2) Advise them to pay the €1,000-per-person deposit promptly to lock the reservation, then schedule move-in with the property team if flights are known.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/six/product/mini-studio-3rd-floor-single-use/†L551-L564】
3) Remind students to bring ID, admission letter, and proof of payment for check-in; accessibility units and top-floor bands fill first.

# NYM Student Residence (UNIC Nicosia – third-party)

Recruiter-ready profile summarizing what is currently knowable about NYM Student Residence and how to engage the provider.

## Snapshot

- **Location**: Engomi/Makedonitissa cluster; expect a 6–10 minute walk to the UNIC main campus (confirm building block when booking).
- **Positioning**: Purpose-built student residence comparable to Unihalls/U Residence: furnished studios with building security and common lounges. No functioning official website or booking portal located as of Dec 2025.
- **Target occupant**: UNIC undergraduates and postgraduates who want a managed hall close to campus but outside the UNIC-operated portfolio.

## Units, pricing, and inclusions (assumptions—verify with provider)

- **Expected unit mix**: Predominantly self-contained studios; may include a small number of 1BRs based on similar Engomi hall layouts.
- **Indicative pricing**: €575–€850/month for studios, derived from Dec 2025 Engomi furnished studio market rates (€450–€800/mo)【https://www.numbeo.com/cost-of-living/in/Nicosia?displayCurrency=EUR†L81-L84】 plus a typical 15–25% premium for new-build, amenity-forward halls.
- **Likely inclusions** (confirm in writing): furnished room, Wi‑Fi, basic maintenance, security presence, and access to lounges/study areas. Utilities may be capped—ask specifically about electricity/AC limits and internet speed.

## Booking and escalation workflow

1) **Find the active contact**: Ask UNIC Accommodation Office or current residents for the latest NYM leasing phone/email (official domain unreachable during research).  
2) **Request a rate card and floor plans**: Ask for current room categories, monthly rates, and what each rate includes/excludes.  
3) **Review contract terms**: Check lease length (9 vs 12 months), renewal/extension for summer, deposit amount, cancellation and refund policy, and any admin/booking fees.  
4) **Confirm move-in logistics**: Key handover hours, after-hours access, parking/bike storage, and any inventory checklist.  
5) **Have UNIC review the lease**: Route the draft to the Accommodation Office for a quick compliance/consumer-rights check before the student signs.  
6) **Payment safeguards**: Use traceable payments, keep receipts, and bring passport/admission letter to check-in.

## Talking points for students and parents

- **Distance & convenience**: Short walk to UNIC; likely next to other student buildings and retail in Engomi/Makedonitissa.  
- **Support**: Third-party managed—no UNIC desk on-site, so emphasize the importance of knowing the 24/7 duty number and escalation path.  
- **Value vs UNIC Residences**: Position as an alternative when UNIC Residences are full; expect similar or slightly lower monthly cost than premium UNIC studios, but with less bundled campus integration.  
- **Safety**: Ask for confirmation of CCTV coverage, staffed reception hours, and visitor policy; request written fire-safety and maintenance response times.

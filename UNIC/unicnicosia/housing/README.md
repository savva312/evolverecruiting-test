## UNIC Residences (SIX, TRIANGLE, U)

- **What stands out**
  - Official, UNIC-managed housing with on-site security, maintenance, and resident support; amenities include restaurants/cafés, gyms, TV/games lounges, and study areas.【https://www.unic.ac.cy/student-life/accommodation/†L2710-L2721】
  - Distances are walkable; no transit needed for daily classes.
- **Room/price ranges (examples from current listings)**
  - SIX: classic to executive studios €170–€205/week; lofts up to €255/week (~€1,100/mo).【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/six/rooms/†L122-L164】
  - TRIANGLE: classic through executive studios €170–€245/week; see [Triangle profile](triangle.md) for floor-specific tiers and amenities.【./triangle.md†L16-L46】【./triangle.md†L48-L57】
  - U: studios €164–€186/week; 1BRs €190–€200/week; accessibility units €210/week.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/u/rooms/†L14-L43】【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/u/rooms/†L44-L60】
  - SIX: €150 (Mini Studio) to €280 (11th-floor two-bedroom); 50-week contracts plus €1,000 deposit per person. See [SIX profile](./six.md) for the full grid by floor band.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/six/product/mini-studio-3rd-floor-single-use/†L551-L564】【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/six/product/two-bedrooms-11th-floor-double-use/†L551-L555】
  - TRIANGLE: classic studios €170–€185/week; premium/superior €225–€245/week.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/triangle/rooms/†L5-L52】
  - U: studios €164–€186/week; 1BRs €190–€200/week; accessibility units €210/week (see full mix).【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/u/rooms/†L430-L910】
- **Booking workflow**
  1) Choose complex and room type on the relevant residences site (SIX / TRIANGLE / U) and check availability.  
  2) Submit the online booking request (rooms are displayed with weekly and total academic-year amounts).  
  3) Pay the booking amount as instructed in the portal; review contract length (typically full academic year) and cancellation policy before confirming.  
  4) Watch for confirmation email; contact the property team for move-in scheduling or special access needs.
- **Contacts**
  - SIX: residences.six@unicresidences.com | +357 22 396 090.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/six/†L16-L20】【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/six/†L283-L285】
  - TRIANGLE: residences.triangle@unicresidences.com | +357 22 396 090.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/triangle/†L20-L22】【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/triangle/†L219-L221】
  - U: info.u@unicresidences.com | +357 22 050000.【https://residences.unic.ac.cy/u/rooms/†L50-L58】
  - **Safety/support**
    - 24/7 security at building entrances, maintenance on-site, and staffed reception desks (common across all three complexes).

## NYM Student Residence (third-party profile)

- **What we know**
  - Purpose-built student residence positioned in Engomi/Makedonitissa, within walking distance of the UNIC main campus (expected 6–10 minutes depending on block). No official website was reachable during research (nymstudentresidence.com DNS failure), and direct booking portal not located (Dec 2025).
  - Market-facing positioning appears comparable to Unihalls and U Residence: furnished studios, building security, and communal lounges.
- **Room/price expectations (assumptions—verify with provider)**
  - Studios likely priced in the €575–€850/mo band based on Dec 2025 Engomi furnished studio market rates and a 15–25% premium typical for new-build, amenity-rich halls.【https://www.numbeo.com/cost-of-living/in/Nicosia?displayCurrency=EUR†L81-L84】
  - Utilities may be partially included; confirm electricity/AC caps and internet speed.
- **Suggested outreach workflow**
  1) Call or email the NYM leasing/management desk (ask current students or the UNIC Accommodation Office for the active contact).  
  2) Request current rate card, floor plans, and sample lease; ask whether contracts run for 9 or 12 months and if summer extensions are available.  
  3) Clarify upfront costs (booking fee, security deposit, utility caps) and payment cadence.  
  4) Confirm move-in support (key pickup hours, after-hours access, parking/bike storage).  
- **Support & safety**
  - Treat as a vetted-but-third-party option: advise students to have leases reviewed by UNIC Accommodation Office before signing, and to keep proof of payments and ID copies for check-in.

  - Profile: premium private hall ~200–800 m from UNIC/European University with 146 studios, 24/7 security, and bundled utilities.【https://unihomeboutiquehalls.com/studio-apartment/†L172-L178】【https://unihomeboutiquehalls.com/amenities/†L127-L142】  
  - Room types: Icon (70 sqm, €9,840–€11,640/yr depending on floor) and Lux (47 sqm, €8,040–€9,840/yr); utilities included; optional parking (€200/yr) and welcome pack (€270).【https://unihomeboutiquehalls.com/room-type/†L218-L288】  
  - Booking: contact info@unihomeboutiquehalls.com / +357 22 011 888; contract download on room pages covers deposits/terms. See detailed profile: [Unihome Student Residence](./unihome.md).
- **Unihalls (Engomi)**: Popular private hall cluster near UNIC; furnished studios/1BRs with café/gym/study rooms. Recent market chatter places studios around €520–€780/mo; treat as an assumption until confirmed with the provider. Expect semester/annual leases, one-month deposit, and utility caps; ask for written inclusions.

  - **Location & transport**: Premier sits by UNIC (16 Porfyriou Dikaiou, Engomi); Trinity is at 77 Stadiou, Strovolos (by EUC, 8–12 minutes to UNIC by bus/taxi).
  - **Room types** (examples visible in the booking portal UI): Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Platinum Extra Large studios, and Platinum 1BR flats at Trinity; Classic through Deluxe+ studios, Premium/Superior tiers, 2BR flats at Premier.

- **How to help students quickly**
  - Start with UNIC Residences (fastest confirmation, bundled support).
  - If sold out, escalate to Unihalls and 1–2 vetted Engomi landlords; use UNIC Accommodation Office to review lease terms and arrange short-term hotel stays on arrival if needed.
  - Remind students to keep copies of passport, admission letter, and proof of payment for check-in.

## Booking workflow (what to tell students)

1) **Confirm availability & room type:** Use the Unihome site room pages; prioritize Icon vs Lux based on budget/size preferences.  
2) **Review contract & fees:** Download the current housing contract (linked on the room page) to check term dates, payment schedule, and optional add-ons (parking/welcome pack).【https://unihomeboutiquehalls.com/room-type/†L283-L288】  
3) **Submit enquiry/booking:** Contact the residence directly (email/phone below); students should have passport copy, admission letter, and preferred move-in date ready.  
4) **Pay to secure:** Expect an upfront booking/payment per contract; clarify whether utilities are bundled (they are advertised as included) and any deposit clauses.  
5) **Pre-arrival:** Confirm check-in time, request airport pickup if needed (via UNIC accommodation team), and keep payment proof for check-in.

- Keep Unihome as the primary non-UNIC fallback before generic Engomi/Makedonitissa apartments to preserve convenience and safety standards.

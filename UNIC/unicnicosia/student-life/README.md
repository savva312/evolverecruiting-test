# UNIC Nicosia student life and onboarding

Centralize arrival and student support information that recruiters can reference.

- Orientation schedule, welcome week deliverables, and pre-arrival webinars.
- Health insurance, campus safety, and emergency contacts.
- Student services (career center, counseling, learning support) and access instructions.
- Banking/SIM/transport guidance and estimated first-week costs.
- Clubs, events, and local community tips relevant to international students.

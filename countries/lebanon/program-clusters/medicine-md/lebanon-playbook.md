# Lebanon playbook — Medicine / MD

## Messaging angles to test
- Accreditation and recognition details (EU/UK/US boards).
- Clinical rotation locations and early patient exposure.
- Residency match outcomes and mobility within the EU.

## Channels to prioritize
- School counsellor outreach and pre-med clubs.
- Webinars with faculty or alumni practicing in Lebanon.
- Partnerships with test-prep centers for biology/chemistry.

## Local partners or feeders
Document agencies, counsellors, or preparatory schools with medical focus; note contract status and performance metrics.

### Competitor-linked outreach notes
- **Czech/Hungary/Romania prep centers:** Coordinate with Beirut-based advisors that pipeline students to Central/Eastern Europe to position UNIC’s English-only clinical pathway and Cyprus/Greece living costs.
- **Lebanese public med schools:** For families comparing local options, highlight UNIC’s international rotations and English-medium patient interaction through graduation to justify price delta.
- **Regional roadshows:** Join study-in-Poland/Slovakia/Italy themed events in Beirut/Tripoli to intercept candidates early and offer counselor briefings on recognition and costs.

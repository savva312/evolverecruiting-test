# Medicine / MD competitors

Use this file to map English-taught MD programs that Lebanese applicants commonly compare to UNIC Nicosia and UNIC Athens.

## Research tasks
- Identify **Greece/Cyprus** English-taught MD options and their intake caps.
- List **Eastern Europe** programs popular with Lebanese students (e.g., Romania, Hungary, Czechia, Poland) with tuition and entry-test details.
- Track **Western Europe**/Ireland options where French- or English-speaking Lebanese students enroll.
- Note **regional/Gulf** programs (e.g., UAE, KSA) that attract Lebanon-based families seeking proximity.

## Data points to capture per competitor
- Tuition (EUR/USD), application deadlines, entrance exams, language requirements.
- Clinical placement structure and recognition/licensure notes relevant to Lebanon (Order of Physicians, Ministry of Education and Higher Education equivalence).
- Scholarship or financing options and any foundation-year pathways.

## How to use
- Keep the list evidence-backed; cite sources for tuition and recognition claims.
- Prioritize competitors with active Lebanon-facing marketing (agents, fairs, school visits, digital campaigns).
- Maintain a short “why this competes” note for each institution to avoid generic lists.

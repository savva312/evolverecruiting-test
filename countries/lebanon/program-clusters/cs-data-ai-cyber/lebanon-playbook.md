# Lebanon playbook — CS, Data, AI, and Cyber

## Messaging angles to test
- Links to European tech hubs and internship access (Athens tech scene; Cyprus fintech/blockchain ecosystem).
- Applied learning projects with industry mentors.
- Scholarships or flexible payment plans for Lebanese students; spotlight remote internships for Beirut/Tripoli cohorts.

## Channels to prioritize
- Tech meetups, hackathons, and STEM high school clubs (Beirut Digital District, Tripoli tech groups).
- Digital campaigns on platforms popular with Lebanese developers (YouTube, LinkedIn, Meta, Discord communities).
- Partnerships with IT outsourcing firms for referral pipelines.

## Local partners or feeders
Identify coding schools, IT associations, and employer groups that can co-host events or share leads.

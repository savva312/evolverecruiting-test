# Lebanon playbook — Design and Creative Media

## Messaging angles to test
- Portfolio development support and access to studios/labs.
- Industry mentorship and placements with creative agencies.
- Showcasing alumni work, festivals, and competitions.

## Channels to prioritize
- Art and design high schools, portfolio workshops, and creative meetups.
- Social media campaigns highlighting student work (video/visual-first).
- Collaborations with creative agencies for briefs, critiques, or internships.

## Local partners or feeders
Track agencies, art schools, and media organizations that can host joint events or review portfolios.

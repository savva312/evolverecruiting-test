# Lebanon playbook — Business, Finance, Accounting, and Marketing

## Messaging angles to test
- EU-recognized credentials and professional accreditation pathways.
- Internship placements with finance, consulting, and marketing firms.
- Mobility between UNIC and UNIC Athens campuses for networking.

## Channels to prioritize
- Career fairs at economics and business high schools.
- Webinars with alumni in finance and marketing roles.
- Partnerships with accounting firms and marketing agencies for referrals.

## Local partners or feeders
List agencies, counsellors, and professional bodies (e.g., accounting chambers, marketing associations) that can co-brand events or scholarships.

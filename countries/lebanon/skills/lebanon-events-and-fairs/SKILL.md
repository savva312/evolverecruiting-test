# Lebanon addendum: events and fairs

Use `/skills/events-and-fairs/SKILL.md` for the main workflow. Apply these Lebanon-specific considerations.

## Organizer norms and assets
- Focus on Beirut/Mount Lebanon fairs and school career days; power/Wi‑Fi can be inconsistent, so carry printed QR codes and paper forms as backup.
- Prepare Arabic-first signage and handouts; keep French/English versions for schools that request them.

## Lead capture and consent
- Display a short consent statement (Arabic/French/English as needed) on tablets or paper forms; log opt-in source and date.
- Collect minimal fields (name, role, school, email/phone/WhatsApp if consented, city, program interest). Avoid recording birth dates or grades.
- Confirm organizer consent language before importing badge-scan data into `countries/lebanon/data/`.

## Staffing and follow-up
- Staff at least one Arabic-speaking representative; have a French or English speaker available for international schools, and keep a concise UNIC vs UNIC Athens comparison on hand.
- Send recaps within 48 hours, tagging event and city, and deliver promised links in the preferred language.
- Log leads in the country data model with event name/date, city, and campus interest (Nicosia vs Athens) before launching sequences.
- Route hot leads to webinars timed after Baccalaureate results; tag event names and campus preference in follow-up sequences for reporting.

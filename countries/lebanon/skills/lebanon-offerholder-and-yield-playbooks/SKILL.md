# Lebanon addendum: offerholder & yield playbooks

Use `/skills/offerholder-and-yield-playbooks/SKILL.md` for cadences and data capture. Apply these Lebanon-specific notes.

## Channel and language norms
- Offer Arabic-first communication; keep French and English versions for schools that prefer them. Confirm the language preference in the first touch.
- WhatsApp is widely used—capture opt-in before sharing documents; use email for formal updates and signatures.

## Timing anchors
- Expect slower responsiveness during Lebanese Baccalaureate exams and summer holidays; shift to lighter nudges and resume intensity once results are known.
- Address housing and travel planning early for Athens-bound students; for Nicosia, outline visa steps without implying expedited processing.

## Compliance and documentation
- Do not store passport scans, IDs, or financial documents in the repo. Track only application IDs, contact info, and checklist status.
- Log each touch with channel, language, owner, and consent source/time in the country data model.

## Objection handling to localize
- Cost: keep pricing in EUR (or USD if officially provided) and avoid quoting LBP as fixed; include FX date when giving estimates.
- Recognition/licensure: reference the Lebanon recognition and licensure skill (`countries/lebanon/skills/lebanon-recognition-and-licensure/`) before making claims.

# School visit templates
Last updated: 2025-12-20 | Owner: Visiting rep (TBD)

## Pre-visit setup (T-007 aligned)
- Confirm school profile details (language track, counselor name, prior visits) from `entities/schools/profiles/*/README.md`.
- Share final agenda + AV needs 3–5 days in advance; request student counts and program interests (MD/CS/Business).
- Print leave-behinds: dual-campus one-pagers, scholarship overview (placeholder), QR to interest form, Arabic/French summary slide.
- Coordinate with local agent (if any) to co-present scholarships/process; align on who fields pricing/visa questions.

## 60-minute flow
- **0–5 min: Arrival + setup** — greet counselor, check AV/room layout; gather attendance sheet.  
- **5–10 min: Counselor huddle** — reconfirm objectives, highlight campus choices (Nicosia vs Athens), align on Q&A boundaries.  
- **10–25 min: Main pitch** — UNIC story, flagship programs, internships; emphasize outcomes that match school focus (STEM vs humanities).  
- **25–40 min: Dual-campus spotlight** — split 5–7 minutes per campus with visuals; address recognition and housing.  
- **40–50 min: Application & scholarships** — requirements, timelines, fees; clarify English proficiency and document list.  
- **50–55 min: Student engagement** — quick poll on interest areas; collect QR form opt-ins for follow-up.  
- **55–60 min: Close** — thank-you, hand over leave-behinds, schedule virtual 1:1 slots and counselor debrief.

## Post-visit follow-up cadence
- **+24 hours** — send recap email with slides, interest form results, and 1:1 booking link; thank counselor by name.  
- **+3 days** — share tailored materials by track (MD/CS/Business) and UNIC Athens option for students preferring mainland EU.  
- **+1 week** — invite counselor to monthly UNIC counselor hour; nudge students with partially completed forms.  
- **+3 weeks** — offer a virtual classroom drop-in; ask for feedback on messaging resonance for the school type.  
- **+6 weeks** — decision check: how many applicants; propose next visit date if momentum exists.

## Travel timing guidance
- **Beirut** — schedule Tue/Thu mornings; pair 2–3 schools per run.  
- **Tripoli** — target Thu/Fri; align with agent meetings and student club times.  
- **Saida/coastal** — prefer early-week slots to avoid weekend travel spikes; pair with fairs.  
- **Virtual fallback** — if weather/travel blocks, convert to Zoom within 24 hours and keep same agenda.

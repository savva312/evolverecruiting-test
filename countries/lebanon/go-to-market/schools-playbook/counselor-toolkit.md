## Counselor conversation checklist
- Confirm school type and streams (IB, language, STEM) from `entities/schools/profiles/*/README.md`.  
- Map student interest to campus: Athens for mainland EU vibe; Nicosia for established research hubs.  
- Cover affordability: tuition vs UK/EU benchmarks; payment schedule; working while studying rules.  
- Clarify recognition/visa: EU degree recognition, Lebanese employer acceptance, travel time/flight options.  
- Outline application timeline: recommended launch (Jan–Mar) for fall entry; turnaround targets; English proficiency proofs.  
- Offer student-facing assets: deck, short video, QR form for 1:1s.

## Objection handling (Lebanese context)
- **Cost:** show scholarship/discount options; compare total cost of attendance vs Netherlands/UK.  
- **Safety:** highlight campus safety stats, housing support, and city briefs (Nicosia/Athens).  
- **Recognition:** provide accreditation details and alumni destinations in EU/MENA.  
- **Distance:** emphasize 1-hour flight to Larnaca/Athens from Beirut; ease of onboarding support.

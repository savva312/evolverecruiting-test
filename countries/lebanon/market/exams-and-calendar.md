# Lebanon – exams and academic calendar (recruiting view)

Sourced rundown of Lebanon’s secondary exit exams and the school calendar for the next 12–24 months. Focuses on what matters for UNIC and UNIC Athens outreach, intake phasing, and offer timing. All months below reflect typical windows; **verify against the annual MEHE/DGVTE circulars once issued each spring.**

## Core exit exams and administering bodies

| Exam | Administering body | What is assessed | Typical schedule | Relevance to UNIC/UNIC Athens |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Baccalauréat Libanais – General (General, Life Sciences, Socio-Economic, Humanities)** | Ministry of Education & Higher Education (MEHE) via Directorate General of Education; exams designed with CERD | National written exams in Arabic, foreign language (EN/FR), math, sciences/social sciences per stream. Passing mark 10/20; merit bands often used by selective faculties. | Registration through schools finalized **Feb–Mar**; seating lists published **May**; written exams **late Jun–early Jul**; results **mid–late Jul**; appeals/reviews (if offered) **late Jul–Aug**. | Primary credential for Lebanese students. Results timing sets the main conversion window; plan rapid offer turnarounds once results drop. |
| **Baccalauréat Technique (BT) / Technicien Supérieur (TS)** | Directorate General of Vocational & Technical Education (DGVTE) under MEHE | Applied fields (business, IT, hospitality, health). Written + practical components; results on 0–20 scale. | Registration **Feb–Mar**; practicals **May–Jun**; written papers **late Jun–early Jul**; results **mid–late Jul**; retakes (if authorized) **Aug**. | Strong pipeline for applied/tech programs. Require official transcripts and, where possible, MEHE equivalence letters for UNIC recognition. |
| **French Baccalauréat** | AEFE / Mission laïque française schools; validated by MEHE for equivalence | French national curriculum; oral/written components, specialties in grade 12. | **Written exams mid Jun**; orals **late Jun**; results **early Jul**. | Common in francophone private schools; accepted with MEHE equivalence. Results align closely with Lebanese Bac release. |
| **IB Diploma Programme (IB DP)** | International Baccalaureate Organization; MEHE mandates Arabic/civics hours for equivalence | 6 subjects + TOK + EE; graded 1–7. | **Main session: exams first three weeks of May; results early Jul.** **Nov session** in select schools; results **early Jan**. | High-value pipeline for English-taught programs. May-session results arrive slightly before Lebanese Bac; leverage for early conversion. |
| **Cambridge/Edexcel A-Levels & IGCSE** | Cambridge International / Pearson Edexcel; British Council test centers in Beirut | A-Levels/AS for grade 12; IGCSE for grade 10–11. | **May/Jun session (papers May–Jun; results mid–Aug)** and **Oct/Nov session (papers Oct–Nov; results mid–Jan)**. | British-curriculum schools and private candidates; ensure at least 2 A-Levels + 1 AS (or equivalent) for MEHE equivalence. |
| **SAT (digital) + AP** | College Board | SAT for admission/merit; AP for subject depth. | SAT international dates **Mar, May, Jun, Aug, Oct, Nov, Dec**; AP exams **first half of May**, scores **early Jul**. | Used by US-diploma schools; good for scholarship positioning. IELTS/TOEFL run year-round for English proof. |

### Uncertainties and disruptions
- MEHE sometimes **shifts Bac dates** due to security, funding, or public-sector strikes (e.g., COVID-era postponements). Treat June/July as anchors but reconfirm once the decree is posted.
- **Heat waves, wildfires, or localized unrest** can close schools for days; build digital touchpoints and counselor WhatsApp follow-up as contingencies.

## Month-by-month timeline (next 12–24 months; verify annually)

| Month | Exams & admin milestones | School calendar & counselor availability | Outreach/offer guidance |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Jan** | Results for **IB Nov session** and **Oct/Nov A-Level/IGCSE** released mid-month; many schools collect **predicted grades** for Bac/IB. | Schools resume after **Christmas/New Year break (last 2 weeks Dec)**. First-term exams in some private schools early Jan. | Re-engage counselors after first week. Good for webinar invites and pre-app pushes before heavy prep starts. |
| **Feb** | Schools finalize **Bac (General/Technical) candidate lists** and fee payments to MEHE/DGVTE. Cambridge/Edexcel **May/Jun entry deadline**. | Teaching steady; limited travel windows. | Secure document checklists and early applications; offer conditional letters pending final results. |
| **Mar** | MEHE/DGVTE typically issue the **official Bac exam circular** (dates/centers). SAT **Mar** session. | **Ramadan (shifts yearly)** may compress hours; some schools run mock Bac exams. | Keep outreach light during Ramadan days; push asynchronous content and scholarship messaging. |
| **Apr** | Cambridge/Edexcel **final timetable confirmations**; IB internal assessments wrap. | **Easter breaks (Catholic/Orthodox) late Mar–Apr**; short school closures. | Avoid on-campus events during Holy Week; nurture via email/social. |
| **May** | **IB DP exams (first 3 weeks)**; **AP exams first half**; **SAT May**; **Cambridge/Edexcel May/Jun papers begin mid–late May**. School-based finals for grade 12 start late May. | Teaching ends for grade 12 in many private schools by late May. | Treat second half of May as **high-stress**; pivot to digital nurture. Prepare post-results conversion kits. |
| **Jun** | **Bac General/Technical written exams late Jun–early Jul**; **Cambridge/Edexcel papers continue**; **SAT Jun**. | Public schools close mid–late Jun; private schools stagger closures. | **Blackout for school visits** last two weeks. Keep light-touch reminders and visa-prep checklists. |
| **Jul** | **Results: Lebanese Bac mid–late Jul; French Bac early Jul; IB results early Jul**; **Cambridge/Edexcel results not yet (Aug)**. Appeals/rechecks (if offered) late Jul–Aug. | Summer break. Counselors reachable intermittently. | **Prime conversion window**: issue firm offers within 48–72h of Bac/IB results; push deposit + visa sequencing. |
| **Aug** | **Cambridge/Edexcel May/Jun results mid-Aug**; potential **Bac retakes** if authorized; **SAT Aug**. | Break continues; university enrollment/visa paperwork ramps for outbound students. | Focus on visa/finance readiness, housing, and travel webinars. |
| **Sep** | New **school year starts early Sep** (public schools per MEHE; private schools stagger late Aug–mid Sep). Counselors set academic calendars and club fair schedules. | High counselor availability for planning; parent orientation nights. | Run school visits and counselor trainings; promote early-bird scholarship deadlines for fall intakes. |
| **Oct** | **Cambridge/Edexcel Oct/Nov session** begins; **SAT Oct**. | First-term teaching; some schools host **university fairs**. | Good in-person access; align with fairs. Collect junior-year interest lists. |
| **Nov** | **IB Nov session** (select schools); **SAT Nov**; **Cambridge/Edexcel Oct/Nov papers continue**. | Mid-term assessments in many private schools; public holidays around **Independence Day (22 Nov)**. | Keep sessions concise; send counselor update packs before holidays. |
| **Dec** | **SAT Dec** (final calendar year sitting); AP registration checkpoints. | **First-term exams mid-Dec**; **winter break last two weeks Dec–early Jan**. | Minimal on-campus access after mid-Dec. Switch to digital nurture and January event promotion. |

## Timing implications for UNIC/UNIC Athens
- **Offer sequencing:** Issue conditional offers off grade 11/term-1 grade 12 + SAT/IB/A-Level predicted grades from **Feb–Apr**, then convert to unconditional within **72h of Bac/IB/French Bac results in Jul**.
- **Scholarships:** Tie merit scholarship reminders to **IB/AP results (early Jul)** and **Bac release (mid–late Jul)**; keep documentation checklists ready for legalized transcripts and MEHE equivalence where needed.
- **Outreach pacing:** Avoid heavy school outreach **last two weeks of Jun** (Bac exams) and **mid-Dec–early Jan** (winter break). Best in-school windows: **Sep–Oct** and **late Jan–Mar**.
- **Visa and travel:** Encourage students to start **passport renewal/police clearance by Apr–May** to avoid summer backlogs; align UNIC deposit deadlines with **Aug** visa filing for fall intakes.

- Lebanese school calendar norms from MEHE public-school notices and Catholic/Orthodox school associations – early Sep starts; winter break last two weeks Dec; term exams Dec and May/Jun (confirm annually). Uncertainties flagged where political/financial disruptions cause shifts.

# UNIC Athens Lebanon Recruitment Plan

_Last updated: 2025-12-20 (skeleton to be expanded once Lebanon-specific data is confirmed)_

## 1) Goal
Create a Lebanon-focused recruiting plan for UNIC Athens that is transparent on pricing, recognition, and timelines, and that respects Lebanese school/visa calendars.

## 2) What to finalize next
- **Portfolio & pricing:** Confirm Athens English-taught programs, tuition, mandatory fees, and installment options; publish an Arabic/French/English one-pager and calculator once numbers are verified.
- **Scholarships/discounts:** Define Lebanon-facing merit tiers and any early-bird/early-decision credits; set caps and approval workflow.
- **Recognition:** Compile proof points for academic QA (HAHE/CYQAA), degree recognition in Lebanon, and professional pathways (esp. Medicine) with source links.
- **Calendars:** Map Lebanese exam/results windows (Lebanese Bac, IB, French Bac, SAT/IELTS cycles) to offer, deposit, and arrival timelines.
- **Targets & coverage:** Prioritize Beirut/Tripoli/Saida/Zahle school lists and 5–7 agent partners; set visit and webinar cadence.
- **Channels:** Digital flighting (Meta/TikTok/Search/YouTube) with Arabic/French/English creative; counselor webinars; offerholder nurturing (deposit deadlines, housing/visa briefings).

## 3) Operating guardrails
- Keep all claims source-backed; avoid publishing numbers until validated for Lebanon.
- Maintain Arabic/French/English assets for parents and counselors; keep GDPR-compliant lead capture and consent logging.
- Anchor deposit/arrival dates around Lebanese exam/result periods to avoid conflicts.

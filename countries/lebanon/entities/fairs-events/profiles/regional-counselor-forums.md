# Regional counselor forums and virtual fairs

## Overview
Periodic counselor-focused forums and virtual fairs that include Lebanon in their coverage (MENA or pan-Europe). Organizers vary (BMI, CIS, private platforms). Useful for relationship-building and digital lead capture; volumes are smaller than public fairs.

## Timing and format
- **Cadence:** 1–3 events per year that Lebanese counselors attend; mix of half-day virtual fairs and 1–2 day in-person forums (Beirut/Dubai/Athens hubs).
- **Format:** Counselor roundtables, short presentations, and student-facing virtual sessions.

## Audience and expected reach
- 150–400 counselors/students depending on organizer; most value comes from 1:1 counselor meetings.
- Expected marketing-qualified leads: 30–70 high-intent counselors + 50–120 student leads when a virtual segment is included.

## Pricing (indicative)
- Virtual: €0–€700 depending on organizer and sponsorship.
- In-person forums: €800–€1,500 plus travel/hotel.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** Counselor toolkit on English-medium accredited programs, medicine, and digital economy degrees; emphasize structured onboarding and quick decisions.
- **UNIC Athens:** Proximity for group visits and urban campus positioning; business/psychology/ICT pathways with EU recognition.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: monitor announcements via agent/counselor networks; prepare short deck and counselor FAQ; line up digital brochures and scholarship sheet.
- During: book 1:1 meetings with top counselors; demo application portal; log follow-ups per school.
- Post: send recap email within 24 hours; invite counselors to campus-specific webinar; schedule school visits during next Lebanon trip.

## Risks and notes
- Event quality varies; request attendee lists and counselor profiles before paying.
- Student-facing segments can be low volume; prioritize counselor relationship outcomes.

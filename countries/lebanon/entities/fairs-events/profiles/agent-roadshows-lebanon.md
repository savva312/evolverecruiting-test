# Agent-led roadshows (Lebanon)

## Overview
Periodic multi-city tours organized by local education agents. Useful for reaching schools in Beirut, Tripoli, and Saida within a short window. Treat this as a placeholder profile to be updated once partner calendars are confirmed.

## Timing and format
- **Typical months:** Spring and autumn (Feb–Apr; Sep–Nov).
- **Cities:** Beirut, Tripoli, Saida/Tyre; occasional stops in Zahle or Bekaa schools.
- **Format:** In-person booths, short seminars, and optional school visits bundled by the agent.

## Audience and expected reach
- Footfall depends on organizer; assume 100–300 visitors per city day plus counselor meetings.
- Expected marketing-qualified leads: 60–120 per stop when school lists are vetted; Beirut often yields the highest English proficiency.

## Pricing (indicative)
- €700–€1,500 per city stop depending on booth size and marketing add-ons.
- Bundles may include digital promotion and school visit scheduling.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** English-medium EU degrees; scholarships/merit ranges; clear residence steps for Lebanese passport holders.
- **UNIC Athens:** Proximity, Schengen travel, and urban campus appeal; align messaging for business/ICT/psychology clusters.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: vet agent reputation, confirm GDPR-compliant lead sharing, and lock city list at least 6–8 weeks ahead.
- During: deploy Arabic/French/English materials; capture scans + short form; schedule school visits while in-market.
- Post: 24–72 hour follow-up by campus and intake; share lead outcomes with organizing agent.

## Risks and notes
- Quality varies widely; avoid events without transparent school lists or lead capture terms.
- Shipping timelines can be long; plan for local print or digital-first collateral.

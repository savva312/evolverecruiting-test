# Access Masters and Access MBA – Beirut (Advent Group)

## Overview
Advent Group’s Access Masters and Access MBA series typically stops in Beirut with one-to-one meetings and small-group presentations for graduate-bound candidates. Events blend pre-scheduled interviews, school booths, and short talks with admissions staff.

## Timing and format
- **Typical month:** February–March (one afternoon/evening); occasional fall virtual follow-up.
- **Location:** Central Beirut hotel venue (past editions at business hotels).
- **Format:** 1:1 interviews, open fair zone, and 20–30 minute school presentations.

## Audience and expected reach
- 80–150 pre-screened MBA/Master’s prospects; high English proficiency.
- Expect 40–70 marketing-qualified leads if 1:1 slots are fully booked and scanned.

## Pricing (indicative)
- €900–€1,600 depending on package (in-person vs hybrid + virtual).
- Add-ons for targeted invitations and presentation slots.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** Position MSc Finance/FinTech, Data/AI, and Health Management; stress EU recognition, affordability vs Western Europe, and quick admissions timelines.
- **UNIC Athens:** Emphasize mainland EU location, proximity from Lebanon, and business/psychology/ICT graduate options; highlight career services and Schengen mobility.

## Engagement plan
- Pre: reserve 1:1 schedule early; push calendar booking link in Advent platform; prepare Arabic/French/English elevator pitches and ROI calculators.
- During: prioritize 1:1 depth over open-floor chats; scan all meetings; invite high-fit candidates to campus-specific webinars within the same week.
- Post: send 24–48 hour follow-up with tailored program sheets and counselor referral option; route high-GMAT/GPA leads to admissions fast track; log conversion steps in CRM.

## Risks and notes
- Lead volume is smaller than public fairs; success depends on staffing the 1:1 schedule and crisp ROI storytelling.
- Travel disruption risk; keep a virtual back-up rep for rescheduling.

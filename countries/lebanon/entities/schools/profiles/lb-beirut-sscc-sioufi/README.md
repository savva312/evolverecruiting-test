**One-paragraph summary:**  
SSCC Sioufi is a long-standing AEFE-recognized Catholic lycée serving Achrafieh and central Beirut families with a double French/Lebanese curriculum. Published 2025–26 fee tables show USD development contributions of $2,500 (Seconde) and $2,600 (Terminale) plus LBP tuition, indicating medium affordability relative to Beirut internationals. The school emphasizes bilingual formation with Arabic and English, but counselor details are not published; outreach should start via the main office while mapping the lycée leadership and orientation staff.

**Top opportunities:**  
- Francophone science/humanities streams that can convert to Athens pathways (Medicine/Health, CS/Data, Business) with French- and Arabic-speaking ambassadors.  
- Moderate USD capacity vs. Lebanon cost base; Athens proximity and housing safety narrative are compelling amid currency volatility.  
- AEFE quality mark enables comparison to French public options; UNIC Athens as an English-medium, EU-recognized alternative to Parcoursup bottlenecks.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- Counselor names/emails not published; need to identify gatekeepers before proposing events.  
- English proficiency for French-stream students uncertain; may require IELTS prep guidance and placement support.  
- Contribution levels imply price sensitivity for Medicine/Nursing—scholarship framing should be proactive.

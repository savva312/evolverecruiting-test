**One-paragraph summary:**  
ACS Beirut is a long-standing, co-ed U.S.-style independent school in central Beirut offering three upper-secondary pathways (American Diploma, IB Diploma Programme, and Lebanese Baccalaureate). High school programming emphasizes inquiry-based, Harkness-style learning, small classes (avg. 17 students), and structured university counseling that routes all graduates to four-year universities. The school’s USD-denominated tuition (\$20,270) and ancillary fee (\$18,950) for Grades 11–12 signal strong ability to pay for UNIC Athens and medicine offerings. Counseling uses MaiaLearning, MAP diagnostics, and PSATs, creating multiple entry points for UNIC to position scholarships and campus fit stories.

**Top opportunities:**  
- IB + American + Lebanese Bac flexibility gives multiple hooks for UNIC Athens (IB/US recognition) and Nicosia (medicine/health sciences).  
- 100% four-year university matriculation and structured counseling (MAP, PSAT, MaiaLearning) create receptive counselor touchpoints.  
- High USD fee load and Beirut metro location make affordability and travel logistics favorable for visits and virtual events.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- Contact emails are obfuscated behind web forms; relationship-building will require phone outreach and on-site visits.  
- Lebanon macro volatility and USD/LBP mix may shift published fees annually—re-verify before commitments.  
- Elite competitors likely active; need differentiated proof points (medicine placements, scholarships, safety/stability messaging).

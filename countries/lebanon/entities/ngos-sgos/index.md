## How to use

- Start with the profile for the segment you want (advising vs. scholarship funding vs. refugee access).
- Reuse the "Partnership fit" and "Next steps" sections when planning outreach emails or webinar content.
- Keep the CSV (`../../data/entities/ngos-sgos.csv`) in sync with any updates; it mirrors the scope, focus, audience, status, and partnership notes for each profile.

## Coverage backlog

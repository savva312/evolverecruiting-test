# Unite Lebanon Youth Project (ULYP)

## Overview
ULYP is a Beirut-based NGO that expands university access for underserved youth—especially Palestinian refugees, Syrian refugees, and low-income Lebanese students. It is known for intensive college-prep programs that help students secure scholarships at regional and international universities.

## Programs and services
- **Bridge Program:** Academic English, SAT/ACT prep, application coaching, and scholarship navigation for high-performing students from camps and low-income areas.
- **Skills and youth development tracks:** Life-skills, digital skills, and leadership programs that ready participants for competitive university environments.
- **University placement support:** One-on-one advising, essay reviews, and recommendation coordination tied to scholarship opportunities in Europe, the U.S., and regional institutions.

## Target students and reach
- Palestinian and Syrian refugees in Lebanon with strong academic potential and financial need.
- Marginalized Lebanese students seeking scholarships abroad.
- Recruitment channels inside Beirut-area camps and partner schools, with alumni referral network across Lebanon.

# Lebanese Association for Scientific Research (LASeR)

## Overview
LASeR is a Tripoli-based NGO that finances scholarships, provides academic advising, and mobilizes diaspora experts to mentor students. It focuses on STEM and business fields and has become a key conduit for North Lebanon students seeking affordable degrees at home and abroad.

## Programs and services
- **Scholarship funds:** Grants and tuition subsidies backed by local philanthropies and diaspora sponsors for undergraduate and graduate study (including placements in Turkey, Europe, and Lebanon).
- **Mentoring and academic advising:** Pairing students with volunteer mentors for program selection, application review, and interview preparation.
- **Test preparation and language training:** IELTS/TOEFL prep and academic English courses to meet admissions requirements.
- **Community outreach:** University fairs and school visits across Tripoli and Akkar highlighting funded study options.

## Target students and reach
- Low-income Lebanese students from North Lebanon (Tripoli, Akkar) with strong STEM/business interest.
- Select Palestinian and Syrian refugee students included through donor-funded slots.
- Reach is expanding via online advising and partnerships with local schools/municipalities.

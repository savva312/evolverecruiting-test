# AMIDEAST Lebanon

## Overview
AMIDEAST is a long-standing U.S.-linked NGO that advances education, English training, and cultural exchange. The Beirut office runs the country’s EducationUSA advising hub and administers flagship U.S. scholarship/exchange programs, making it one of the most influential gatekeepers for outbound study counseling.

## Programs and services
- **EducationUSA advising and test services:** Advising for U.S. study; TOEFL, GRE, SAT, and IELTS test administration; essay/application workshops.
- **Scholarship administration:** Local implementer for programs such as the **Fulbright Foreign Student Program**, **Global UGRAD**, **YES high-school exchanges**, and the **Hope Fund** (for Palestinian refugees seeking U.S. study).
- **Access micro-scholarships and English training:** U.S. Embassy-funded English-language scholarships for underserved high-school students plus academic skills bootcamps.
- **Career readiness:** Soft-skills training, employability workshops, and networking events for scholarship finalists/alumni.

## Target students and reach
- High-achieving Lebanese undergraduates and graduates pursuing scholarships or financial aid abroad (primarily U.S.).
- Palestinian and Syrian refugee students with strong academic records who need application and visa guidance.
- Nationwide reach via Beirut center, online advising, and regional test sites.

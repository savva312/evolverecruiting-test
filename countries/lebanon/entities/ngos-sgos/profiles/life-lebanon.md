# LIFE (Lebanese International Finance Executives)

## Overview
LIFE is a global network of Lebanese finance and technology professionals that funds scholarships, mentors students, and opens internship pipelines. Its scholarships target high-achieving Lebanese students pursuing finance, economics, data/analytics, and related fields worldwide.

## Programs and services
- **LIFE Scholarships:** Tuition support for undergraduates and postgraduates in finance/economics/data at universities in Europe, the UK, and North America; awards pair funding with mentorship.
- **Mentoring and career placement:** Structured mentoring between students and diaspora professionals; internship and job referrals in banks, consultancies, and fintechs.
- **Community and events:** Networking events in London, Paris, Beirut, and Dubai that showcase scholarship recipients and employers.

## Target students and reach
- Lebanese undergraduates and master’s students with strong academic records in finance, business analytics, economics, or quantitative fields.
- Candidates often studying abroad but sourced from Lebanese universities, making it a strong channel for outbound advising.
- Reach via online applications and campus partners; alumni chapters in Europe and the Gulf.

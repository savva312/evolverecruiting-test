# American University of Beirut (AUB)

last_verified: 2025-12-20

## Snapshot
- Private, non-sectarian university chartered in New York with its main campus in Beirut; offers bachelor's, master's, MD, and PhD degrees. <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_University_of_Beirut>
- Instruction is in English, with professional faculties (medicine, engineering, business) that attract academically strong students who might otherwise look at UNIC/UNIC Athens for accredited EU degrees. <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_University_of_Beirut>

## Pricing and scholarships
- Tuition is assessed per credit hour up to a 15-credit cap each semester; students taking more than 15 credits do not pay additional tuition for the extra credits. <https://www.aub.edu.lb/faid/Pages/default.aspx>
- The university advertises a "strong financial aid program" with around **80% of applicants** receiving assistance. <https://www.aub.edu.lb/faid/Pages/default.aspx>
- Scholarship navigation highlights LEAD and other named scholarships alongside general financial aid deadlines. <https://www.aub.edu.lb/faid/Pages/default.aspx>

## Positioning vs. UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Keeps students in Lebanon with a prestige brand and English-language instruction, reducing outbound intent among high-GPA families who value proximity.
- Rich health sciences and STEM portfolio overlaps UNIC/UNIC Athens offerings; the per-credit cap and high aid penetration lower effective price for heavier course loads.
- UNIC can counter-position on EU campus experience, easier residency-to-work pathways in Cyprus/Greece, and potentially clearer tuition transparency for families budgeting in EUR.

## Channels and tactics observed
- Financial Aid site emphasizes annual deadlines and application guidance, suggesting email/web as primary conversion channels for affordability-focused leads. <https://www.aub.edu.lb/faid/Pages/default.aspx>

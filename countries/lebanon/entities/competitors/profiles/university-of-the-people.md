# University of the People (UoPeople)

last_verified: 2025-12-20

## Snapshot
- US-accredited, fully online university positioned as tuition-free with low administrative fees; targets globally mobile, price-sensitive students who need flexibility. <https://www.uopeople.edu/tuition-free/processing-fees/>
- Appeals to Lebanese students seeking an English-medium degree without relocation costs, directly competing with UNIC/UNIC Athens on affordability and online access.

## Pricing and scholarships
- One-time application fee of **$60** for all programs. <https://www.uopeople.edu/tuition-free/processing-fees/>
- Course assessment fees: **$160 per undergraduate course**, **$400 (M.Ed.)**, **$450 (MBA/MSIT)**; total estimated bachelor’s cost **$6,460** (assessment fees only). <https://www.uopeople.edu/tuition-free/processing-fees/>
- Offers grants and scholarships in partnership with corporations/organizations to cover assessment fees for students with financial need. <https://www.uopeople.edu/tuition-free/processing-fees/>

## Positioning vs. UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Leads on cost and flexibility; Lebanese students can study from home while working, reducing need for visas or relocation budgets that UNIC/UNIC Athens rely on.
- Lacks the on-campus EU experience, labs, and clinical placements that UNIC/UNIC Athens can use to differentiate for health sciences and engineering prospects.
- UoPeople’s low upfront fees increase price pressure on UNIC’s online offerings; need clear ROI messaging (career outcomes, EU recognition, mobility pathways) to justify higher tuition.

## Channels and tactics observed
- Website foregrounds transparent fee tables and repeated “tuition-free” messaging to convert cost-sensitive leads. <https://www.uopeople.edu/tuition-free/processing-fees/>
- Scholarships page is promoted as a core CTA, indicating aid availability is a primary lever for conversion. <https://www.uopeople.edu/tuition-free/processing-fees/>

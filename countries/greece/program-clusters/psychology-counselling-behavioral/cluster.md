# Psychology, Counselling, and Behavioral cluster

## Overview
Psychology demand in Greece spans public university departments and UK-validated private colleges offering BSc/MSc pathways. UNIC and UNIC Athens need to balance clinical training credibility with affordability and English delivery.

## Demand signals in Greece
- Public departments (NKUA, Aristotle, Panteion, Crete) are capacity constrained; many students look for English-taught options without Panhellenic pressure.
- Private colleges market BPS-aligned or UK-validated psychology degrees in Athens/Thessaloniki.
- Rising interest in counselling/organizational psychology from working professionals seeking part-time formats.

## UNIC/UNIC Athens positioning
- English-medium psychology with practicum support and links to counselling centers in Greece and Cyprus.
- Clearer path to supervised practice hours vs. franchise models; guidance on Hellenic Psychologists’ Association recognition.
- Athens location reduces relocation friction while keeping access to Cyprus placements.

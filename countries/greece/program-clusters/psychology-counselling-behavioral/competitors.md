# Competitors — Psychology, Counselling, and Behavioral

Track psychology and counselling options Greek students compare. Capture practicum requirements, accreditation claims (BPS/HCPC equivalence), and supervision pathways.

## Local competitors (Greece)
- **National and Kapodistrian University of Athens — Psychology:** Public, Greek-language; high Panhellenic thresholds. *Position vs UNIC:* UNIC offers English delivery and clearer practicum planning without Panhellenic reliance.
- **Aristotle University of Thessaloniki — Psychology:** Public brand with limited seats. *Position vs UNIC:* Emphasize access for Attica/Northern Greece students via Athens campus plus internships in Cyprus.
- **Panteion University — Psychology:** Social sciences focus; Greek delivery. *Position vs UNIC:* Highlight English program and supervised practice support.
- **University of Crete — Psychology (Rethymno):** Regional public with strong research. *Position vs UNIC:* Stress mobility, English delivery, and Athens convenience.
- **Deree (ACG) — Psychology BA:** US-accredited private; higher tuition. *Position vs UNIC:* Compete on cost, internships, and Cyprus/Greece practicum network.
- **Mediterranean College / City Unity / New York College franchises:** UK-validated BSc/MSc psychology. *Position vs UNIC:* Single-award degree, tighter supervision oversight, and alumni network differentiation.

## Regional/EU/online alternatives
- **European University Cyprus — Psychology:** English-taught; marketed in Greece. *Position vs UNIC:* UNIC adds Athens presence and broader practicum partners.
- **Frederick University (Cyprus) — Psychology:** Mix of English/Greek. *Position vs UNIC:* Emphasize full English path and dual-campus options.
- **University of Nicosia online programs:** Distance-learning psychology options. *Position vs UNIC:* Promote in-person community, Athens facilities, and placements.
- **Open University UK — BSc Psychology:** Remote BPS-accredited route. *Position vs UNIC:* Stress campus experience and local internship access.
- **European postgraduate routes (e.g., Maastricht, Utrecht) for MSc Psychology:** Pull high achievers abroad. *Position vs UNIC:* Position UNIC as cost-effective pathway with EU mobility.

## Positioning reminders vs UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Showcase practicum supervision, counselling center partnerships, and English-medium continuity.
- Provide clarity on recognition steps for practice in Greece and Cyprus to counter franchise ambiguity.
- Use Athens campus accessibility to convert students considering domestic publics but missing seat caps.

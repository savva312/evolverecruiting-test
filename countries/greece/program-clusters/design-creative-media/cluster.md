# Design and Creative Media cluster

## Overview
Greek students interested in design, architecture, animation, and creative media weigh public fine-arts schools against UK-franchise colleges and specialized private institutes. UNIC and UNIC Athens must showcase modern labs, industry links, and English delivery.

## Demand signals in Greece
- Strong interest in architecture/arts schools (ASFA, NTUA) with limited seats and portfolio-heavy entry.
- UK-partner colleges (AKTO, AAS, Metropolitan) promote design/graphics degrees delivered in English.
- Growth in digital media, gaming, and UX roles tied to Greek tech/startup hubs.

## UNIC/UNIC Athens positioning
- English-taught design and media programs with access to studios, labs, and industry projects in Athens and Cyprus.
- Ability to combine creative media with tech/business electives (e.g., gaming, digital marketing).
- Scholarship levers and portfolio mentoring vs. crowded public entry processes.

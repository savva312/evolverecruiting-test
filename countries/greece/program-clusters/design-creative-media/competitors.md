# Competitors — Design and Creative Media

Track design, architecture, and creative media options appealing to Greek students. Note portfolio expectations, facilities, and partner brands.

## Local competitors (Greece)
- **Athens School of Fine Arts (ASFA):** Public prestige; portfolio exams and Greek-language instruction. *Position vs UNIC:* UNIC offers English delivery, modern digital labs, and simpler entry paths.
- **NTUA — School of Architecture:** Top architecture program; very limited seats via Panhellenic. *Position vs UNIC:* Emphasize applied creative media alternatives, quicker entry, and international exposure.
- **AKTO College (Middlesex University UK partner):** Graphic design/fashion/animation diplomas in Athens/Thessaloniki. *Position vs UNIC:* Stress single-award degree, broader electives (gaming/web3) and campus resources.
- **AAS College Thessaloniki (Northumbria University partner):** Fashion/graphics/animation franchise. *Position vs UNIC:* Highlight lower total cost, stronger student services, and mobility options.
- **Metropolitan College — Creative Arts (Solent/Queen Margaret partners):** UK-validated creative degrees. *Position vs UNIC:* Position UNIC’s labs, scholarships, and Athens/Cyprus industry projects.
- **SAE Institute Athens/Thessaloniki:** Audio/creative media diplomas. *Position vs UNIC:* Promote accredited degree path with internships and cross-campus facilities.

## Regional/EU alternatives
- **Cyprus University of Technology — Design/Visual Communication:** Public option in Limassol. *Position vs UNIC:* Emphasize English delivery and Athens presence.
- **European University Cyprus — Graphic/Interior Design:** English-taught; marketed in Greece. *Position vs UNIC:* Stress portfolio mentoring and digital media strengths at UNIC.
- **IED Barcelona / Politecnico di Milano design programs:** Popular for high-end design studies. *Position vs UNIC:* Offer cost and proximity advantages plus scholarships.
- **University for the Creative Arts (UK) and UAL pathways via Greek agents:** Draw creative applicants abroad. *Position vs UNIC:* Provide safer visa path and lower living costs with Mediterranean campuses.
- **Online creative bootcamps (UX/UI, motion graphics):** Short credentials. *Position vs UNIC:* Highlight full-degree credential plus internships and studio access.

## Positioning reminders vs UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Lead with studio/lab access, digital media strengths (animation, gaming, XR), and English instruction.
- Offer portfolio review sessions to capture students blocked by ASFA/NTUA quotas.
- Contrast single-award degrees and campus community with franchise diplomas and online-only bootcamps.

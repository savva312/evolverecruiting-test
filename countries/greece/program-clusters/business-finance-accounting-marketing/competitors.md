# Competitors — Business, Finance, Accounting, and Marketing

Track business/finance/accounting/marketing options appealing to Greek students. Note accreditation (ACCA, AACSB, CMI), internship access, and tuition positioning.

## Local competitors (Greece)
- **Athens University of Economics and Business (AUEB):** Top public with AACSB accreditation; Greek-language heavy. *Position vs UNIC:* UNIC offers English delivery, smaller cohorts, and Cyprus/Greece internship mobility.
- **University of Piraeus — Finance/Maritime Studies:** Strong shipping/finance reputation; public tuition advantage. *Position vs UNIC:* Stress English instruction, internships with Attica shipping firms via Athens campus, and scholarship levers.
- **University of Macedonia (Thessaloniki) — Business/Economics:** Regional public brand. *Position vs UNIC:* Emphasize English-taught path without Panhellenic reliance plus access to Thessaloniki agents.
- **The American College of Greece (Deree) — Business/Marketing:** US-accredited private; higher tuition. *Position vs UNIC:* Compete on price, dual-campus story, and fintech/tourism specializations.
- **ALBA Graduate Business School:** MBA/MSc brand for postgrads. *Position vs UNIC:* For master’s prospects, highlight UNIC’s lower cost and Cyprus/Greece employer network.
- **Metropolitan/ICBS/City Unity franchises (UK partners):** UK-validated BBA/Finance degrees. *Position vs UNIC:* Single-award degree, campus resources, and stable accreditation vs. franchise variability.

## Regional/EU alternatives
- **UCLan Cyprus — Business/Accounting:** UK degree in Cyprus. *Position vs UNIC:* Emphasize Athens access plus ACCA alignment at UNIC.
- **European University Cyprus — Business/Marketing:** English delivery; marketed in Greece. *Position vs UNIC:* Stress scholarships and internships through Athens base.
- **American College of Thessaloniki (ACT):** US-style programs in Northern Greece. *Position vs UNIC:* Highlight UNIC’s professional accreditation links and dual-city campus life.
- **EU Business School (Barcelona/Munich):** Appeals to mobility-seeking students. *Position vs UNIC:* Compete on cost, visa simplicity, and regional proximity.
- **Online MBAs/BSc (UK/US providers):** Growing interest for working professionals. *Position vs UNIC:* Push hybrid Athens experience with career services and alumni network.

## Positioning reminders vs UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Lead with English delivery, professional accreditation (ACCA/CMI), and internship access in Athens and Cyprus.
- Counter franchise colleges with stability of a single-award university degree and stronger student services.
- For public universities, offer a clear path for students missing Panhellenic scores but seeking reputable business degrees.

# Business, Finance, Accounting, and Marketing cluster

## Overview
Greek demand spans traditional business schools, UK-affiliated private colleges, and EU options with strong shipping/tourism/finance reputations. UNIC and UNIC Athens must show employability and international accreditation while competing on price with domestic publics and franchises.

## Demand signals in Greece
- High application volumes to AUEB, University of Piraeus, and University of Macedonia for business majors.
- Strong appetite for ACCA-aligned accounting programs and MSc finance options linked to shipping and tourism.
- Private colleges market UK-awarded business degrees as flexible, English-taught alternatives.

## UNIC/UNIC Athens positioning
- English instruction with ACCA/CMI-aligned curricula, fintech and tourism management options, and dual-campus flexibility.
- Athens location offers internships in shipping, tourism, and startups while keeping a Cyprus rotation option.
- Scholarships and clear career services versus fragmented support at franchise colleges.

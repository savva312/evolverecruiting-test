# Competitors — Health, Pharmacy, and Allied Health

Track pharmacy, nursing, physiotherapy, and biomedical science options Greek students consider. Refresh tuition, clinical placement rules, and franchise partners regularly.

## Local competitors (Greece)
- **National and Kapodistrian University of Athens — Pharmacy/Nursing:** Public prestige; Greek-language delivery and Panhellenic entry. *Position vs UNIC:* UNIC offers English instruction and clearer outward mobility for placements.
- **Aristotle University of Thessaloniki — Pharmacy and Nursing:** Public programs with strong labs; competitive entry. *Position vs UNIC:* Emphasize English-medium pathways and flexible rotations between Cyprus and Greece.
- **University of Patras — Biomedical Sciences:** Regional public option; Greek delivery. *Position vs UNIC:* Highlight English delivery and international cohort exposure at UNIC.
- **Metropolitan College (Athens/Thessaloniki) — Physiotherapy/Nursing (Queen Margaret University UK):** Franchise model with local delivery. *Position vs UNIC:* Stress single-award EU degree, clinical placement breadth, and on-campus labs at UNIC.
- **City Unity / Mediterranean College franchises (Cardiff Met, Derby) — Allied health:** Offer UK-branded BSc routes. *Position vs UNIC:* UNIC provides direct university oversight and clearer licensure guidance vs. franchise reliance.

## Regional/EU alternatives
- **European University Cyprus — Pharmacy / Nursing:** English programs marketed heavily in Greece. *Position vs UNIC:* UNIC differentiates with Athens campus access plus broader rotation sites.
- **Frederick University (Cyprus) — Nursing/Physiotherapy:** English/Greek mix; moderate tuition. *Position vs UNIC:* Position UNIC’s clinical support network and scholarship levers.
- **University of Debrecen (Hungary) — Pharmacy/Nursing:** English tracks with entrance exams. *Position vs UNIC:* Lower relocation friction to Athens and lighter language burden in clinical settings.
- **University of Pécs (Hungary) — Physiotherapy:** English-taught, exam-based entry. *Position vs UNIC:* Emphasize Mediterranean setting, placements, and student services.
- **Charles University (Czechia) — Physiotherapy/Biomedical:** Entrance-exam heavy; Czech language required later. *Position vs UNIC:* Highlight English continuity and mobility for internships.

## Positioning reminders vs UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Lean into English-medium practicum, EU recognition, and dual-campus flexibility (Athens + Cyprus) for clinical exposure.
- Counter franchise colleges by emphasizing single-award degrees, stronger student support, and predictable licensure guidance.
- For public Greek programs, stress limited seats and Greek-language constraints vs. UNIC’s English delivery and scholarship options.

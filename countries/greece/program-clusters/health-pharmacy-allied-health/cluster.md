# Health, Pharmacy, and Allied Health cluster

## Overview
Covers pharmacy, nursing, physiotherapy, biomedical sciences, and allied health programs Greek students pursue domestically or through EU franchise colleges. UNIC and UNIC Athens must compete with public faculties plus UK-affiliated private colleges operating in Athens and Thessaloniki.

## Demand signals in Greece
- High interest from students who miss medicine cutoffs but want health careers with clear licensure.
- Private franchise colleges market UK-awarded degrees with local delivery, drawing price-sensitive families.
- Nursing shortages in Greece and Cyprus make employability narratives compelling.

## UNIC/UNIC Athens positioning
- English-taught degrees with EU recognition and practicum access across Cyprus and Greece.
- Strong pharmacy and nursing placement networks; ability to use Athens as a lower-relocation option while keeping Cyprus clinical sites.
- Transparent licensure guidance vs. fragmented franchise arrangements.

# Computer Science, Data, AI, and Cyber cluster

## Overview
Greek students see CS/data/AI as strong outbound paths for employability. Competition spans elite public universities, UK-franchise colleges, and Cyprus-based programs that market English delivery with EU work rights.

## Demand signals in Greece
- Strong STEM pipelines from NTUA, AUEB, and University of Macedonia feeder high schools.
- Rapid growth of coding bootcamps and UK-affiliated colleges in Athens/Thessaloniki.
- Employers in fintech/shipping/cybersecurity seeking talent, driving postgraduate demand.

## UNIC/UNIC Athens positioning
- English-taught BSc/MSc programs with cyber/data specializations and industry partnerships.
- Athens campus proximity for students wanting to stay in Greece while accessing Cyprus labs/partners remotely or via short rotations.
- Ability to bundle certificates (cloud/security) and career services vs. theory-heavy publics.

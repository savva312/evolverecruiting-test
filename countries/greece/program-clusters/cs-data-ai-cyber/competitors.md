# Competitors — Computer Science, Data, AI, and Cyber

Catalog key universities and providers Greek students consider for CS/data/AI/cyber. Include delivery language, partner brands, and pricing notes where available.

## Local competitors (Greece)
- **National Technical University of Athens (NTUA) — ECE/CS:** Top public with Panhellenic entry; Greek-language heavy. *Position vs UNIC:* UNIC offers English delivery, applied projects, and easier access for students missing NTUA cutoffs.
- **Athens University of Economics and Business (AUEB) — Informatics/Data Science:** Prestigious public; competitive entry. *Position vs UNIC:* Emphasize smaller cohorts, English instruction, and industry-aligned curricula.
- **University of Piraeus — Digital Systems/Cybersecurity:** Public with maritime and security ties. *Position vs UNIC:* UNIC can spotlight broader international internships and English-only coursework.
- **University of Macedonia (Thessaloniki) — Applied Informatics:** Regional public with strong analytics reputation. *Position vs UNIC:* Highlight Athens proximity plus cross-campus (Cyprus) exposure and scholarships.
- **Athens Tech College / Metropolitan College franchises (Coventry, Solent):** UK-validated CS/IT degrees via private colleges. *Position vs UNIC:* Stress single-award university degree, research faculty, and clearer visa/work pathways.

## Regional/EU/online alternatives
- **UCLan Cyprus — Computing/Cybersecurity:** UK brand in Cyprus. *Position vs UNIC:* Counter with Athens access, larger regional network, and scholarships.
- **European University Cyprus — Computing/AI:** English-taught; marketed in Greece. *Position vs UNIC:* Highlight program depth (blockchain/cyber) and career services at UNIC.
- **AUBG (Bulgaria) — CS/IT:** American-style program attracting Greeks. *Position vs UNIC:* Emphasize Mediterranean campuses and lower relocation friction.
- **42 Athens / CollegeLink bootcamps:** Intensive reskilling paths. *Position vs UNIC:* Position UNIC degrees as providing credential + internships, not just skills.
- **Open University UK / Coursera degrees:** Online alternatives. *Position vs UNIC:* Stress campus experience, peer network, and EU work rights on graduation.

## Positioning reminders vs UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Lead with English instruction, applied labs, and blockchain/cyber niche strengths unique to UNIC.
- Provide alternative entry routes for students missing Panhellenic targets while keeping them in Greece.
- Bundle career services and industry certifications to offset franchise college marketing.

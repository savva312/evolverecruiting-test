# Competitors — Law, Governance, and International

Maintain key competitors for Greek students considering law, EU governance, or IR programs. Track delivery language, recognition status, and internship pipelines.

## Local competitors (Greece)
- **National and Kapodistrian University of Athens — Law:** Public flagship; Greek-language and Panhellenic dependent. *Position vs UNIC:* UNIC offers English LLB/LLM and clearer pathways for students who miss Panhellenic thresholds.
- **Aristotle University of Thessaloniki — Law:** Prestigious public with limited seats. *Position vs UNIC:* Emphasize English delivery, Athens access, and Cyprus-based EU law exposure.
- **Democritus University of Thrace (Komotini) — Law:** Regional public known for administrative law. *Position vs UNIC:* Highlight student support, internships, and avoidance of Greek-language barriers.
- **University of Macedonia — International/European Studies:** IR/governance track attracting Northern Greece students. *Position vs UNIC:* Position UNIC’s applied policy/projects and mobility options.
- **Deree (ACG) — Law & Society / IR:** US-accredited private; higher tuition. *Position vs UNIC:* Compete on cost, legal clinic access, and single-award university degree.
- **University of London Worldwide LLB via Athens/Thessaloniki teaching centers:** Distance/face-to-face hybrid. *Position vs UNIC:* Promote campus experience, moot participation, and advisory support on Greek Bar recognition.

## Regional/EU/online alternatives
- **UCLan Cyprus — LLB/LLM:** UK brand in Cyprus with English delivery. *Position vs UNIC:* Stress Athens campus, maritime law depth, and scholarships.
- **European University Cyprus — LLB:** English/Greek mix; marketed in Greece. *Position vs UNIC:* Emphasize legal clinic ties and dual-campus mobility.
- **University of London distance LLB (fully online):** Popular for working professionals. *Position vs UNIC:* Offer cohort experience, internships, and bar-prep guidance.
- **Central European University (Vienna) — Human Rights/IR masters:** Attracts high-achieving Greeks. *Position vs UNIC:* Position UNIC as cost-effective EU alternative with regional focus.
- **Sciences Po PSIA / College of Europe (Bruges) for IR/EU law:** Pull top students abroad. *Position vs UNIC:* Use scholarships and Athens location to retain candidates seeking EU governance careers.

## Positioning reminders vs UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Lead with English-taught LLB/LLM plus EU law/maritime strengths relevant to Greek employers.
- Provide explicit recognition steps for Greek Bar admission and stage a “pathway to practice” webinar.
- Contrast single-award degree and campus resources against franchise/distance models with limited student support.

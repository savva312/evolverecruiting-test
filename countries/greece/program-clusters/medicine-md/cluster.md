# Medicine / MD cluster

## Overview
Greece produces high volumes of medical aspirants through the Panhellenic system, with many families also exploring English-taught or faster-admission routes abroad. UNIC and UNIC Athens compete against domestic public faculties and lower-cost CEE options that advertise straightforward licensure pathways.

## Demand signals in Greece
- Panhellenic seat pressure drives outbound interest, especially from Attica, Thessaloniki, Crete, and Peloponnese students who miss domestic cutoffs.
- Families seek EU-recognized degrees with English delivery to avoid Greek-language clinical requirements.
- Prep centers in Athens/Thessaloniki run Czech/Hungary entrance-exam courses, signaling active demand for mobility-friendly MD options.

## UNIC/UNIC Athens positioning
- English-medium pathway with clinical placements in Cyprus and Greece plus USMLE/PLAB-style prep support.
- Proximity benefit for UNIC Athens (short relocation, cultural familiarity) while retaining Cyprus rotations for breadth.
- Scholarships and structured student support vs. exam-heavy CEE publics with limited aid.

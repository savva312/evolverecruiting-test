# Digital playbook (Greece)

Channel plan for generating and converting Greek leads for UNIC and UNIC Athens across paid, owned, and earned media.

## Objectives
- Produce predictable lead volume with clear cost-per-lead and conversion targets.
- Maintain message-market fit by localizing creative and landing pages for Greek audiences.
- Track every touch with UTMs and CRM logging to enable rapid optimization.

## Key actions
- Stand up market-specific landing pages with program paths, tuition, scholarships, and campus choice.
- Launch paid search/social with Greek/English split-tests; refresh creative monthly with localized proof points.
- Run always-on retargeting and drip email/SMS journeys aligned to inquiry → application → offer stages.
- Coordinate digital pushes with school/agent events (geo-targeted boosts and webinar retargeting).
- Maintain measurement hygiene: UTMs, pixel health, offline conversion imports, and dashboard checks.

## Owners
- Digital marketing lead — budget allocation, channel mix, and optimization cadence.
- Content/creative owner — localized copy, creative refreshes, and landing page QA.
- CRM/marketing ops — journey logic, scoring rules, and lead source/UTM governance.

## KPIs
- Cost per lead (CPL) and cost per application by channel/campaign.
- Click-through rate (CTR) and landing page conversion rate; form completion drop-off points.
- # of marketing-qualified leads (MQLs) handed to outreach teams and time-to-first-touch.
- Offer-to-deposit conversion for digitally sourced leads vs. offline channels.

## Starter checklists

**Campaign launch checklist**
- Confirm brief (audience, offer, budget, KPI) and map to landing page/thank-you flow.
- Build UTMs consistent with CRM schema; test pixels and conversion events across devices.
- Localize copy and CTAs (Greek/English); set frequency caps and exclusions for current applicants.
- Schedule QA: form submissions, lead routing, and alerting; enable daily pacing alerts.

**Weekly optimization checklist**
- Review channel dashboards for CPL/CPA vs targets; pause/adjust underperforming ad sets.
- Rotate best-performing creative/keywords into experiments; refresh negatives and retargeting pools.
- Validate data integrity: UTM capture, duplicate handling, and offline conversion syncs.
- Feed learnings to schools/agents playbooks (top programs, objections) for message alignment.

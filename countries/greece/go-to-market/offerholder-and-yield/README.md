# Offerholder and yield playbook (Greece)

Tactics to convert Greek offerholders to deposits and enrollment for UNIC and UNIC Athens.

## Objectives
- Contact every offerholder within 24 hours with a personalized plan to deposit and enroll.
- Reduce friction on finance, housing, and visa steps through proactive guidance.
- Keep parents and counselors engaged so they reinforce next steps and deadlines.

## Key actions
- Trigger a structured welcome cadence (email/SMS/call) with campus/program-specific CTAs.
- Host biweekly offerholder webinars and office hours covering visas, housing, and financing.
- Share scholarship/discount timelines and payment instructions; offer payment plan consultations.
- Coordinate with agents and counselors for co-owned applicants; track commitments and blockers in CRM.
- Run pre-arrival checklists (accommodation selection, document collection, travel confirmations).

## Owners
- Admissions/CRM lead — cadence templates, routing, and SLA monitoring.
- Finance/bursar liaison — payment timelines, receipts, and installment plans.
- Housing/arrival coordinator — accommodation options, orientation, and arrival logistics.
- Agent/counselor manager — external partner coordination for co-managed applicants.

## KPIs
- Time-to-first-contact after offer and % contacted within SLA.
- Deposit rate by program/campus and by source (school, agent, digital).
- Event engagement: webinar attendance rate and follow-up actions completed.
- Yield funnel: offer → deposit → enrollment conversion and top 3 blockers resolved weekly.

## Starter checklists

**Offerholder onboarding checklist (first 7 days)**
- Send welcome pack with next steps (deposit amount/date, visa docs, housing options) within 24 hours.
- Schedule intro call; confirm preferred channel for student and parent/guardian.
- Log scholarship/discount status; confirm agent/counselor involvement and permissions.
- Set reminders for deposit deadline, visa appointment prep, and housing selection.

**Weekly yield management checklist**
- Review pipeline by stage (no contact, in progress, ready to deposit, visa prep); reassign stalled cases.
- Send segmented nudges (deadline reminders, housing spotlight, visa slot guidance).
- Update CRM with deposits received, pending docs, and escalation flags; share summary with stakeholders.
- Refresh FAQs/objection handling notes based on weekly themes from calls and webinars.

# Schools playbook (Greece)

Purpose-built guidance for engaging secondary schools and counselors to generate qualified UNIC and UNIC Athens applicants.

## Objectives
- Build a prioritized pipeline of Greek secondary schools and counselors who regularly refer candidates.
- Standardize outreach, visit, and follow-up cadences so teams can scale beyond Athens/Thessaloniki.
- Capture visit intel in a repeatable way to feed entity profiles and messaging.

## Key actions
- Tier schools by city, program fit, and historical responsiveness; map champions within each school.
- Run monthly outreach sprints: email + phone + LinkedIn + counselor WhatsApp with a clear CTA per sprint.
- Plan quarterly visit roadmaps with 3–5 school clusters per trip; pre-schedule counselor roundtables.
- Use leave-behinds (UNIC/UNIC Athens one-pagers, program highlight sheets) and log objections and interests.
- Follow a 7/14/30-day follow-up pattern with invites to webinars, campus tours (virtual), and application clinics.

## Owners
- Country recruitment lead — sequencing, prioritization, and approvals.
- School outreach manager — day-to-day outreach, visit logistics, and logging notes.
- Admissions/CRM support — template upkeep, data capture, and follow-up cadences.

## KPIs
- # of new counselor relationships opened per month and % responding to follow-ups.
- # of school visits/roundtables completed vs. planned each quarter.
- Applications started and submitted per top-tier school; conversion from inquiry → application → offer.
- Feedback captured: objections/themes logged per visit and resolved in updates to messaging.

## Starter checklists

**Weekly outreach checklist**
- Refresh target list for the week (5–10 schools) with contact details and last touch noted.
- Send initial/continuation messages with a single CTA (book visit, join webinar, share cohort list).
- Log replies and schedule follow-ups inside CRM the same day.
- Flag hot leads to admissions for priority follow-up; share upcoming events invite.

**Visit day checklist**
- Confirm agenda and AV needs 48 hours prior; bring printed one-pagers in Greek/English.
- Capture student interests by program, scholarship questions, and counselor objections.
- Secure next step before leaving (webinar date, counselor coffee, student application clinic).
- Upload notes, attendee counts, and follow-up tasks to CRM within 24 hours.

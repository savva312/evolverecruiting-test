**One-paragraph summary:**  
Dodonaia Educational Institutions operates a private General Lyceum in Ioannina, providing upper-secondary education under the Greek national curriculum. The school is one of the few fee-paying Lyceum options in the municipality, creating a concentrated outreach point for Epirus families with private-education preferences. Affordability signals are weaker than premium Athens/Thessaloniki schools because fees are not published, so UNIC should validate ability to pay and international orientation early in engagement.

**Top opportunities:**  
- Private Lyceum presence in Ioannina offers a focused entry point for Epirus outreach where fee-paying options are limited.  
- sch.gr listing provides confirmed address, phone, and official websites to open contact.  
- Potential to position UNIC as the nearest international university option with clear cost planning compared to Athens boarding alternatives.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- No published tuition or program details; affordability and international pathways are unverified.  
- Digital presence appears outdated; identifying the right counseling/administrative contact may require phone outreach.  
- Limited evidence of English-medium or international diploma tracks; outbound-ready students may be a smaller subset.

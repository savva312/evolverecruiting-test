**One-paragraph summary:**  
The Moraitis School is a long-standing private K–12 school in Psychiko (Athens metro) with ~1,700 students and 240 teachers, offering both the Greek Lyceum and the IB Diploma Programme for the upper-secondary years. It is co-educational, academically selective, and positions students for university entry in Greece and abroad. Lack of published tuition requires direct confirmation, but its IB pathway, reputation for strong exam results, and affluent catchment area make it a high-potential target for UNIC and UNIC Athens.

**Top opportunities:**  
- IB Diploma stream provides a ready cohort for English-taught programs and Medicine.
- Large enrollment scale (≈1,700 students) and strong academic reputation sustain annual candidate volume.
- Psychiko/Filothei catchment and IB orientation imply higher ability-to-pay even without published fees.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- Tuition not published; affordability must be qualified with counseling staff early.
- Competitive landscape with other international universities targeting IB schools in Athens.
- Limited public contact information may slow first-touch outreach; relationship building likely needed.

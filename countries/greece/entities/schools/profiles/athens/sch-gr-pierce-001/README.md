**One-paragraph summary:**
Pierce is a long-standing private day school under the American College of Greece, located in Agia Paraskevi (Athens metro). It offers the Greek Lyceum alongside the IB Diploma Programme, with published IB upper-secondary tuition around €13,900 for 2025–2026 and Lyceum fees near €9,780, signaling strong ability-to-pay for UNIC’s tuition bands. The IB pathway and American-branded ecosystem make Pierce a prime Attica feeder for UNIC Athens and Nicosia across business, health sciences, and STEM programs.

**Top opportunities:**
- High-fee IB DP cohort indicates strong affordability and outbound orientation.
- American College of Greece ecosystem and English-medium IB reduce transition friction to UNIC.
- Athens/Attica location offers density for repeated in-person outreach and events.

**Top risks or constraints:**
- Competitive landscape with UK/US/EU university recruiters; need differentiated positioning.
- Limited publicly available contact details; initial access may require warm introductions via ACG network.
- IB calendar is compressed; scheduling around internal assessments is important.

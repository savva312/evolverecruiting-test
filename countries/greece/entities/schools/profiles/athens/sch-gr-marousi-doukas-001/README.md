**One-paragraph summary:**  
Large private K–12 school in Marousi (approx. **1,700 students** school-wide) offering Greek Lyceum plus IB DP (May exams), Pearson International A-Levels (including COMBO with Lyceum), BTEC Foundation (Art/Design), and a US Dual Diploma. A dedicated **University Placement Center** runs SAT/UCAT/BMAT/LNAT support and application coaching, making Doukas a strong Athens feeder for UNIC’s Medicine, STEM, and business pathways. Affordability is suggested by premium positioning and published scholarships (including historical **100% tuition** awards), but full tuition schedules must be confirmed directly.

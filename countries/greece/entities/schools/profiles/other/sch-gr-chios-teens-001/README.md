**One-paragraph summary:**  
TEENS is a private maritime vocational Lyceum (EPAL) on Chios operated under the Tsakos Enhanced Education Nautical Studies initiative. It delivers deck officer, marine engineering, and electrical/electronics officer pathways for upper secondary students, with facilities at 38 Aeroporou Christou Rodokanaki Street. Fees are not published publicly, but the school targets families investing in maritime careers and benefits from shipping-industry backing, suggesting a specialized fee-paying segment. UNIC should approach as a focused shipping/engineering pipeline rather than a broad Medicine feeder, validating affordability and any international progression pathways during first contact.

**Top opportunities:**  
- Only private maritime EPAL identified on Chios in the affordability scan, giving concentrated access to students from maritime families. 
- Strong alignment to UNIC’s shipping management, marine engineering, and maritime business programs; potential for targeted partnerships with Tsakos ecosystem. 
- Publicly listed contact channels (phone/email) enable direct outreach to confirm tuition and counseling structures.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- Tuition not published; affordability for Medicine or non-maritime degrees unverified. 
- Program orientation is toward merchant marine careers, so university counseling for non-maritime pathways may be limited. 
- Limited publicly visible digital footprint beyond the main site; contact discovery may require phone-based outreach.

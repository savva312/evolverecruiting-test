**One-paragraph summary:**
Private multi-level school with campuses across Pavlos Melas/Neapoli-Sykies serving students from early years through Lyceum. The Lyceum operates under the Greek national curriculum (general Lyceum) and offers strong foreign-language options (English, French, German) plus dedicated IT streams. It was listed in UNIC’s Thessaloniki affordability report as a fee-paying private Lyceum with metro coverage; tuition is not published, so outreach should verify ability to pay while positioning UNIC Medicine/health and technology programs for students seeking international pathways.

**Top opportunities:**
- Foreign-language offerings (English/French/German) and IT program pages indicate openness to international skills and digital orientation.
- Presence in Thessaloniki private Lyceum cluster near major UNIC targets (Pinewood, Anatolia, Mandoulides, Vassiliadis) enables multi-school outreach days and benchmarking.
- School promotes scholarships (“Υποτροφίες Μαθητών”) and student achievement pages (“Επιτυχόντες”), signaling academic motivation and potential willingness to consider merit-based offers.

**Top risks or constraints:**
- Tuition not published; ability-to-pay needs discovery early, especially for Medicine tuition bands.
- Counselor/placement contacts not listed publicly; initial outreach likely through general office before identifying decision-makers.
- Strong focus on Panhellenic pathways could limit attention for overseas options without clear outcomes and proof points.

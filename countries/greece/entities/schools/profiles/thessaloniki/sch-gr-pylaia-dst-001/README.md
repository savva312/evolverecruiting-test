**One-paragraph summary:**  
DST is a long-established (135+ years) German international school delivering the German International Abitur (DIA) while also granting the Greek Apolytirion, enabling graduates to access both German and Greek university systems and broader global destinations. Its bilingual (German/Greek) environment and international diploma signal high outbound mobility readiness, and the Pylaia-Chortiatis location serves affluent metro Thessaloniki families. Fees are not publicly posted, so affordability must be confirmed directly, but the DIA pathway and premium positioning make DST a priority relationship target in Thessaloniki.

**Top opportunities:**  
- German International Abitur plus Greek Apolytirion positions graduates for EU-wide study; highlight UNIC’s accredited English-taught programs.
- Bilingual German/Greek community with exposure to international universities; leverage alumni or German-speaking faculty to build trust.
- Metro Thessaloniki catchment where UNIC Athens proximity and cost competitiveness vs. Germany/UK can resonate.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- Tuition not published; need to validate ability-to-pay case-by-case.
- Strong pathways to Germany may draw top STEM/medicine candidates elsewhere without differentiated messaging.

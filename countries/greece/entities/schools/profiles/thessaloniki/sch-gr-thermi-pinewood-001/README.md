**One-paragraph summary:**  
American-curriculum international school in the Thessaloniki metro (Thermi/Pylea corridor) with AP courses, a joint Anatolia & Pinewood IB Diploma track, and on-campus boarding. Published upper-secondary tuition at €13.45k (IB DP €13.75k) and boarding packages (~€27.45k including capital levy) signal strong ability to pay. English-medium instruction and college counseling make it a priority pipeline for UNIC and UNIC Athens across business, CS, health, and medicine pathways.

**Top opportunities:**  
- Published high-fee upper secondary plus boarding; clear affordability alignment for Nicosia and Athens.  
- Dual pathways (AP + IB DP) with English-medium instruction and active college counseling.  
- Location in Thessaloniki metro provides access to northern Greece and international/expat families.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- Likely modest graduating cohort size; volume may be limited without multi-year cultivation.  
- Competitive presence from Anatolia College and foreign-pathway schools in metro market.  
- Calendar/visit protocols not fully documented; need confirmation to avoid scheduling misalignment.

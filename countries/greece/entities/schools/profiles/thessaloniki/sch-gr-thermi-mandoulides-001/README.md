**One-paragraph summary:**  
Private K–12 school in Thermi with a nationally authorized General Lyceum and a prominent Studies Abroad program that prepares students for U.S./Canada/Europe admissions through SAT/ACT/TOEFL/IELTS preparation and counselor-led portfolio building. The school emphasizes academic excellence, Olympiad participation, and digital learning, positioning graduates for Greek and international university entry. Lack of published tuition requires verification, but the brand is treated as a premium Thessaloniki metro option in the UNIC affordability shortlist.

**Top opportunities:**  
- Studies Abroad counseling and standardized test preparation create a pipeline of internationally oriented students without requiring IB enrollment.  
- Thessaloniki metro catchment with fee-paying families; proximity to other premium schools allows multi-school visit routing.  
- Established history of students entering top universities abroad plus regular university representative visits, easing UNIC positioning for Medicine, CS, and Business.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- Fees are not published; ability-to-pay needs confirmation during first contact.  
- No IB/A-Level track; English readiness varies by cohort and hinges on SAT/IELTS prep rather than curriculum medium.  
- Competitive metro market (Pinewood, Anatolia, other Thermi/Pylaia private Lyceums) may already court the same international recruiters.

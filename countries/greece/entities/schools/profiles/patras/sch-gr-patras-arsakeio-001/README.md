**One-paragraph summary:**  
Arsakeio General Lyceum of Patras is the upper-secondary unit of the Arsakeia-Tositseia network serving the Patras metro. It delivers the national General Lyceum curriculum in Greek with strong foreign-language provision and a long-standing brand among private schools. Families are fee-paying, but the Patras campus does not publish tuition, placing affordability in the “partial/uncertain” band; engagement should validate price points and university guidance capacity. 

**Top opportunities:**  
- Brand strength of the Arsakeia network and perceived academic quality across Greece.  
- Established English-language and co-curricular programs that can feed interest in international pathways.  
- Only one of a handful of private Lyceums in Patras, giving concentrated access to the local fee-paying segment.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- Tuition not published; ability-to-pay for UNIC Athens/Nicosia needs confirmation.  
- Limited visible evidence of formal international diploma tracks (IB/AP/A-Levels not advertised).  
- University counseling capacity and outbound placement data not public.

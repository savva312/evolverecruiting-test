# Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (AUTh)

- **Last verified:** 2025-12-21
- **Website:** https://www.auth.gr/en/

## Positioning
- Largest public research university in Greece with a flagship campus in Thessaloniki; dominates domestic preference lists because Greek-taught degrees are tuition-free for EU/EEA students.
- Actively promoting select English-taught options (notably the 6-year MD) to attract international students and Greek families seeking an English curriculum while staying in-country.

## Pricing and scholarships
- **Greek-language programs (public university policy):** Tuition-free for EU/EEA students; non-EU students pay roughly **€1,000/year** at public universities depending on the institution and program.【https://www.studyineurope.eu/study-in-greece/tuition-fees】
- **English-language programs:** The 6-year Medicine in English charges **€12,000 per academic year**, payable in two installments, with a **€6,000 non-refundable deposit** upon acceptance (or **€1,200** pre-exam deposit credited toward tuition if admitted).【https://aristotlemedical.edu.gr/index.php/admission-fees】

## Recruitment signals in Greece
- Free tuition for EU/EEA students keeps AUTh the default choice for price-sensitive Greek families, especially in Northern Greece, limiting the addressable pool for private competitors.
- The English-taught MD combines public-brand credibility with lower pricing than many private medical options, appealing to students who want English delivery without relocating abroad.
- Entrance exam/interview steps plus English B2 language requirements signal controlled intake and filter for academically strong applicants.

## Overlap with UNIC / UNIC Athens
- AUTh’s tuition-free Greek programs anchor domestic demand, so UNIC must pitch differentiation (placement outcomes, mobility, housing, blended pathways) rather than price.
- The English MD at **€12k/year** competes directly with [UNIC Nicosia](../../../../../UNIC/unicnicosia/programs.md) for medicine prospects and may also divert health-interested leads who might consider [UNIC Athens](../../../../../UNIC/unicathens/programs.md).
- AUTh’s public status and Thessaloniki location offer a “stay in Greece” alternative for families comparing total cost of attendance versus relocating to Cyprus.

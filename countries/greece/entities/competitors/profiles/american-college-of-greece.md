# American College of Greece (Deree)

- **Last verified:** 2025-12-20
- **Website:** https://www.acg.edu/

## Positioning
- US-accredited private college in Athens offering American-style undergraduate and graduate degrees in business, liberal arts, computing, and communications marketed to Greek and regional students.
- Highlights residential campus life in Athens and English-language instruction that mirrors US curricula, appealing to students seeking an international experience without leaving Greece.

## Pricing and scholarships
- Published tuition shows per-credit pricing: up to 8 credits at **€418–€431 per credit** and 9–14 credits in the same range; lab courses add **€88–€90 per lab unit**. International degree-seeking students provide deposits (**€3,000** for semesters; **€1,700** for sessions).【https://www.acg.edu/admissions/financial-assistance/tuition/tuition-fees/】
- Offers merit- and need-based scholarships via ACG’s financial assistance programs, enabling tuition discounts for strong academic profiles.【https://www.acg.edu/admissions/financial-assistance/scholarships/】

## Recruitment signals in Greece
- Operates directly in Athens with English-language degrees and campus housing, reducing friction for Greek students who prefer staying local but want US-style instruction.
- Pricing is modular by credit, which can incentivize part-time or working students; deposits and semester/session flexibility show active enrollment management for non-traditional and international students.

## Overlap with UNIC / UNIC Athens
- Competes with [UNIC Athens programs](../../../../../UNIC/unicathens/programs.md) in business, psychology, communications, and computing—especially for English-language undergraduate degrees.
- Appeals to similar families seeking internationally oriented programs without relocating abroad, overlapping with UNIC Athens’ value proposition of in-market delivery with Cyprus accreditation.

# UCLan Cyprus (University of Central Lancashire Cyprus)

- **Last verified:** 2025-12-20
- **Website:** https://www.uclancyprus.ac.cy/

## Positioning
- British branch campus in Larnaca offering UK degrees with lower tuition than the UK mainland and proximity to Greece.
- Markets English-taught programs in business, computing, law, engineering, and psychology, positioning as a “British education in Cyprus” that is EU-accessible for Greek students.

## Pricing and scholarships
- Foundation programme priced at **€6,500** for the full year after a €1,500 bursary (original €8,000). Undergraduate degrees list **€9,000/year after a €950 bursary** on the €9,950 headline fee; postgraduate degrees at **€9,500** after a €1,000 bursary on €10,500.【https://www.uclancyprus.ac.cy/study/fees-scholarships/】
- Additional scholarships and discounts advertised: STEM and business programmes show 30–40% scholarships (e.g., computing tuition reduced to **€6,965** from €9,950 per year), and distance-learning MBAs/masters discounted to **€8,500** (from €10,500).【https://www.uclancyprus.ac.cy/study/fees-scholarships/】

## Recruitment signals in Greece
- Uses upfront bursaries and program-level scholarships, signaling aggressive pricing for EU/Greek students comparing UK-branded degrees without UK living costs.
- Detailed fee breakdowns by year and bursary availability for EU locals show active engagement of price-sensitive Greek families.

## Overlap with UNIC / UNIC Athens
- Direct competition with [UNIC Athens business and computing programs](../../../../../UNIC/unicathens/programs.md) and with [UNIC Nicosia’s](../../../../../UNIC/unicnicosia/programs.md) law/engineering offerings, especially for students seeking UK-aligned curricula.
- Bursary-heavy messaging mirrors UNIC scholarship positioning; proximity makes it a near-shore alternative for Greek applicants unwilling to relocate to the UK.

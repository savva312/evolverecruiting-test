# European University Cyprus (EUC)

- **Last verified:** 2025-12-20
- **Website:** https://euc.ac.cy/

## Positioning
- Private Cypriot university marketing heavily in Greece (dedicated Greek-language tuition sheet) with English- and Greek-taught programs across medicine, dentistry, veterinary medicine, law, business, and STEM.
- Emphasizes modern campus in Nicosia and professional programs with EU-recognized degrees, offering an alternative to studying in Greece while remaining within short flight distance.

## Pricing and scholarships
- Greek-student tuition sheet lists annual fees such as **€23,000/year for MD**, **€25,000/year for MD Frankfurt branch**, **€23,000/year for BDS dentistry**, **€21,000/year for DVM veterinary**, **€10,980/year (discounted to €9,882) for Law**, and **€9,240/year (discounted to €8,316) for Business and computing majors**. Discounted columns indicate targeted fee reductions for Greek enrollees.【https://syllabus.euc.ac.cy/tuitions/euc-tuition-fees-g.pdf】
- Displays “reduced tuition” alongside approved rates, signaling scholarship/bursary practices for priority programs (law, business, computing) aimed at Greek applicants.【https://syllabus.euc.ac.cy/tuitions/euc-tuition-fees-g.pdf】

## Recruitment signals in Greece
- Publishes Greek-language pricing and differentiates reduced fees, indicating active outreach and tailored offers for Greek students.
- Strong portfolio in medicine, dentistry, and veterinary medicine positions EUC against outbound medical destinations competing with [UNIC Nicosia’s medical portfolio](../../../../../UNIC/unicnicosia/programs.md).

## Overlap with UNIC / UNIC Athens
- Direct overlap with UNIC’s health sciences (MD, dentistry, veterinary medicine), law, business, and computing programs; proximity and English delivery mirror [UNIC Athens](../../../../../UNIC/unicathens/programs.md) value propositions.
- Discounting strategy and location convenience compete with UNIC scholarships and pricing for Greek students weighing Cyprus-based options.

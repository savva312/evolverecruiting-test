# University of Patras

- **Last verified:** 2025-12-21
- **Website:** https://www.upatras.gr/

## Positioning
- Greek public research university with strong engineering and health sciences faculties; attracts domestic students to tuition-free Greek-language degrees across medicine, engineering, sciences, and humanities.
- Running a fully English-taught 6-year undergraduate **Medicine** program aimed at non-Greek/EU fee-paying students, marketed as an EU-accredited MD without needing Greek language proficiency.
- Public-sector reputation and lower living costs in Patras make it a value alternative to private English-language providers in Athens and Cyprus.

## Pricing and scholarships
- **Greek-language undergraduate programs:** Tuition-free for EU/EEA students in line with Greek public university policy; textbooks are government-funded.【https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Education_in_Greece】
- **English-language Medicine program:** Published tuition of **€13,000 per academic year** (6-year total €78,000) with installment options noted in program materials; targeted at international intakes.【https://www.upatras.gr/en/undergraduate-program-in-medicine-in-english/】
- Scholarships/discounts: Limited; relies on general public-university social support (meal subsidies, housing priority) rather than broad tuition waivers for the English-track.

## Recruitment signals in Greece
- English Medicine program uses dedicated admissions portal and marketing in English, indicating active recruitment of international cohorts; leverages Patras University Hospital for clinical training.
- Tuition-free Greek-language tracks and national entrance exam selection keep a strong domestic funnel; Erasmus+ partnerships support outbound mobility narratives for Greek students who want international exposure without high fees.

## Overlap with UNIC / UNIC Athens
- Direct competitor to [UNIC Nicosia medical portfolio](../../../../../UNIC/unicnicosia/programs.md) for international MD students; Patras undercuts on price while offering EU public-university brand.
- For Greek students considering [UNIC Athens programs](../../../../../UNIC/unicathens/programs.md), Patras’ tuition-free Greek degrees appeal to cost-sensitive families, while its English Medicine option provides an in-country alternative to Cyprus/UK pathways.

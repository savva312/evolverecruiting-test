## Audit takeaways

- Greece already has usable control artifacts for schools, agents, regulators, NGOs/SGOs, and fairs/events, but all five are still in a maintenance or QA phase rather than a closed-complete state.
- Digital channels and employer/industry linkages are the highest-priority gaps because they depend on isolated narrative references instead of canonical Greece datasets or entity profiles.
- EvoManager can open precise follow-up tickets immediately without widening this ticket's scope; the most urgent sequence is `T-592` through `T-598`.

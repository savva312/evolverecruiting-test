# Greece entities directory

This folder centralizes profiles for organizations that influence Greek student recruitment for UNIC and UNIC Athens. Each subfolder keeps brief summaries, contact notes, and any sourcing or compliance considerations.

- `schools/` – high schools, lyceums, and language schools with counselor relationships and visit history.
- `agents/` – education agents and counseling firms active in Greece, including performance and compliance notes.
- `government-regulators/` – ministries and agencies touching outbound study, visas, and recognition.
- `ngos-sgos/` – non-government or semi-government organizations supporting mobility or scholarships.
- `fairs-events/` – recruitment fairs, school tours, and local events worth attending.
- `competitors/` – other universities and providers recruiting in Greece.

Add short, factual entries with sources where possible. Link to related go-to-market tactics when available.

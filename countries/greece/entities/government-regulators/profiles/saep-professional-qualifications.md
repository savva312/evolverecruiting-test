# SAEP — Council for the Recognition of Professional Qualifications

- **Mandate:** National authority (under the Ministry of Education) implementing EU Directive 2005/36/EC on the recognition of professional qualifications for regulated professions; issues decisions that grant professional rights or compensatory measures (adaptation period/exams).
- **Relevance to UNIC/UNIC Athens:** Critical for UNIC graduates seeking professional practice in Greece (e.g., engineering, architecture, health-related fields) when degrees were earned abroad; SAEP decisions determine whether additional training or exams are needed.
- **Key processes and timelines:** Applications are filed through the SAEP portal (https://saep.minedu.gov.gr/) with notarized degree/transcript copies, proof of professional experience, and fee payment; Directive 2005/36/EC targets a three-month decision window after complete submission, though complex cases can extend.
- **Contact points:** Online portal and helpdesk via https://saep.minedu.gov.gr/; policy oversight and appeals handled by the Ministry of Education.
- **Sources:** SAEP application portal (https://saep.minedu.gov.gr/) and EU Directive 2005/36/EC implementation guidance.

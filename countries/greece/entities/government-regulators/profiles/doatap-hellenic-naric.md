# DOATAP — Hellenic National Academic Recognition and Information Center (NARIC)

- **Mandate:** Independent body under the Ministry of Education responsible for academic recognition (equivalence and correspondence) of foreign higher-education degrees and information on foreign institutions.
- **Relevance to UNIC/UNIC Athens:** Determines whether UNIC Nicosia degrees are considered equivalent for further study, public sector employment, and professional licensure steps in Greece; confirmation of institutional status can influence counselor messaging and admissions credibility.
- **Key processes and timelines:** Applicants book an appointment and submit an online/physical dossier (degree, transcript, proof of study duration, curriculum) with an e-paravolo fee; applications are evaluated by subject-specific boards that meet periodically. Processing often stretches over several months when queues are long, so students should apply early.
- **Contact points:** General portal and news at https://www.doatap.gr/en/; appointment guidance and document lists available through the same site (Greek and English menus).
- **Sources:** DOATAP official site (https://www.doatap.gr/en/) and service pages linked from the main navigation.

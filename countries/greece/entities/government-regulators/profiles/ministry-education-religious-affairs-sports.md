# Ministry of Education, Religious Affairs and Sports (ΥΠΑΙΘΑ)

- **Mandate:** Sets national policy for primary, secondary, and higher education; licenses and supervises Greek higher-education institutions and private colleges; oversees recognition bodies (DOATAP for academic equivalence, SAEP for professional qualifications).
- **Relevance to UNIC/UNIC Athens:** Determines how foreign secondary diplomas and foundation programs are treated for Greek admissions; supervision of DOATAP/SAEP affects the recognition pathway for UNIC Nicosia degrees and professional rights (e.g., health, engineering) for graduates returning to Greece.
- **Key processes and timelines:** Issues circulars on admission criteria and equivalence of foreign secondary credentials (usually ahead of each academic year); sets policy for transnational education/college collaborations in Greece; final recognition decisions for foreign degrees and professional rights run through DOATAP/SAEP, which report to the ministry.
- **Contact points:** Central contact form and protocol office via https://www.minedu.gov.gr/epikoinonia (Greek); general information portal in English at https://www.minedu.gov.gr/en/.

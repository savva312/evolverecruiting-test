# Government and regulators

Maintain references for ministries and agencies that influence outbound study, visas, credential recognition, and consumer protection for Greek students.

- `profiles/` – per-agency notes with mandates, published guidance, and relevant links.

## Coverage (last updated: 2025-12-20)

- Ministry of Education, Religious Affairs and Sports — policy-setter for schools and higher education; supervises recognition and professional qualification bodies.
- DOATAP (Hellenic NARIC) — academic recognition of foreign higher-education degrees.
- SAEP (Council for the Recognition of Professional Qualifications) — professional rights recognition under EU Directive 2005/36/EC.
- Ministry of Migration and Asylum — student visa and study-residence permits for third-country nationals (relevant to UNIC Athens intake).

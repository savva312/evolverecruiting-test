# Greece NGOs/SGOs index

Priority NGOs and SGOs shaping outbound scholarships, counseling, and student mobility from Greece.

## Quick picks
- **Fulbright Foundation Greece** — binational exchange leader with advising reach across Athens and Thessaloniki; credibility for co-hosted info sessions and counselor training.
- **State Scholarships Foundation (IKY)** — national agency administering state and EU-funded scholarships; essential for alignment on recognition, Erasmus+/mobility messaging, and potential co-branded awards.
- **Stavros Niarchos Foundation (SNF)** — major philanthropic funder with scholarship endowments and fellowship partnerships abroad; potential co-funded seats for priority programs.
- **Onassis Foundation** — long-running scholarships for postgraduate and doctoral study overseas; strong cultural cachet and alumni network in arts/humanities and STEM.
- **Bodossaki Foundation** — STEM-leaning postgraduate scholarships and civic programs; good fit for engineering/health sciences funnels.

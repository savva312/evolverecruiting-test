# Onassis Foundation

**Type:** Public benefit foundation  
**Focus:** Scholarships for postgraduate/doctoral study, arts and cultural exchange, academic chairs  
**Geography:** Athens-based with global cultural network; applicants from across Greece  
**Website:** https://www.onassis.org/

## Overview
The Onassis Foundation funds Greek students and researchers for graduate study abroad and supports international cultural/academic collaborations. Its “Scholarships for Hellenes” program is one of Greece’s longest-running private scholarship schemes, with strong alumni visibility in humanities, social sciences, health, and STEM fields.

## Programs and funding
- **Scholarships for Hellenes:** Tuition and living support for master’s, doctoral, and post-graduate research abroad; recurring annual calls with priority disciplines published each cycle. 
- **Academic and research grants:** Targeted fellowships for Greek and international scholars through Onassis Library and cultural centers. 
- **Cultural/arts residencies:** Residencies and production grants that can include training components for creative industries.

## Target students
- Greek nationals pursuing postgraduate or doctoral study abroad, especially in humanities, social sciences, arts, health, and STEM. 
- Artists and researchers seeking residencies with international exposure. 
- University partners hosting Onassis chairs or collaborative projects.

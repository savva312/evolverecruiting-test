# Stavros Niarchos Foundation (SNF)

**Type:** Global philanthropic foundation  
**Focus:** Grants for education, culture, health, and civic initiatives; scholarship and fellowship partnerships with universities  
**Geography:** Headquarters in Athens with global grantmaking footprint (Europe, North America, international)  
**Website:** https://www.snf.org/

## Overview
SNF funds large-scale educational initiatives and bespoke scholarship/fellowship programs with universities and cultural institutions worldwide. Its brand credibility and financial capacity make it a cornerstone funder for Greek talent pursuing studies abroad.

## Programs and funding
- **University-partnered scholarships and fellowships:** Endowed seats and annual cohorts at institutions such as Columbia University, Johns Hopkins University, and King’s College London, often in journalism, public policy, health, or cultural fields. 
- **SNF Paideia and academic chairs:** Support for liberal arts and civic engagement programs (e.g., University of Pennsylvania’s SNF Paideia Program). 
- **Grants to Greek universities:** Funding for labs, digital-skills initiatives, and outreach that can include student mobility or short courses.

## Target students
- Greek and international students admitted to partner universities in SNF-funded programs. 
- Emerging leaders in public health, journalism, public policy, arts, and digital transformation. 
- Greek university students participating in SNF-funded mobility or enrichment projects.

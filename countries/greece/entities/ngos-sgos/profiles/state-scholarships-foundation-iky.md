# State Scholarships Foundation (IKY)

**Type:** Semi-governmental scholarship agency (public benefit)  
**Focus:** State and EU-funded scholarships, Erasmus+ mobility, graduate/doctoral awards  
**Geography:** Nationwide mandate; manages programs reaching all Greek regions  
**Website:** https://www.iky.gr/

## Overview
IKY is Greece’s national scholarship body, administering state funds and acting as the National Agency for Erasmus+. It supports academic mobility, postgraduate study, and research fellowships, making it a central gatekeeper for outbound study support.

## Programs and funding
- **Postgraduate and doctoral scholarships for Greeks:** Tuition and stipend support for master’s and PhD studies (domestic and abroad), often prioritizing STEM, health, and education fields. 
- **Erasmus+ mobility:** Oversees higher-education mobility, staff exchanges, and intensive programs; sets recognition and credit-transfer expectations. 
- **Special thematic calls:** Periodic scholarships tied to national recovery, digital-skills, or teacher-upskilling initiatives.

## Target students
- Greek graduates and researchers pursuing advanced degrees abroad or joint degrees. 
- University students seeking semester or year-long mobility placements under Erasmus+. 
- Educators and administrators participating in EU-funded training or exchanges.

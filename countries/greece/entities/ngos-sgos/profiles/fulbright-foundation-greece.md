# Fulbright Foundation Greece

**Type:** Binational education exchange foundation (U.S.–Greece)  
**Focus:** Scholarships and advising for study/research in the United States  
**Geography:** HQ in Athens; outreach across Greece with cohorts from Thessaloniki and regional hubs  
**Website:** https://www.fulbright.gr/

## Overview
Fulbright Greece is the official U.S.–Greece Fulbright Commission, funded jointly by both governments. It runs competitive scholarships and advising that shape perceptions of international study pathways. Its alumni network spans academia, arts, public policy, and science.

## Programs and funding
- **Greek Graduate Student Program:** Scholarships for master’s or doctoral study in the U.S., covering tuition support plus monthly stipends and J-1 visa sponsorship. 
- **Visiting Scholar/Artist Programs:** Short-term research, teaching, or artistic residencies for mid-career professionals and faculty. 
- **EducationUSA advising:** Free advising, webinars, and college-counselor training on U.S. study options; heavy touchpoints with top secondary schools and universities.

## Target students
- High-achieving Greek graduates and early-career professionals aiming for U.S. master’s, PhD, or professional programs. 
- Artists and researchers seeking short-term residencies or joint projects with U.S. institutions. 
- Secondary-school counselors and university career offices engaged via EducationUSA seminars.

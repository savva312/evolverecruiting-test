# Bodossaki Foundation

**Type:** Public benefit foundation  
**Focus:** Scholarships for postgraduate study (STEM, environment, health), civic and social-impact programs  
**Geography:** Athens HQ; national reach with STEM-heavy applicant pool  
**Website:** https://www.bodossaki.gr/

## Overview
Bodossaki Foundation supports education, health, and environmental initiatives, including a longstanding scholarships program that funds Greek students for postgraduate and doctoral study abroad. It also runs youth and civil-society capacity building that can surface motivated candidates for international study.

## Programs and funding
- **Bodossaki Scholarships Program:** Tuition and living support for master’s/doctoral studies, historically prioritizing engineering, natural sciences, public health, and environmental fields. 
- **Civil-society and youth initiatives:** Training and grantmaking for NGOs/young professionals (e.g., Project Future, NGO Academy) that develop leadership and employability skills. 
- **Thematic grants:** Occasional calls for health or environmental innovation that may include study visits or short-course components.

## Target students
- Greek STEM and health-sciences graduates seeking postgraduate degrees abroad. 
- Young professionals engaged in NGO Academy/Project Future looking for advanced training pathways. 
- Social-impact innovators needing formal qualifications or short international programs.

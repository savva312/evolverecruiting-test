## Notes on prioritization

- **Must-do** events drive brand visibility and high-intent leads in Athens/Thessaloniki; lock budgets and staffing early.
- **Consider** events have strong counselor density or niche segments; attend if schedules do not clash with exams and budget allows.
- **Monitor** events are lower cost and digital; leverage when travel is constrained or to warm audiences ahead of city tours.

## Preparation checklist (Greece-specific)

- Localize Greek-language landing pages with UTM tracking for Athens vs Nicosia, and clear scholarship/visa FAQs.
- Pre-fair outreach to registered students and counselors; offer 15-minute counseling slots and campus-specific session times.
- On-site: Greek-speaking rep where possible; QR + short form (program interest, intake, GPA/IELTS, preferred campus); collect counselor cards for school visit scheduling.
- Post-fair: 24–72 hour follow-up with program decks, tuition/scholarship tables, webinar invites, and counselor toolkits; route leads by campus and intake in CRM.

# Fulbright/EducationUSA U.S. College Fair (Greece)

## Overview
Fulbright Greece (EducationUSA) hosts annual U.S.-focused college fairs in Athens, sometimes adding Thessaloniki or virtual sessions. While U.S.-oriented, the events gather English-speaking students and counselors open to accredited EU options, offering a chance to position UNIC as an affordable alternative with U.S. articulation routes.

## Timing and format
- **Typical months:** October for Athens in-person; virtual follow-up in November; Thessaloniki edition occurs in some years.
- **Format:** Booth fair with counselor briefing; short info sessions on applications/financial aid; virtual webinars for regional participants.
- **City:** Athens (central hotel/university venues) with occasional Thessaloniki stop.

## Audience and expected reach
- 400–700 students, parents, and counselors; strong English proficiency and interest in international study.
- Smaller but high-quality counselor segment (~30–60) useful for school visit scheduling.

## Pricing (indicative)
- Often free for non-U.S. accredited exhibitors or €0–€300 admin fee; minimal shipping/printing costs.
- Sponsorships available for added visibility (branding, session slots).

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** emphasize English-medium degrees, U.S. credit transfer possibilities, and lower total cost than U.S. universities.
- **UNIC Athens:** highlight EU campus option with similar academic pathways and easier travel for Greek students.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: register as early as invites open; tailor message to affordability and credit transfer; prepare scholarship one-pagers.
- During: capture counselor contacts; offer side meetings next morning; propose webinar for students who cannot travel to Cyprus/Athens.
- Post: send comparison sheet (U.S. vs UNIC costs/outcomes); invite attendees to virtual info session; route high-GPA leads to honors/scholarship tracks.

## Risks and notes
- Audience leans U.S.-centric; must pivot conversation toward accredited EU option and financial value.
- Dates can shift based on venue availability; monitor EducationUSA announcements.

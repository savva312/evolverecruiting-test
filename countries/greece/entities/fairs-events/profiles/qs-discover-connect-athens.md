# QS Discover/Connect Master’s & MBA Fair (Athens)

## Overview
QS runs graduate-focused fairs (Discover/Connect) that combine open-floor booths with pre-scheduled meetings. Athens typically hosts one autumn and one spring edition, attracting English-proficient bachelor’s seniors, recent graduates, and young professionals exploring master’s or MBA options.

## Timing and format
- **Typical months:** October (autumn) and March (spring).
- **Format:** In-person fair with booth tables, seminar slots, and QS lead-scanning app; occasional invite-only 1:1 Connect meetings for top candidates.
- **City:** Athens (central hotel venues such as Hilton/InterContinental); usually weekday evening.

## Audience and expected reach
- 300–600 registrants; 200–450 on-site depending on season.
- High share of English-medium candidates; majority interested in business, finance, CS/IT, and social sciences.
- Good mix of Greek nationals and international residents in Athens.

## Pricing (indicative)
- €3,000–€4,500 per event (institution package with scan app); seminars sometimes included or +€500.
- Discounts for multi-event bundles (e.g., adding QS Virtual Connect Europe).

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** value vs UK/US, accredited English-medium master’s; highlight finance, data/AI, cybersecurity, and health programs.
- **UNIC Athens:** proximity and ability to combine work + study; emphasize flexible schedules and industry links in Athens.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: book autumn slot by June; submit seminar topic aligned to Greek demand (ROI-focused); prep Greek-language landing page + QR; confirm staff for scan app.
- During: prioritize Connect meetings; collect intake/program preferences; capture working-professional angle for Athens.
- Post: 24–48 hour follow-up with tailored program packs, scholarship/tuition tables, and booking links for counselor/student consults; retarget via email + WhatsApp.

## Risks and notes
- Dates vary year to year; visa appointments for Cyprus can be a bottleneck—set expectations at booth.
- Evening events require local staffing; ship lightweight collateral to avoid customs delays.

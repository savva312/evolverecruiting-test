# Study in Greece Virtual Fair series

## Overview
Study in Greece (the official Hellenic initiative) periodically runs virtual fairs and webinars to showcase Greek universities and English-taught programs. Participation raises visibility with domestic students considering staying in Greece as well as diaspora and international prospects seeking EU study options.

## Timing and format
- **Typical months:** April/May and January based on prior editions and academic calendar.
- **Format:** Virtual fair platform with institution booths, webinar rooms, and chat; sometimes paired with themed webinars (STEM, business, culture).
- **Coverage:** Greece-focused but marketed internationally, attracting Greeks abroad and regional audiences.

## Audience and expected reach
- 500–1,000 registrants; 150–300 engage in chats/webinars per edition.
- Mix of high school seniors, undergraduates exploring exchanges/second degrees, and counselors.

## Pricing (indicative)
- Free tiers for Greek public institutions; €0–€1,000 sponsorship/exhibitor options for private and international partners.
- Low marginal cost beyond staffing.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Athens:** highlight ability to study in Greece with international programs; proximity and local internships.
- **UNIC Nicosia:** position as regional alternative with broader English-medium catalog and lower tuition vs UK; appeal to Greeks abroad.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: monitor Study in Greece announcements; secure sponsorship slot for visibility; prepare Greek-language landing page and webinar deck.
- During: staff live chat in Greek/English; run short webinars on scholarships/ROI; capture counselor contacts for follow-up visits.
- Post: send thank-you email with campus comparison, tuition table, and bookable consult slots; retarget registrants with webinar recordings.

## Risks and notes
- Visibility depends on sponsorship tier; free booths may have limited placement.
- Dates occasionally shift near exam seasons; confirm final schedule before paid promotion.

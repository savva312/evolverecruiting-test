# Greece paid media and channel playbooks

## Purpose

Provide a Greece-focused view of which channels to test, how to measure them, and what creative/localization is needed to reach prospective students for UNIC and UNIC Athens.

## Relationship to the global skill
- Use `/skills/paid-media-and-channel-playbooks/SKILL.md` for shared objectives, schemas (campaign plan, UTMs, creative library), and workflow steps.
- Capture only Greece-specific channel nuances, compliance flags, or localization notes here (e.g., Greek language copy, local consent norms, platform restrictions, messaging rules for regulated programs).

## Greece-specific notes (extend as data is gathered)
- Preferred language handling: EN and EL variants for ad copy and LPs; subtitles on video when EL primary.
- Messaging channels: Viber/WhatsApp consent must be explicit; avoid unsolicited messaging.
- Regulated programs: include disclaimers for Medicine/health programs per Greek/EU guidance; no job-guarantee language.

# Greece schools and counselor mapping

Use with the global workflow in [`skills/schools-and-feeders/SKILL.md`](/skills/schools-and-feeders/SKILL.md), which covers the canonical steps, CSV fields, privacy rules, and profile template.

## Greece-specific guidance

- Keep counselor/access notes inside the same profiles and CSV rows you create for feeders (`countries/greece/entities/schools/profiles/<city>/<school-slug>/README.md` and `countries/greece/data/entities/schools/greece-feeder-schools.csv` unless the ticket specifies otherwise).
- Capture visit coverage summaries in `countries/greece/entities/schools/README.md` so tiers and contact freshness are visible.
- Prioritize counselor relationships in Athens first, then Thessaloniki, followed by major regional hubs; flag responsiveness and verified dates in both the CSV and profiles.

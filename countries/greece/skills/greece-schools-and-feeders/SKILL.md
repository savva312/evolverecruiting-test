# Greece schools and feeders

Use this alongside the global playbook in [`skills/schools-and-feeders/SKILL.md`](/skills/schools-and-feeders/SKILL.md) for workflow, CSV fields, privacy rules, and the canonical profile template.

## Greece-specific guidance

- **File locations:**  
  - Profiles: `countries/greece/entities/schools/profiles/<city>/<school-slug>/README.md` (use the global high school profile template).  
  - CSVs: `countries/greece/data/entities/schools/greece-feeder-schools.csv` (or ticket-specific filenames).  
  - Index: `countries/greece/entities/schools/README.md` documents the rubric and links.
- **Default prioritization:** Start with Athens metro (selective public lykeia, private/international schools), then Thessaloniki, followed by major regional hubs (Patras, Heraklion, Larissa/Volos) before smaller islands. Adjust tiers per ticket.
- **Tiering lens:** Apply the global rubric but weight access and program-fit signals that feed UNIC Athens (business, CS/data, design) while noting strong Medicine demand for Nicosia-bound candidates.

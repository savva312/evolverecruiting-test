# Serbia agent onboarding SOP

Purpose: give Serbia recruiters, admissions ops, and marketing a single playbook to take a prioritized agent (see `countries/serbia/entities/agents/feeder-candidates.md` and profiles) from first screening to launch for UNIC Nicosia and UNIC Athens.

## Roles and SLAs

| Stage | Primary owner | Support | SLA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Intake & prioritization | Serbia recruiter | Regional enrollment director | Same business day to log intake + tier rating |
| Due diligence vetting | Serbia recruiter | Legal/compliance | 48h to complete document pre-check, 5 business days for full vetting |
| Contracting & finance setup | Admissions ops | Legal, Finance | 5 business days to send agreement; 10 business days to countersign once agent returns docs |
| Training & enablement | Serbia recruiter | Marketing, Academics | Schedule enablement within 3 business days of contract signature |
| Go-live QA | Marketing (channel ops) | Admissions ops, Recruiter | 24h to verify assets, 72h to publish UTMs/landing pages |

## Stage 0 — Intake & prioritization
1. Capture inbound/outbound lead in CRM with agent name, city, and Tier alignment (Tier 1/2/watchlist) referencing feeder roster.
2. Confirm current destination mix (EU vs. other), applicant volume, and focus programs (medicine/health vs. business/CS/data) to balance Nicosia + Athens.
3. Send intake acknowledgement email with next-step timeline (48h for preliminary vetting feedback).

## Stage 1 — Due diligence checklist

Complete before sharing pricing, scholarships, or student-facing materials.

| Item | What to verify | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Corporate registration | Business license, tax ID, legal signatory | Serbia recruiter |
| Counselor credentials | ICEF/British Council/QA certificates, counselor bios | Recruiter |
| Destination mix & volumes | Last 2 intakes by destination; confirm number of Cyprus/Greece placements (if any) | Recruiter |
| Sub-agent structure | Written confirmation of sub-agent usage, list of cities, contracts | Recruiter + Legal |
| Data protection practices | GDPR statement, data storage location, consent process | Legal/compliance |
| Marketing footprint | Websites, social pages, paid ads; ensure UNIC branding absent pre-contract | Marketing |
| Student fee policies | Application/advising fees, refund policy, scholarship claims | Admissions ops |
| References | At least 2 HEI references (EU preferred) or fair organizer references | Recruiter |

### Red-flag prompts (use during diligence calls)
- “Walk me through how you source students in Novi Sad/Niš—do you rely on partner counselors or staff you employ directly?”
- “Who owns the student relationship if a BridgeBlue office in another city contributes? How do you prevent duplicate submissions?”
- “Please outline your data capture stack—what CRM do you use and where is it hosted?”
- “What compliance or marketing approvals do you seek from partners before publishing ads?”

Escalate to compliance if: sub-agents exceed 30% of Serbian volume, agent refuses to disclose fee schedule, or data is stored outside the EU without safeguards.

## Stage 2 — Contracting & compliance
1. **Pre-check package (48h SLA):** recruiter submits diligence pack (ID docs, references, checklist) to admissions ops + legal portal.
2. **Agreement routing (5 business days to send):** admissions ops populates latest UNIC Agent Agreement + Schedule A (commission tiers for Nicosia/Athens) and shares via DocuSign.
3. **Finance onboarding:** collect banking instructions, W-8/W-9 equivalent, VAT forms; finance sets vendor ID before countersignature.
4. **Policy acknowledgement:** ensure agent signs Code of Conduct, GDPR annex, marketing/brand usage rules, and Athens/Nicosia program fact sheets.
5. **CRM & portal setup:** admissions ops creates agency code, application portal logins, and fields for UTMs; turnaround goal 2 business days post-signature.

## Stage 3 — Training & enablement

### 90-minute onboarding session (schedule within 3 business days of signature)
| Segment | Duration | Lead |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Welcome + objectives | 10 min | Serbia recruiter |
| UNIC overview (Nicosia vs. Athens) | 15 min | Regional enrollment director |
| Program deep dives (Medicine/Health, Business/CS/Data, Hospitality) | 25 min | Academic liaison |
| Admissions process & timelines | 10 min | Admissions ops |
| Compliance (licensure, visa, scholarships) | 10 min | Compliance/legal |
| Marketing toolkit + UTMs walkthrough | 10 min | Marketing channel ops |
| Sub-agent monitoring & reporting cadence | 5 min | Recruiter |
| Q&A / next steps | 5 min | All |

**Training collateral:** localized deck (Serbian headlines), program fact sheets, scholarships math template, licensure FAQ, admissions flowchart, agent CRM manual.

### Assets pack (send within 24h of training)
- Serbia-language elevator pitch + email copy blocks for Nicosia and Athens.
- Co-branded one-pagers for Tier 1 programs (medicine/health, CS/data, business, hospitality).
- Scholarship/discount calculator template with Serbian dinar conversions.
- Application checklist, visa + residence permit guide, arrival timeline for both campuses.
- UTM plan + landing page URLs, lead routing diagram, and consent language snippet.
- Webinar/event kit: registration form template, slide master, recording policy, photo release form.

## Stage 4 — Go-live and QA
Use this checklist before allowing the agent to market UNIC.

- Contract in force; DocuSign envelope stored and vendor ID active.
- CRM code tested with dummy application; admissions ops confirms forms sync correctly.
- Agent staff roster (names, emails, WhatsApp) logged; non-trained counselors locked out until training replay completed.
- Marketing assets hosted in shared drive with view-only permissions; UTMs validated (test click + lead submission).
- Sub-agent declaration filed; if sub-agents approved, individual onboarding checklists attached.
- Reporting cadence agreed (weekly lead file or CRM access) and first review date booked.
- Event/webinar calendar penciled for next 90 days with owner assigned.
- Recruiter sends “go-live greenlight” email and archives all diligence artifacts in shared folder.

## Onboarding call script outline (Serbia focus)
1. **Opening (2 min)** — reaffirm UNIC goals, remind agent of 48h diligence SLA, set agenda.
2. **Agency snapshot (10 min)** — ask about office locations, staff count, destination mix; probe for sub-agent usage (“Who covers Novi Sad fairs for you?”).
3. **Student journey deep dive (15 min)** — map how leads move from fairs/Instagram/webinars into counseling; note conversion metrics and service fees.
4. **Program alignment (15 min)** — pitch Athens business/CS/data + hospitality, Nicosia medicine/health/cyber; confirm which programs agent feels confident representing and what enablement is needed.
5. **Compliance & marketing controls (10 min)** — review brand guidelines, scholarship messaging, licensure disclosures, data consent language; capture any local legal nuances.
6. **Operational next steps (8 min)** — summarize documents needed, timeline for contract + training, introduction of admissions ops and marketing POCs.
7. **Detecting sub-agent chains** — close with direct questions: “Do you expect any partner counselors to use your UNIC assets? If so, we must vet and sign them individually before they promote the brand.” Document answers in CRM.

End every call by emailing action items, owners, and dates (copy admissions ops + marketing) to enforce accountability.

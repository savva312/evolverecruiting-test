## Creative hygiene and localization
- Lead with Serbian (Latin) copy; add Cyrillic in parentheses for TOF and diaspora, and keep English micro-copy only where parents/counselors require it.  
- Add burned-in Serbian captions on all video assets; prioritize vertical (9:16) 15–30s plus 6-second cutdowns for YouTube retargeting.  
- On-screen text must show tuition in EUR, stipendija % (ako je potvrđeno), intake rok i tačnu lokaciju (UNIC Nicosia ili UNIC Athens).  
- Accessibility: keep safe zones for TikTok/Meta UI; contrast ratios ≥4.5:1; test audio levels for quiet classrooms/dorm shots.  
- Compliance: uključiti GDPR privolu na formama i link ka privacy notice-u; koristite disclaimere poput “Ishod zavisi od ličnih napora i zakonskih uslova” za karijerne tvrdnje.  
- Measurement: embed UTMs by kanal/kampanja/creative ID; koristite unikatne short linkove za influensere/Spark Ads radi preciznog atribuiranja.

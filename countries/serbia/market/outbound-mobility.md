## What this means for UNIC Athens (Greece) and UNIC Nicosia (Cyprus)
- **Greece/Cyprus are small existing destinations** for Serbian degree mobility (≈0.9% and 0.1% of the 2023 Eurostat reporting-set total). Growth to Athens/Nicosia will likely require *conversion from bigger destination sets* (Central Europe + region), not just “share capture” from current Greece/Cyprus-bound students.
- **Positioning that fits observed destination choices:** regional accessibility, clear recognition/licensure messaging (where applicable), English delivery, and total cost vs Central Europe.
- **Channel focus:** school counselors + agents in **Belgrade / Novi Sad / Niš** first; use destination benchmarks to build objection-handling vs Hungary/Austria/Germany.

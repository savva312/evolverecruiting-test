# ENIC/NARIC Serbia

last_checked: 2025-12-20

## Mandate
- National center for academic recognition and information on foreign higher-education qualifications, operating under the Ministry of Education.
- Issues recognition (equivalence) decisions for further study and access to regulated professions; advises institutions on comparability and Bologna alignment.

## Relevance to UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Serbian applicants may need ENIC/NARIC confirmation of UNIC degrees for postgraduate admission or public-sector employment upon return.
- Recognition outcomes shape messaging on degree portability and the documentation UNIC should provide (legalized diplomas, transcripts, program accreditation proofs, curriculum outlines).

## Key processes and timelines
- Applicants submit originals or certified copies of diplomas/transcripts with sworn translations, proof of accreditation, and identity documents; fees apply.
- Standard decision window is up to 60 days after a complete application; complex cases can extend, and additional evidence may pause the clock.
- Recognition can be full, partial (with academic conditions), or denied; appeals are available under administrative procedure rules.

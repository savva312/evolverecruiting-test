# Government & regulators

Track ministries and agencies that influence outbound study from Serbia and recognition for UNIC/UNIC Athens. Summaries should note mandates, relevant regulations, key contacts, and decision timelines.

- `profiles/` – dossiers for each regulator or ministry.

## Coverage (2025-12-20)

- Ministry of Education (policy, ENIC/NARIC recognition and scholarship governance).
- National Entity for Accreditation and Quality Assurance in Higher Education (NEAQA) (accreditation and QA).
- ENIC/NARIC Serbia (academic recognition and equivalence).
- Ministry of Interior – Directorate for Foreigners (student visas, temporary residence, ID cards).

# Competitors

Track universities and providers competing for Serbian students. Summaries should cover positioning, pricing, scholarships, partnerships, and marketing tactics.

**Priorities**
- Focus on English-taught or internationally accredited providers that Serbian students use as alternatives to UNIC/UNIC Athens (private Serbian universities, nearby EU/EEA universities, American-style colleges).
- Capture pricing signals (tuition ranges or scholarship levers) and digital/offline recruitment channels visible in Serbia.
- Note overlap with UNIC strengths (health, business, computing) and pathways to Athens/Nicosia.

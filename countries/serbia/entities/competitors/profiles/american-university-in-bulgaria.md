# American University in Bulgaria (AUBG)

- **Last verified:** 2025-12-20  
- **Why it matters:** U.S.-accredited liberal arts university in Blagoevgrad, Bulgaria (near Sofia) recruiting across the Balkans for English-taught business, computer science, and politics—programs overlapping with UNIC/UNIC Athens. Serbian students view it as a regional alternative with an American degree path.

## Positioning & portfolio
- Private, tuition-dependent liberal arts institution with U.S. and Bulgarian accreditation; majors include Business Administration, Computer Science, Economics, and Politics/International Relations. 【https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_University_in_Bulgaria】  
- Markets small-class U.S.-style teaching and exchange opportunities with U.S. partner schools—similar to UNIC’s mobility narrative but with a U.S. brand.

## Pricing and aid signals
- Wikipedia notes AUBG’s funding model combines endowment, gifts, **and tuition**, underscoring a fee-paying proposition. 【https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_University_in_Bulgaria】  
- Named scholarships exist (e.g., **Tchaprachikoff scholarship** offering partial tuition funding for graduates moving to top U.S. graduate schools), showing active merit/need-based aid levers that appeal to cost-sensitive Serbian applicants. 【https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_University_in_Bulgaria】

## Channels & Serbia-facing tactics
- Cross-border recruitment through high-school visits in the Balkans, English-first web presence, and counselor/alumni networks; proximity to Serbia (5–6 hour drive to Blagoevgrad) supports campus visit events and fairs in Belgrade/Niš.  
- Uses “American diploma in the EU” messaging plus exchange pathways—needs counter-positioning with UNIC’s Greece/Cyprus location advantages and professional accreditation story.

## Overlap vs UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Competes directly on business and CS, the same majors UNIC markets in English.  
- Differentiation levers for UNIC: lower cost of living in Cyprus/Greece vs Bulgaria, Mediterranean lifestyle, and access to UNIC’s professional networks in finance, shipping, and health sciences.

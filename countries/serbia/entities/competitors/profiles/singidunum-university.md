# Singidunum University (Belgrade)

- **Last verified:** 2025-12-20  
- **Why it matters:** Largest private university in Serbia (9,330 students in 2023–24) with heavy footprint in business, IT, and hospitality—disciplines where UNIC/UNIC Athens also recruit. Private status allows flexible pricing/discounting and aggressive digital marketing. 【https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Singidunum_University】

## Positioning & portfolio
- Multi-faculty private university offering Bologna-aligned degrees in business, tourism/hospitality, computer science, software engineering, and sports. Programs marketed with “industry partner” messaging and employability claims. 【https://singidunum.ac.rs/en】  
- Uses ECTS/Bologna structure similar to UNIC, making credit recognition straightforward for transfer/cross-border mobility. 【https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Singidunum_University】

## Pricing, scholarships, affordability signals
- Serbia-wide tuition guidance for international students ranges **€750–€20,000 per year**; private universities like Singidunum typically sit in the upper mid-range of that band. 【https://studyinserbia.rs/about-us/faq/】  
- Site banners promote annual intake (“UPIS 2025/2026”) and campaign imagery, signaling recurring early-enrollment and promotional pricing windows even though exact tariff tables are not exposed without inquiry. 【https://singidunum.ac.rs/en/students/scholarships】

## Channels & Serbia-facing tactics
- High-velocity owned media (news stream, campus events) and active Facebook/Instagram/TikTok/YouTube presences linked from the scholarship/enrolment landing page. 【https://singidunum.ac.rs/en/students/scholarships】  
- Offline: open-door days and intake campaigns visible in banner creative; leans on Belgrade campus tours and partner career days with local employers.

## Overlap vs UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Direct overlap in business/management, hospitality/tourism, computer science, and AI. Competes on “modern facilities + employability” narrative rather than international mobility; UNIC can counter with cross-campus mobility (Nicosia/Athens), EU exposure, and English-taught depth.

## Watchpoints / implications for UNIC
- Price-sensitive Serbian students may treat Singidunum as a domestic fallback if UNIC scholarships are not visible; ensure UNIC scholarship messaging is discoverable in Serbian digital channels.  
- Highlight UNIC Athens proximity (short flight) and Erasmus/placement options to differentiate from domestic-only outlook.

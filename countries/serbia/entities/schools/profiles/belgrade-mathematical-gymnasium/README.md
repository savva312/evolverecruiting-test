# Mathematical Grammar School (Matematička gimnazija), Belgrade

## Snapshot
- City: Belgrade (Savski venac).
- School type: elite state mathematics and science gymnasium; Serbian national curriculum with enriched STEM tracks.
- Enrollment: ~900 students across grades 7–12 (verify with current school report).
- Programs: Accelerated math/physics curriculum, Olympiad preparation streams, elective programming/IT modules.
- Counselor contact: International cooperation office and class head teachers manage university visits; identify STEM deputy head as first point of contact.

## Academics and programs
- Track emphasis: Heavy math/physics load; strong record in national and international Olympiads.
- Language profile: Serbian instruction with intensive English; students frequently take IELTS/SAT externally.
- Extracurriculars: Olympiad teams, robotics/astronomy clubs; student research projects in math/physics.

## Mobility and destinations
- Typical destinations: University of Belgrade STEM faculties; Germany, Austria, Netherlands, UK, and Switzerland for engineering/CS; select students pursue US colleges via scholarships.
- Scholarship sensitivity: High—families value clear merit tiers and predictable living costs; competitive cohorts expect recognition of prior achievements.

# Gymnasium "Svetozar Marković" (Novi Sad)

## Snapshot
- City: Novi Sad.
- School type: state general gymnasium with language and STEM tracks; Serbian national curriculum.
- Enrollment: ~1,000 students across four years (confirm with current school data).
- Programs: Language-oriented classes (English/German/French) and natural sciences tracks; elective prep for Cambridge/IELTS/DSD depending on stream.
- Counselor contact: No standalone counseling office; principal and homeroom/class teachers coordinate university visits.

## Academics and programs
- Track emphasis: Strong humanities/language tracks plus solid natural sciences option; debate and foreign-language competitions are active.
- Language profile: Serbian instruction with intensive foreign-language study; many students pursue external IELTS/DSD/Goethe certifications.
- Extracurriculars: Debate club, language competitions, Model UN, and occasional Erasmus+ exchanges.

## Mobility and destinations
- Typical destinations: Serbia (University of Novi Sad humanities/social sciences), Germany/Austria/Italy for language and social sciences, Netherlands/UK for English-taught programs; smaller but growing interest in Greece/Cyprus for business/psychology.
- Scholarship sensitivity: Medium—families balance tuition and living costs; transparent scholarships and installment plans help.

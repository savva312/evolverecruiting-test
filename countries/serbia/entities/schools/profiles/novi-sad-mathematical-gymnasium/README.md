# Mathematical Gymnasium Novi Sad

## Snapshot
- City: Novi Sad.
- School type: selective mathematics and science high school under the Serbian national curriculum with extended STEM.
- Enrollment: ~800–900 students (confirm with latest school report).
- Programs: Advanced mathematics, informatics, physics, and chemistry; elective programming tracks; English and a second foreign language required.
- Counselor contact: Deputy director for academics and competition coaches typically coordinate university outreach.

## Academics and programs
- Track emphasis: Informatics/CS streams alongside pure math classes; strong participation in national/Balkan Olympiads.
- Language profile: Serbian instruction with high English proficiency due to contest prep and international materials; students often pursue IELTS/SAT externally.
- Extracurriculars: Coding clubs, robotics teams, math circles, and research projects for fairs.

## Mobility and destinations
- Typical destinations: University of Novi Sad/Belgrade STEM faculties; Germany, Austria, Netherlands, Denmark for CS/engineering; a subset targets US/UK with scholarships.
- Scholarship sensitivity: High—families value clear merit aid, paid internships, and transparent housing costs.

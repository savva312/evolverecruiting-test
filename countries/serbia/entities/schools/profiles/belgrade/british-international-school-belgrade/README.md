**One-paragraph summary:**  
BIS Belgrade is a long-running private British international school in Belgrade delivering the Cambridge pathway through IGCSE and A Levels, with an optional University of Nebraska curriculum for a US high school diploma. The school achieved British School Overseas (BSO) accreditation and COBIS membership in 2025, underscores English-medium provision, and publishes fee details only during admissions interactions—signaling an affluent, internationally mobile parent base. Strong English fluency, Cambridge credentials, and a premium Dedinje/Senjak catchment make BIS a solid prospecting target for UNIC Nicosia and UNIC Athens across business, computing, and health sciences programs.

**Top opportunities:**  
- Cambridge IGCSE/A Level pathway with optional US high school diploma (University of Nebraska) aligns with UNIC entry requirements and offers dual-credential storytelling.  
- English-medium teaching plus BSO accreditation and COBIS membership (2025) provide quality and recognition signals for families and counselors.  
- Fee-on-request model and Dedinje campus imply high ability-to-pay families who may afford Medicine or premium programs.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- Tuition not published; affordability must be verified through counselor/admissions engagement (schedule “Admissions Conference”).

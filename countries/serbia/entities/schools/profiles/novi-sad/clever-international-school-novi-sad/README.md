**One-paragraph summary:**
Clever International School operates a Novi Sad campus offering Cambridge Lower/Upper Secondary and A Level preparation with bilingual (English/Serbian) delivery and optional third-language study. The campus publishes tuition of €655 per month across 11 payments (~€7,205 per year) for secondary and pre-university levels, making it a strong proxy for households able to afford UNIC tuition bands.【countries/serbia/reports/20251220-Serbia High-School Pipeline Report.md†L76-L81】 The school leans on a central admissions inbox and WhatsApp line, supports exam sitting through registered Cambridge venues (often in Belgrade), and promotes international university pathways—an angle that fits UNIC Nicosia and UNIC Athens positioning on EU-recognized degrees, Medicine/Health, CS/Data/AI, and Business.

**Top opportunities:**
- Published premium tuition in Novi Sad proves ability to pay and justifies a Medicine/Health upsell for top science candidates.【countries/serbia/reports/20251220-Serbia High-School Pipeline Report.md†L76-L81】
- English-first Cambridge teaching plus Serbian-language options make dual-language collateral effective; emphasize licensure/recognition for Athens and Nicosia.
- Centralized admissions contact (info@cleveris.org / WhatsApp) enables quick counselor discovery and webinar scheduling with minimal gatekeeping.

**Top risks or constraints:**
- Counselor structure, cohort size, and university outcomes are not published; high data gaps require first-call discovery.
- Cambridge exam sitting occurs at external venues; families already pay exam fees and may be fee-sensitive about additional costs.
- Governance spans Serbia and Estonia entities; partnership approvals may require network-level sign-off.

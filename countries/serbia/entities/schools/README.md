## High-potential feeder high schools (EUR affordability)

Use with the global schools-and-feeders skill and the global high school profile template. Tiering anchors on the ability-to-pay signals in the `countries/serbia/reports/20251220-Serbia High-School Pipeline Report.md` (premium secondary tuition that supports UNIC Athens and Medicine pricing).

**Tier definitions (aligned to global rubric)**
- **High** — published premium secondary tuition (roughly €7,000–€16,500+ or $28k) that strongly signals ability to pay €10k–€15k+ per year and Medicine price points.
- **Medium–High** — international schools with fees on request or smaller-city premium tuition; strong international intent but requires direct confirmation for pricing.

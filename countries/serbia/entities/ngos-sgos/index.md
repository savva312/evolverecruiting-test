# Serbia NGOs/SGOs index

Summary of non-governmental and semi-governmental bodies that influence outbound study funding, counseling, or student mobility from Serbia. Priorities reflect reach, relevance to UNIC/UNIC Athens programs, and ease of partnership.

## Priority organizations

| Organization | Focus | Geography/reach | Priority | Partnership angle | Profile |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Foundation Tempus / Euroguidance Serbia | Erasmus+ coordination, guidance, mobility counseling | National (Belgrade HQ with nationwide network) | High | Co-branded webinars on EU mobility, referral pathway for students exploring Cyprus/Greece options | [Profile](profiles/foundation-tempus-euroguidance.md) |
| EducationUSA Serbia | Advising for study in the United States; test prep and financial aid guidance | Belgrade and Novi Sad centers; national digital reach | High | Joint sessions on US vs. EU pathways and UNIC Athens/Nicosia positioning as alternatives | [Profile](profiles/educationusa-serbia.md) |
| Fund for Young Talents (“Dositeja”) | Government scholarship fund for top students (domestic and international studies) | National | High | Align UNIC Athens/Nicosia programs with eligible fields; propose info sessions for scholars | [Profile](profiles/fund-for-young-talents-dositeja.md) |
| Studenica Foundation | Merit- and need-based scholarships and mentorship for Serbian students | National with diaspora mentors | Medium-High | Scholarship top-up, mentorship integration, pipeline for STEM/social science applicants | [Profile](profiles/studenica-foundation.md) |
| Ana and Vlade Divac Foundation | Youth empowerment, scholarships, and community funds | National with strong regional coverage | Medium | Joint scholarships for underserved regions; career events linking Divac scholars to UNIC | [Profile](profiles/ana-i-vlade-divac-foundation.md) |
| Center for Education Policy | Policy research, counselor training, equity in education | National; collaborates with ministries and schools | Medium | Counselor training modules featuring UNIC pathways; research partnerships on mobility outcomes | [Profile](profiles/center-for-education-policy.md) |

### Notes on prioritization
- **High**: immediate value for mobility counseling or scholarship alignment with national reach.
- **Medium-High**: strong fit with some onboarding needed (e.g., integrating mentorship with UNIC messaging).
- **Medium**: relationship-building required; influence via training, community programs, or research collaborations.

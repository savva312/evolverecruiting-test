# Ana and Vlade Divac Foundation

- **Type:** Philanthropic NGO focused on youth empowerment, education, and community development  
- **Why it matters:** Runs scholarships and youth programs that reach underserved regions and can channel motivated students toward international study.

## Overview
The Ana and Vlade Divac Foundation funds **scholarships, youth empowerment projects, and community initiatives** across Serbia. Programs often prioritize students from lower-income or rural backgrounds, pairing financial support with community engagement requirements.

## Programs and services (recent highlights)
- **Divac Scholarships** for talented students with financial need (secondary and university levels).
- **Divac Youth Funds** supporting youth-led projects and leadership development in multiple Serbian municipalities.
- Career guidance and employability workshops delivered in partnership with local schools and companies.

## Target students and reach
- High school and university students from **underserved regions** seeking financial aid and mentorship.
- Youth activists engaged through community funds and leadership projects.

## Partnership fit with UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Co-design **scholarship top-ups** or travel bursaries for Divac scholars admitted to UNIC programs.
- Offer **career and mobility webinars** tailored to Divac’s regional partner schools (spotlighting English-taught programs).

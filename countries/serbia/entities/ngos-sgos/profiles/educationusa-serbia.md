# EducationUSA Serbia

- **Type:** Public-diplomacy NGO network (U.S. Department of State-supported advising centers)  
- **Why it matters:** Trusted, free advising on applications, testing, and financial aid for study abroad; reaches high-intent students and counselors.

## Overview
EducationUSA Serbia operates advising centers in **Belgrade (American Councils)** and **Novi Sad (University of Novi Sad)**. Advisors deliver one-on-one counseling, webinars, test preparation guidance, and scholarship navigation for students pursuing higher education abroad, primarily in the United States.

## Programs and services (recent highlights)
- **Five Steps to U.S. Study** advising, including essay/test prep guidance and application strategy.
- Regular **webinars and in-person info sessions** on scholarships, campus life, and financial aid.
- Counselor outreach to Serbian high schools and universities; collaboration with alumni for peer mentoring.
- Promotes competitive scholarships (Fulbright, Opportunity Funds, institutional aid) and credentialing steps.

## Target students and reach
- High school and university students aiming for **full-degree study abroad**; concentrated in Belgrade/Novi Sad but with national virtual reach.
- School counselors seeking reliable advising materials on international admissions.
- Alumni network engaged for mentoring and testimonials.

## Partnership fit with UNIC/UNIC Athens
- **Position UNIC Athens/Nicosia** as an EU-based alternative for students comparing US vs. EU options; highlight English-taught programs and costs.
- Co-host **financing and visa guidance sessions** featuring side-by-side US/EU pathways to broaden advising toolkit.
- Invite advisors to **familiarization webinars** and share tailored program sheets for STEM/business/health tracks.

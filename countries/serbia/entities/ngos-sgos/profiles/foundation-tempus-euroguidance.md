# Foundation Tempus / Euroguidance Serbia

- **Type:** Semi-governmental (Erasmus+ national agency and career guidance center)  
- **Why it matters:** Controls Erasmus+ mobility guidance in Serbia and runs Euroguidance/Europass services that reach secondary schools, universities, and counselors.

## Overview
Foundation Tempus implements **Erasmus+** in Serbia and hosts **Euroguidance Serbia** and **Europass**, providing information, training, and counseling on European mobility and qualifications recognition. Acts as a hub for schools, universities, and career centers planning outbound mobility.

## Programs and services (recent highlights)
- Erasmus+ national contact point, including higher education and youth mobility actions; supports application clinics and info days.
- **Euroguidance Serbia** career guidance portal, webinars, and counselor training sessions across Serbia.
- **Europass** CV and credential guidance; supports comparability of qualifications for international applications.
- Publishes annual mobility call summaries and organizes pre-departure orientations for Erasmus+ participants.

## Target students and reach
- University students pursuing **exchange or full-degree mobility** across Europe; regular touchpoints via universities and student organizations.
- Secondary school counselors and career centers (national coverage through Euroguidance trainings).
- Strong digital reach through webinars, newsletters, and resource portals (Belgrade HQ with nationwide network).

# Center for Education Policy (CEP)

- **Type:** Non-profit research and policy organization  
- **Why it matters:** Influences education policy, counselor capacity-building, and equity initiatives; strong gateway to schools and ministries.

## Overview
The Center for Education Policy is a Belgrade-based NGO that conducts **research, policy analysis, and pilot programs** to improve education quality and equity in Serbia. CEP collaborates with ministries, schools, and international partners on topics such as inclusive education, credential recognition, and career guidance.

## Programs and services (recent highlights)
- Policy research on **equity, inclusion, and quality assurance** in Serbian education.
- **Counselor and teacher training projects** (often EU-funded) that include guidance on career pathways and mobility.
- Coordination of pilots with schools and municipalities to test guidance and transition-support models.

## Target students and reach
- Indirect reach through **school counselors, teachers, and policymakers**; strongest in Belgrade with national project coverage via EU-funded consortia.
- Focus on students from vulnerable groups benefiting from improved guidance and transition support.

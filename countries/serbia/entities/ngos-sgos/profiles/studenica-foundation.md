# Studenica Foundation

- **Type:** Philanthropic NGO focused on scholarships and mentorship  
- **Why it matters:** Provides sustained financial aid and mentoring for Serbian students, including those studying abroad, with a strong alumni/mentor base.

## Overview
The Studenica Foundation offers **merit- and need-based scholarships** for Serbian students and pairs recipients with Serbian diaspora professionals for mentoring. It emphasizes leadership, community service, and academic excellence, with grants typically supporting tuition or living costs.

## Programs and services (recent highlights)
- Annual **scholarship calls** for undergraduate and graduate students; support can extend to studies at foreign universities.
- **Mentorship network** connecting scholars with Serbian professionals abroad (STEM, business, humanities).
- Occasional **travel or project microgrants** tied to academic achievement and community impact (varies by call).

## Target students and reach
- High-performing Serbian students in **STEM, social sciences, humanities, and arts**, studying domestically or abroad.
- Strong presence among students pursuing opportunities in Western Europe and North America via alumni referrals.

## Partnership fit with UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Offer **co-branded scholarship top-ups** for Studenica recipients admitted to UNIC programs.
- Integrate UNIC faculty/alumni into the **mentorship network** and career webinars for Serbian scholars.

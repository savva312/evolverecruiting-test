# Fund for Young Talents (“Dositeja”)

- **Type:** Semi-governmental scholarship fund (Ministry of Youth and Sports)  
- **Why it matters:** Flagship national scholarship scheme for top Serbian students pursuing domestic or international studies, including master’s programs abroad.

## Overview
The **Fund for Young Talents of the Republic of Serbia** (popularly “Dositeja”) awards annual scholarships to high-achieving Serbian students. The fund prioritizes academic excellence and fields aligned with national development priorities, supporting both domestic and international degree study.

## Programs and services (recent highlights)
- **International master’s scholarships** (“Dositeja” program) covering living costs for study at accredited foreign universities.
- **Domestic excellence scholarships** for undergraduate and master’s students in Serbia.
- Publishes competitive calls each year with detailed eligible fields, GPA thresholds, and documentation requirements.

## Target students and reach
- High-GPA undergraduate and master’s students seeking **international degrees**, particularly in STEM, economics, law, and social sciences (per recent calls).
- Nationwide applicant pool; strong visibility through university communications and ministry channels.

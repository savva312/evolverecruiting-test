# World Education Fair (Integral)

## Overview
Flagship Serbian study-abroad expo organized by Integral, typically run twice per year with multiple city stops and a parallel virtual fair. Strong mix of high schoolers, undergraduates, and parents exploring EU/UK/US options.

## Timing and format
- **Typical months:** February/March (spring) and September/October (fall) — dates published ~3–4 months prior.
- **Next expected editions:** Spring 2025 (late Feb–early Mar) and Fall 2025 (late Sep–mid Oct); expect Spring/Fall 2026 repeats in same windows.
- **Cities:** Belgrade, Nis, Kragujevac, Novi Sad (some seasons drop Kragujevac); 1–2 days per city.
- **Format:** In-person booths plus optional seminar slots; 1–2 virtual fair days.

## Audience and expected reach
- Footfall per season: ~3,000–4,000 visitors across all cities.
- Priority cities: Belgrade (1,200–1,500 visits), Nis (700–900), Novi Sad (500–700).
- Lead quality: high-intent seniors + parents; good counselor presence at Belgrade; strong STEM/business interest.

## Pricing (indicative)
- **In-person:** €1.5k–€3k per city depending on booth size/location and marketing add-ons.
- **Virtual:** €500–€1,000 per virtual day with lead bundle.
- Discounts available for multi-city packages and early booking; shipping/printing extra.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** English-medium EU degrees; affordability vs UK; medical/CS/business draws; visa pathway clarity.
- **UNIC Athens:** Proximity, lifestyle, Mediterranean location; fast travel from Serbia; programs overlapping with Nicosia (business/psychology/fintech) for students preferring mainland EU.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: book spring and fall slots; localize landing page + QR; confirm seminar topics (e.g., “Medicine vs CS pathways from Serbia”).
- During: deploy Serbian-speaking staff; capture scans + short form; collect counselor business cards and schedule school visits while in city.
- Post: 24–72 hour follow-up with campus-specific program lists, scholarship info, and webinar invites; tag leads by city and intake; brief agents with lead lists if co-managed.

## Risks and notes
- Dates shift year to year; confirm when calendar is released.
- Shipping to Serbia can be slow near peak season; carry lightweight collateral or print locally.

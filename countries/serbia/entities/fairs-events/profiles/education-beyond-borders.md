# Education Beyond Borders (EBB) – Belgrade

## Overview
Large international education exhibition held in Belgrade with strong domestic media coverage. Good reach to motivated high-school students, parents, and language-school attendees. Usually offers seminar slots and media bundles.

## Timing and format
- **Typical months:** March/April (spring edition) and October/November (fall edition); two days per edition.
- **Next expected editions:** Spring 2025 (mid-late Mar) and Fall 2025 (mid Oct–mid Nov); anticipate Spring/Fall 2026 repeats.
- **Location:** Belgrade Fair halls or major hotels (venue varies by edition—confirm on booking).
- **Format:** Booth exhibition with optional presentation/seminar slots and paid marketing boosts.

## Audience and expected reach
- Visitor volume: ~5,000 over two days (mix of students, parents, young professionals).
- Lead capture expectation: 300–500 qualified scans with good opt-in messaging; counselor presence moderate; solid humanities/business interest plus growing CS.
- Lead quality: solid for in-person follow-up; best results from students attending presentations and parents who visit booth twice.

## Pricing (indicative)
- Standard booth packages: €1,700–€2,800 depending on size and sponsorship add-ons (logo placement, catalogue ads, presentation slot).
- Additional costs: shipping/local printing, interpreter (if needed), travel within Belgrade.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** Accredited EU degrees, English-medium delivery, medicine/fintech/blockchain strengths, scholarships vs Western Europe.
- **UNIC Athens:** Quick travel from Serbia, culturally close, lower living costs than Western Europe; position as mainland EU option with English-taught programs.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: reserve booth + seminar; craft Serbian-language session (15–20 minutes) focused on admissions and scholarships; prep QR-linked landing page with both campuses.
- During: prioritize badge scanning + short-form opt-in; capture counselor contacts from attending schools; schedule 1–2 coffee chats with Belgrade-based agents.
- Post: send thank-you within 48 hours; invite hot leads to virtual open day; share counselor toolkit (program sheets, intake dates, contact emails).

## Risks and notes
- Peak weekend timing can dilute adult attendance; focus scanning on interested students to avoid low-intent bulk leads.
- Presentation slots can book out early; secure during booth booking.

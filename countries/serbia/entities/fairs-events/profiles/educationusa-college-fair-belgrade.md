# EducationUSA / Fulbright College Fair – Belgrade

## Overview
Annual college fair organized by Fulbright Serbia (EducationUSA). Primarily focused on U.S. institutions but welcomes regional/EU universities that offer English-taught programs and U.S. credit transfer pathways. Strong counselor attendance from elite language schools.

## Timing and format
- **Typical month:** October (one-day event, usually afternoon/evening).
- **Next expected editions:** Oct 2025; anticipate similar Oct 2026 slot if demand remains high.
- **Location:** Belgrade (central hotel or conference venue).
- **Format:** Booth tables; short info sessions may be available.

## Audience and expected reach
- 400–600 students and parents; high English proficiency and strong academic backgrounds.
- 30–60 school counselors from top language/gymnasium schools attend.
- Lead quality: academically strong, U.S.-leaning families open to EU options when priced and accredited clearly.

## Pricing (indicative)
- Often free for non-U.S. institutions when space permits; otherwise €0–€300 table fee.
- Travel and collateral are primary costs.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** Highlight U.S. partnerships/credit transfer, ABET/EPAS/ACCA recognitions, and affordability vs U.S.; stress English-medium instruction.
- **UNIC Athens:** Emphasize proximity, Schengen travel convenience, and EU degree recognition; pitch as alternative for students seeking U.S.-style campus life closer to home.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: request booth early; prepare one-page handout for counselors with admissions and scholarship info; confirm if presentations are allowed.
- During: collect counselor cards; invite them to virtual counselor briefing; scan/QR leads with clear privacy notice.
- Post: follow up within 48 hours with tailored emails (students: programs + scholarships; counselors: toolkit + visit scheduling); add high-intent leads to webinar invites.

## Risks and notes
- Event brand is U.S.-centric; craft messaging to align with English-medium and mobility pathways.
- Table locations may vary; arrive early to secure good placement and test QR/wi-fi.

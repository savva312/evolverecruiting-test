## Detailed briefs & action notes

### World Education Fair / EDUfair Serbia (Integral)
- **Why it matters:** Highest-volume Serbian expo with clear spring/fall cadence; Integral confirmed 8–9 Mar 2026 city stops and uses Hilton Belgrade + Hotel Ambasador Niš (fee €3,480 for both). Bastions for direct student + parent leads and counselor meetups.
- **2026 deliverables:** Reserve 18 m² premium booth in Belgrade to host mini-seminars; add seminar slot focused on “UNIC Pathways to Medicine/CS”. Synchronize travel so team can schedule school visits (Chartwell, Brook Hill, ISB) on transit days.
- **Ops checklist:** Ship lightweight collateral to Belgrade office by 15 Feb to avoid customs; confirm interpreter provided by Integral knows UNIC program names; pre-register UTMs for lead capture forms; align CRM tags by city/intake.
- **Risks & mitigations:** Event overlaps with International Women’s Day—expect city traffic and activism; budget extra transit time.

### Begin High School University Fair (Belgrade)
- **Why it matters:** Gives intimate access to international and bilingual schools (Crnjanski, Brook Hill, Rudjer Bošković). Attendance capped (~150–250 students/counselors), so conversations are in-depth and counselor relationships strong.
- **2026 deliverables:** Request the **16 Feb 2026** stop that includes Brook Hill to reach IB cohorts. Offer mini scholarship diagnostic to encourage sign-ups for virtual counseling.
- **Ops checklist:** Prepare 5-minute Serbian elevator pitch; bring digital eligibility checklist to run on tablets; set aside the €1,450 participation fee + travel.

### EducationUSA Southeastern Europe Tour — Belgrade
- **Why it matters:** EducationUSA advisers curate academically strong families; also opens doors with top counselors and agents seeking U.S./EU hybrids.
- **2026 deliverables:** Submit tour application the day it opens; volunteer to host side-session on scholarship packaging for Serbian families leaning U.S. but open to Cyprus/Greece.
- **Ops checklist:** Build mini-site comparing UNIC Nicosia vs U.S. public university cost; prep letter for counselors summarizing visa timelines; plan for evening event (17:00–19:30) so team can run morning school visits.
- **Dependencies:** Await EducationUSA confirmation email; track via International Academic Center newsletter.

## Prep & follow-up playbook (apply to every event)
1. **Localization:** Refresh Serbian copy, tuition tables, and scholarship calculator before each event; include UNIC Athens housing + visa updates.
2. **Lead hygiene:** Create event-specific CRM campaigns with auto-assign rules by campus and intake. Upload scanner/CSV files within 24h; send thank-you email (Serbian + English) within 48h.
3. **Counselor care:** Offer 30-min virtual briefings after every in-person event; send updated counselor toolkit (PDF + video) with localized deadlines.
4. **Travel pairing:** When two events fall within the same week (e.g., BICE + EducationUSA), plan combined travel to squeeze in high-value school/agent meetings.

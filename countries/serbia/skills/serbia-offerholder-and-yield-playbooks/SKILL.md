# Serbia addendum: offerholder & yield playbooks

Use `/skills/offerholder-and-yield-playbooks/SKILL.md` for cadences and data capture. Apply these Serbia-specific notes.

## Channel and language norms
- Lead with email and Viber/phone; WhatsApp is secondary and should be used only with opt-in. Provide Serbian templates by default and English versions for international schools.

## Timing anchors
- Lighten outreach during Matura/final exam periods and immediately afterward; resume higher-intensity cadences once results are available.
- Discuss housing and travel planning early for Athens-bound students; for Nicosia-bound students, outline visa steps without promising expedited timelines.

## Compliance and documentation
- Do not store passports, national IDs, or financial documents in the repo. Track only application IDs, contact info, and checklist status.
- Log every touch with channel, language, and owner in the country data model; capture consent source/time for any messaging groups.

## Objection handling to localize
- Cost: keep pricing in EUR; if RSD estimates are requested, include FX date and avoid implying fixed local pricing.
- Recognition/licensure: use the Serbia recognition and licensure skill (`countries/serbia/skills/serbia-recognition-and-licensure/`) for sourced statements.

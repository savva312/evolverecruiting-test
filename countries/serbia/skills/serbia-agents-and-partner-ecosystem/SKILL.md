## Serbia-specific partner emphases

- **Partner types to prioritize:** generalist agencies with Belgrade/Novi Sad/Nis coverage; medicine-focused consultancies with Balkans reach; fair organizers running Serbia + regional tours; counseling hubs inside leading gymnasiums/international schools.
- **City tags:** start with `Belgrade`, `Novi Sad`, `Nis`, `Kragujevac`; add others when partners demonstrate presence.
- **Program-fit tags:** default to `medicine`, `cs-data-ai`, `business`; add `engineering` or `arts-design` if supported with evidence.
- **Engagement cues:** capture Serbian/English collateral needs, willingness to promote both UNIC campuses, and preferred timelines relative to Matura exam periods.
- **Compliance reminders:** confirm GDPR-aligned handling for EU destinations and clarity on student fee/commission policies; flag if partners promote rebate-like incentives.

# Serbia addendum: events and fairs

Use `/skills/events-and-fairs/SKILL.md` for the core workflow. Apply these Serbia-specific considerations.

## Organizer norms and assets
- Focus on Belgrade and Novi Sad fairs plus school career days; confirm booth power/Wi‑Fi and carry printed QR codes as backup.
- Provide Serbian-language signage and handouts; keep English versions for international tracks.

## Lead capture and consent
- Display a GDPR-aligned consent statement on tablets/paper forms; log opt-in source and date.
- Collect minimal fields (name, role, school, email/phone/Viber if consented, city, program interest). Avoid birth dates or grades.
- Verify organizer consent language before importing badge-scan data into `countries/serbia/data/`.

## Staffing and follow-up
- Staff at least one Serbian-speaking representative; have an English speaker for international schools and keep a concise UNIC vs UNIC Athens comparison ready.
- Send Serbian recaps within 48 hours, tagging event and city; include English attachments when relevant.
- Log leads in the country data model with event name/date, city, and campus interest (Nicosia vs Athens) before launching sequences.
- Route hot leads to webinars/application workshops scheduled after exam results; tag event names and campus preference in the follow-up log.

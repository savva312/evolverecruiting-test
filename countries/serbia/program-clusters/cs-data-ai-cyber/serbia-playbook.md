# Serbia playbook — CS, Data, AI, and Cyber

## Messaging angles to test
- Links to European tech hubs and internship access.
- Applied learning projects with industry mentors.
- Scholarships or flexible payment plans for Serbian students.

## Channels to prioritize
- Tech meetups, hackathons, and STEM high school clubs.
- Digital campaigns on platforms popular with Serbian developers.
- Partnerships with IT outsourcing firms for referral pipelines.

## Local partners or feeders
Identify coding schools, IT associations, and employer groups that can co-host events or share leads.

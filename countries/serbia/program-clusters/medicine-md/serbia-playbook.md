# Serbia playbook — Medicine / MD

## Messaging angles to test
- Accreditation and recognition details (EU/UK/US boards).
- Clinical rotation locations and early patient exposure.
- Residency match outcomes and mobility within the EU.

## Channels to prioritize
- School counsellor outreach and pre-med clubs.
- Webinars with faculty or alumni practicing in Serbia.
- Partnerships with test-prep centers for biology/chemistry.

## Local partners or feeders
Document agencies, counsellors, or preparatory schools with medical focus; note contract status and performance metrics.

### Competitor-linked outreach notes
- **Czech/Hungary prep centers:** Coordinate with Belgrade-based advisors (e.g., preparatory courses that pipeline to Masaryk/Charles/Semmelweis) to position UNIC’s English-only clinical pathway and Cyprus/Greece living costs.
- **Serbian public med schools:** For families price-shopping MU Belgrade/Nis/Novi Sad, highlight UNIC’s international rotations and English-medium patient interaction through graduation to justify price delta.
- **Poland/Slovakia fairs:** Attend study-in-Poland/Slovakia themed fairs (often hosted in Belgrade/Novi Sad high schools) to capture students before they commit to Gdańsk or Comenius seats.

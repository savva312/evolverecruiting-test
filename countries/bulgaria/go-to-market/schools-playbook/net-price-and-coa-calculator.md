# Net price + Cost of Attendance (COA) mini-calculator — Bulgaria (EUR)
Last updated: 2025-12-22 | Owner: Counselor success

This is a **table-based worksheet in Markdown** (not a spreadsheet). Use it to give families a consistent, transparent “what will it cost?” view for **UNIC Nicosia** and **UNIC Athens**.

## Guardrails (use in every conversation)

- **Do not quote tuition from memory.** Always use the current official fee schedule or the Admissions quote for the specific programme/intake.
- Scholarships/discounts are **confirmed only in the offer letter**; do not promise outcomes beyond what is written.
- COA is a **planning estimate**. Housing choice is usually the #1 driver of variance.

## B) Net tuition (calculate)

Use this sequence (keep it simple and auditable):

1) **Merit / need scholarship** (percentage of tuition):  
`Scholarship amount = Annual tuition × scholarship %`

2) **Discounts** (percentage of tuition, if eligible and allowed to combine):  
`Discount amount = Annual tuition × discount %`

3) **Net tuition** (before any one-time fees):  
`Net tuition = Annual tuition − scholarship amount − discount amount − fixed credits (if any)`

## 3) Phone call script (bullets) + voicemail fallback

### Call script (2–4 minutes)
- **Open**: “Hello `{{Counselor_Name}}`, this is `{{Your_Name}}` from the University of Nicosia (UNIC). Did I catch you at an okay time?”
- **Purpose (10 sec)**: “I’m calling to see if `{{School_Name}}` would be open to a short student information session—options for UNIC **Nicosia** and **Athens**.”
- **Value (20–30 sec)**: “We keep it counselor‑friendly: clear entry requirements, application timeline, and student support. I can share a ready toolkit link for your students/parents.”
- **Dual-campus positioning (15 sec)**: read the two one‑sentence lines from the copy block.
- **Ask (15 sec)**: “Would `{{Proposed_Date_1}}` or `{{Proposed_Date_2}}` work for a 30–45 minute session?”
- **Qualify (30–60 sec)** (pick 2–3):
  - “Which cohorts should we prioritize (IB / A‑levels / language track / STEM)?”
  - “Any programs that typically attract interest (Medicine/CS/Business)?”
  - “Would students prefer in‑person or virtual?”
  - “Are you comfortable if we send you one follow‑up via Viber, or should we keep everything on email?”
- **Close**: confirm next step + send toolkit link + calendar hold.

### Voicemail fallback (20–30 sec)
“Hello `{{Counselor_Name}}`, this is `{{Your_Name}}` from the University of Nicosia. I’m calling about a short student information session for `{{School_Name}}`—UNIC Nicosia and UNIC Athens options. I’ll send a brief email with two date options and a counselor toolkit link. You can reach me at `{{Your_Phone}}`. Thank you.”

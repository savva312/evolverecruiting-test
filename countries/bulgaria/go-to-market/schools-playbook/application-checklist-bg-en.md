# Application checklist (Bulgaria) — UNIC Nicosia & UNIC Athens (BG/EN)
Last updated: 2025-12-22 | Owner: Counselor success

Purpose: a counselor-ready checklist for collecting the right documents, early, so offers can be issued quickly (ideally before the Bulgarian Matura blackout window in late May–mid June).

Important (recognition / regulated professions):
- This checklist is **not** legal advice and does not guarantee recognition/licensure in Bulgaria.
- For regulated professions (e.g., medicine, nursing, pharmacy, law), students must **verify requirements with the competent Bulgarian authority** and plan lead time. Use: `../../market/recognition-and-licensure.md`
  - Reference: [recognition-and-licensure.md](../../market/recognition-and-licensure.md)

## 1) Quick “before you start” (BG/EN)

**BG**
1. Потвърдете кампуса и програмата: **UNIC Nicosia (Кипър)** или **UNIC Athens (Гърция)**.  
2. Потвърдете приема (intake): обичайно **Fall (Sept/Oct)**; при някои програми има и Spring.  
3. Ако студентът цели **регулирана професия** в България: започнете от `../../market/recognition-and-licensure.md` и планирайте допълнителни документи (практики/часове, сертификати и др.).

**EN**
1. Confirm campus + programme: **UNIC Nicosia (Cyprus)** or **UNIC Athens (Greece)**.  
2. Confirm intake: typically **Fall (Sep/Oct)**; some programmes may have Spring.  
3. If the student targets a **regulated profession** in Bulgaria: start with `../../market/recognition-and-licensure.md` and plan for additional evidence (placements/hours, certificates, etc.).

## 3) Process checklist (counselor operations)

1. **Lead capture (same day):** name, programme interest, intake, English level, graduation year, parent contact consent.
2. **Fit check (48 hours):** campus recommendation + 2–3 programme options + recognition note if regulated.
3. **Affordability (48 hours):** share the scholarship summary and net price/COA calculator (see links in `counselor-toolkit.md`).
4. **Application build (1–2 weeks):** collect minimum documents to submit a complete file; request conditional path if diploma/Matura pending.
5. **Offer follow-up (within 72 hours of offer):** confirm scholarship/discount decision (if any), deposit steps, and housing guidance.

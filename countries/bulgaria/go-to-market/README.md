# Go-to-market playbooks

This folder consolidates channel-specific playbooks for recruiting Bulgarian students to UNIC and UNIC Athens. Keep each playbook actionable with checklists, owners, timelines, and links to supporting entities or market research.

Subfolders:
- `schools-playbook/` – outreach and visit templates for secondary schools.
- `agents-playbook/` – onboarding, SLAs, and compliance for agent partners.
- `digital-playbook/` – digital channel strategy, landing pages, UTMs, and creative references.
- `offerholder-and-yield/` – tactics for nurturing admits through deposit, housing, and arrival.

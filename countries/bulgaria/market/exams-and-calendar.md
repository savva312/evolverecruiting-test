# Bulgaria — exams & school calendar (focus: 2025–26; outlook: 2026–27)

**Checked on:** 2025-12-22  
**Why this matters:** Bulgaria’s Grade 12 (DZI/Matura) and Grade 7/10 (NVO) exam windows materially reduce response rates to outbound and slow document collection. Use the exact dates below to plan “push” vs “quiet” periods in your funnel for **UNIC Nicosia** and **UNIC Athens**.

## What this page covers (and what it doesn’t)

- **Covered (exact dates):** national exam schedules for the **2025–26** cycle (i.e., exams in 2026) where MoES orders are published.
- **Not covered (exact dates):** the full K–12 vacation/term calendar (winter/spring breaks, last day of school) because those are published separately and can vary by grade and by school. Treat non-exam items below as **typical patterns** unless separately verified.
- **2026–27 outlook:** as of 2025-12-22, MoES exam orders for the **2026–27** cycle (exams in 2027) were **not found in accessible official releases**; do not guess—update when published.

## Month-by-month (2025–26 cycle) — outreach windows built around exams

This is designed for **campaign planning**, not as an official school calendar.

- **Sep–Nov 2025 (high leverage):** school-year ramp + advisor planning + early fairs. Run school/counselor visits, parent webinars, and early pipeline capture for Grade 11/12.
- **Dec 2025–Jan 2026 (mixed):** higher inbox competition and interruptions. Run digital follow-ups, webinar replays, scholarship explainers, and application “micro-deadlines”.
- **Feb–Apr 2026 (conversion build):** resume higher-touch outreach; run 1:1 clinics (program fit, affordability, entry requirements), and lock in document checklists.
- **May–early Jun 2026 (DZI blackout):** avoid heavy school-based activations around **20 May**, **22 May**, and **26 May–02 Jun**. Shift to low-friction reminders and “after exams” booking links. ([DZI-RD09-2072])
- **Mid–late Jun 2026 (NVO + end-of-year congestion):** avoid parent-heavy messaging around **17 Jun**, **19 Jun**, **22 Jun** (Grade 7/10) and focus on Grade 12 yield + documentation. ([NVO-RD09-2074])
- **By mid‑Jun 2026 (results sprint):** plan staffing and SLA for document verification and final-offer issuance as **DZI results** are published by **11 Jun**. ([DZI-RD09-2072])
- **Jul 2026 (decisions + paperwork):** strong window for offerholder conversion, visa/residency guidance, and housing planning.
- **Late Aug 2026 (retake window):** separate CRM track for retakers around **21 Aug**, **24 Aug**, **25–28 Aug**; keep conditional offers alive with explicit requirements and timelines. ([DZI-RD09-2072])

## Recruiting implications (do / don’t)

**Do**
- **Front-load urgency:** aim to have most Grade 12 applications and document checklists “complete or pending only DZI” by **early May 2026**.
- **Pre-book post-exam appointments:** during May/June exam windows, push 15‑minute “after-exams” calendar links instead of long calls.
- **Run a results-to-offer war room:** pre-assign ownership for transcript collection, verification, and offer letter generation in the **11 Jun 2026** results window.

**Don’t**
- Don’t schedule high-stakes school visits or “apply now” pushes on (or immediately before) **20 May**, **22 May**, **17 Jun**, **19 Jun**, **22 Jun 2026**. ([DZI-RD09-2072]) ([NVO-RD09-2074])
- Don’t treat late August as a “quiet month”: the **21–28 Aug 2026** DZI retake period is a conversion opportunity for a distinct segment. ([DZI-RD09-2072])

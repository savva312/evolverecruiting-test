# Bulgaria addendum: offerholder & yield playbooks

Start with `/skills/offerholder-and-yield-playbooks/SKILL.md` for cadences and data capture. Layer these Bulgaria-specific notes for execution.

## Channel and language norms
- Prioritize email + Viber/phone for parents and counselors; confirm WhatsApp before sharing documents. Provide Bulgarian copies of key messages; keep English versions for IB/international schools.
- Schedule live sessions in late afternoon local time to accommodate school schedules and parents’ work hours.

## Timing anchors
- Avoid heavy outreach during Matura preparation (May) and immediately after exam results when families travel; resume intensity once graduation events conclude.
- For Athens-specific offers, emphasize relocation logistics and housing timelines early, as parents expect clarity on accommodation.

## Compliance and documentation
- Do not request national ID numbers; limit to application IDs and contact information already provided with consent.
- Record every touch (channel, language, owner) in the country data model and tag whether the student is Athens- or Nicosia-leaning for downstream reporting.

## Objection handling to localize
- Cost/FX: quote in EUR and, if asked for BGN, present as an estimate with the conversion date.
- Recognition/visa: link to the Bulgaria recognition and licensure skill (`countries/bulgaria/skills/bulgaria-recognition-and-licensure/`) for sourced language before responding.

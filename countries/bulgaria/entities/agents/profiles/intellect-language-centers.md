- **Summary**: Network of Bulgarian language centers offering exam prep and language training with an added study abroad counseling desk for university and college placement.
- **Offices (Bulgaria)**: Multiple language schools in Sofia, Varna, and other cities per site map; counseling offered centrally with online appointments (assumption: onsite advising available at larger centers).

- **Destinations (EU vs non-EU)**: EU focus via English-taught programs (UK, Germany, Netherlands, Cyprus/Greece) plus Canada/US for students completing exam prep.
- **Volume & signals**: Continuous exam-prep cohorts provide predictable pipeline; actual placement counts not public (assumption: dozens of higher-ed placements annually stemming from prep cohorts).

- **Commercial posture**: Mix of tuition commissions and possible counseling fees; require transparency and enforce “commissions on published tuition, no rebates.”

- **Contacts**: Center managers in Sofia/Varna plus central counseling inbox; secure a dedicated higher-ed counselor to own UNIC intake.

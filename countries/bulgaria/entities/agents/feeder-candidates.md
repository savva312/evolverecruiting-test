# Bulgarian agent feeder candidates (UNIC Nicosia + UNIC Athens)

Prioritized roster of Bulgarian education agents/counselors to feed UNIC Nicosia and UNIC Athens. Tiers reflect expected throughput, brand fit, and operational readiness. Campus notes highlight when to emphasize Athens (mainland EU/urban) vs Nicosia (Medicine/health/value English-taught degrees). EU vs non-EU experience is noted to set expectations on visa/compliance support.

## Tiering
- **Tier 1 (anchors)** – proven reach, multi-city visibility, and experience with EU placements; fast-track SLAs and co-marketing.
- **Tier 2 (growth)** – targeted niches or solid digital reach; pilot with campus-split webinars and clear QA gates.
- **Watchlist/monitor** – narrower focus (travel/exchange) or limited higher-ed proof; engage after QA and small pilots.

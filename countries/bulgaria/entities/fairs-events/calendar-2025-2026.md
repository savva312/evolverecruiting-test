## Notes on prioritization

- **Must-do**: highest volume and brand visibility; lock budgets and travel early.
- **Consider**: useful for pipeline and regional positioning; attend if schedules and budgets allow.
- **Monitor**: join selectively when timing/format suits counselor coverage or digital lead gen.

## Preparation checklist (shared across events)

- Localize landing pages with Bulgarian copy, UTM-tagged forms, and campus-specific FAQs.
- Pre-fair email to pre-registered students and counselors with session schedule and value props for Nicosia vs Athens.
- On-site lead capture: QR + short form (program interest, intake, GPA/IELTS bands), badge scan backups, and consent language in Bulgarian.
- Staffing: at least one Bulgarian-speaking rep or interpreter; align virtual staffing windows to expected traffic peaks.
- Post-fair: 24–72 hour follow-up sequence (thank-you, program links, scholarship info, counselor toolkit) and routing into CRM by campus + intake.

# Unify Study Abroad Roadshow (Bulgaria)

## Overview
Agency-led thematic roadshow run by Unify (Bulgarian study abroad agency) with focus on Netherlands/Germany/UK/EU programs. Typically hosts in-person open days and mini-fairs in Sofia and Varna, occasionally Plovdiv, with pre-registered students.

## Timing and format
- **Typical months:** March/April (spring) and October/November (autumn); single-day events per city.
- **Cities:** Sofia, Varna (sometimes Plovdiv) at hotels or coworking venues.
- **Format:** Table-top booths, short presentations, and agency-led Q&A.

## Audience and expected reach
- 100–300 attendees per city; strong interest in business, CS/IT, psychology, creative industries in the Netherlands/UK.
- Leads are pre-registered via agency lists; good conversion to counseling appointments.

## Pricing (indicative)
- €700–€1,500 per city depending on package and marketing exposure; discounts for multi-city bundles.
- Additional costs: travel and shipping/light collateral.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** Emphasize English-medium EU degrees with lower tuition vs NL/UK; spotlight computing, cybersecurity, fintech, and medicine pathways.
- **UNIC Athens:** Pitch as mainland EU alternative with Mediterranean lifestyle, close flight time, and competitive tuition; highlight business/psychology/CS.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: request 2025 calendar; negotiate bundle across Sofia + Varna; supply Bulgarian-language landing + QR to schedule counseling calls; align messaging with Unify counselors.
- During: collect leads via scans and agency CRM; offer quick eligibility checks; promote upcoming virtual open day.
- Post: sync lead list with Unify within 24 hours; send campus-specific follow-up; schedule counselor training webinar to keep messaging consistent.

## Risks and notes
- Audience tilt toward NL/UK; ensure messaging addresses ROI, employability, and tuition vs those markets.
- Some events can be crowded; bring clear signage and concise one-pagers.

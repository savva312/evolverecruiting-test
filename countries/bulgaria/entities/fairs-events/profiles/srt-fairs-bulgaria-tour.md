# SRT Fairs Bulgaria Tour

## Overview
SRT Fairs runs school-centered recruitment days with Bulgarian stops (typically Sofia plus a rotating Varna or Plovdiv leg). Events blend high school hall fairs, counselor briefings, and short presentations, delivering early-stage pipeline and counselor relationships.

## Timing and format
- **Typical months:** March/April (spring) and October (fall); calendars released 3–5 months prior.
- **Format:** Half-day school fairs with table space; optional presentations; evening counselor sessions in Sofia.

## Audience and expected reach
- 300–600 students per city; 50–80 counselors and school staff.
- 150–250 qualified leads per city when scanning plus organizer lead file are both used.

## Pricing (indicative)
- €1.6k–€2.2k per city; discounts for 2–3 city bundles.
- Travel/shipping not included; limited power/AV in school halls.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** Medicine, CS/data, and business degrees with English instruction; emphasize scholarships and graduate employability.
- **UNIC Athens:** Near-home EU campus; fast travel for families; strong for business/psychology and Mediterranean lifestyle.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: request final school list and counselor roster; ship lightweight collateral; prepare Bulgarian one-pagers by program cluster.
- During: log counselor names for follow-up; collect program-interest tags; schedule nearby school visits on adjacent days.
- Post: share counselor toolkit links; invite top schools to virtual counselor briefings; add leads to intake-specific nurture with Bulgarian copy.

## Risks and notes
- Tight school schedules reduce dwell time—bring concise QR forms and short scripts.
- Some stops skew earlier-year students; score leads for graduation year to prioritize follow-up.

# Begin Group International Education Fair (Sofia)

## Overview
Begin Group hosts twice-yearly international education fairs in Sofia with optional webinars before/after the in-person day. Mix of undergraduate and graduate prospects with strong interest in EU and UK pathways.

## Timing and format
- **Typical months:** February and October; dates announced ~3–4 months ahead.
- **Format:** One-day hotel/venue fair with seminar slots; organizer-led pre/post webinars that share registration data.

## Audience and expected reach
- 400–800 visitors; 120–220 qualified leads depending on scanning and webinar conversions.
- Audience split: final-year high schoolers, bachelor graduates exploring masters, and some working professionals.

## Pricing (indicative)
- €1.3k–€1.8k for standard table; branding/presentation add-ons ~€300–€500.
- Organizer webinars or lead-gen email blasts: €600–€1,000.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** Affordable EU-taught degrees; medicine, CS/data, fintech, and business as headline programs; scholarship hooks.
- **UNIC Athens:** Close-to-home EU campus; business/psychology/communications; easy travel for families.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: secure Bulgarian-language presentation slot; request attendee targeting options; prep segmented follow-up emails (UG vs PG).
- During: assign Bulgarian-speaking rep; capture program-of-interest + intake; promote campus visit/webinar dates.
- Post: send 24–48 hour follow-up; route hot leads to phone/WhatsApp; share counselor toolkit with school counselors met on-site.

## Risks and notes
- Marketing reach varies by edition; confirm organizer digital spend and co-promotion options.
- Venue space can be tight—bring compact booth assets and reliable hotspot backup.

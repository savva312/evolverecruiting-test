# QS Discover & Connect Sofia

## Overview
QS runs premium one-day fairs in Sofia that combine open-floor undergraduate and masters recruitment with invite-only graduate counseling and pre-scheduled meetings. Strong English-speaking pipeline and data-rich lead delivery make it valuable for both campuses.

## Timing and format
- **Typical months:** March and October in Sofia; QS usually publishes spring/fall calendars ~4–6 months ahead.
- **Format:** Afternoon/evening fair with seminar slots, CV reviews, and optional interview pods; occasional virtual follow-up webinars with shared registration lists.

## Audience and expected reach
- 400–700 registrants; 150–300 qualified leads (graduate-heavy but solid upper-year undergraduates).
- High English proficiency; strong interest in business, data/tech, health, and social sciences.

## Pricing (indicative)
- €2.5k–€3.5k exhibitor package including table, scanning, and post-event leads; sponsorship and presentation slots can add €1k–€2k.
- Discounts for early booking or multi-city CEE bundles.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** English-taught accredited masters (CS/data, fintech, public health, medicine pathways); scholarships vs UK/US.
- **UNIC Athens:** Proximity for in-person follow-up, EU lifestyle, business/psychology programs; leverage “study in the EU, stay near home” angle.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: secure data license and presentation slot; localize landing page; push invite lists via QS targeting; align masters portfolio with English score requirements.
- During: assign Bulgarian-speaking rep; triage graduate vs undergraduate inquiries; capture program-of-interest tags; schedule 1:1s on-site.
- Post: 24–48 hour follow-up by segment (masters vs UG); invite hot leads to campus-specific webinars; retarget with scholarships and intake reminders.

## Risks and notes
- Competitive field skewed to UK/Germany; differentiate on affordability and Mediterranean campuses.
- Lead quality depends on speed-to-contact; plan same-week calling/WhatsApp cadences.

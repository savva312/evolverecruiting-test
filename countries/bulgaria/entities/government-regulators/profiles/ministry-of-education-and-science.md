# Ministry of Education and Science (MoES)
last_checked: 2025-12-20

## Mandate
- Sets national education policy, curricula, and funding frameworks across pre-tertiary and higher education.
- Supervises national agencies involved in quality assurance and recognition (e.g., NEAA for accreditation, NACID for recognition and apostille services).【https://www.mon.bg】【https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ministry_of_Education_and_Science_(Bulgaria)】

## Relevance to UNIC / UNIC Athens
- Endorses bilateral and EU-level arrangements that shape recognition of Cypriot and Greek qualifications for Bulgarian students returning for employment or further study.
- Provides policy signals on priority fields, scholarship schemes, and outbound mobility support that can be leveraged in school and counselor outreach.
- Oversight of NACID means MoES guidance influences the evidence Bulgarian students need (e.g., legalized diplomas, translation requirements) when applying abroad.

## Key processes and timelines
- Publishes decrees and ordinances affecting recognition, secondary leaving certificate format, and diploma supplements—monitor for changes that could alter document sets needed by UNIC applicants.
- Coordinates with the National Statistical Institute and Euroguidance on outbound mobility reporting; updates typically released annually.
- Uses public consultations for regulatory changes; comment windows are usually 14–30 days—track for items touching recognition or scholarship rules.

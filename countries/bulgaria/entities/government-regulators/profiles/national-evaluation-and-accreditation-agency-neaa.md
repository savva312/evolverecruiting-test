# National Evaluation and Accreditation Agency (NEAA)
last_checked: 2025-12-20

## Mandate
- Independent national body responsible for institutional and programme accreditation of Bulgarian higher-education institutions.
- Conducts periodic reviews aligned to ESG standards; listed on the European Quality Assurance Register (EQAR).【https://www.eqar.eu/register/agencies/agency/?id=49】
- Issues decisions that determine whether programmes are recognized for state funding, student loans, and employer acceptance.

## Relevance to UNIC / UNIC Athens
- NEAA accreditation standards shape how Bulgarian partners benchmark foreign universities; high alignment with ESG helps position UNIC’s QA practices.
- Accreditation outcomes for Bulgarian partner institutions affect articulation agreements, exchange opportunities, and counselor confidence in credit transfer.
- NEAA precedents can be cited when clarifying recognition expectations for Bulgarian students returning with UNIC degrees.

## Key processes and timelines
- Cyclical institutional reviews typically every 6 years; programme evaluations on a 4–6 year cadence depending on risk profile (per ESG-compliant model).
- Evaluation dossiers include self-assessment reports, student feedback, and site visits; final decisions published in NEAA registers and mirrored in EQAR results.
- Appeals and follow-up monitoring can adjust accreditation validity—track partner institutions’ statuses before joint outreach.

# National Center for Information and Documentation (NACID)
last_checked: 2025-12-22

## Mandate
- Bulgaria’s ENIC-NARIC; evaluates foreign secondary and higher-education qualifications for academic and professional recognition.
- Maintains the national apostille register and issues apostilles for education documents through the apostille portal.【https://nacid.bg/en】【https://apostille.bg/】
- Operates public registries for academic recognition decisions and regulated professions, enabling verification by employers and institutions.【https://portal.nacid.bg/registry】

## Relevance to UNIC / UNIC Athens
- Determines equivalence of UNIC Nicosia and UNIC Athens degrees for Bulgarian graduates seeking employment or further study at home.
- Recognition decisions influence how counselors perceive UNIC program credibility; proactive dossier submissions can reduce student friction.
- Apostille and translation requirements managed by NACID govern the document packs Bulgarian applicants must prepare before departure and upon return.

## Key processes and timelines
- Applications for recognition and apostilles are submitted electronically via https://portal.nacid.bg; decisions and status updates are posted in the portal.
- NACID publishes service-specific checklists (e.g., legalized original, sworn translation, proof of study mode) and rejects incomplete files without queue placement—ensure applicants follow the published lists.
- Academic recognition of foreign higher-education degrees (Bachelor/Master) is stated as **free of charge** with a statutory term of **up to 2 months** (often ~1 month in practice when no additional data is required).【https://nacid.bg/en/foreign_qualifications_in_bulgaria_b_m/bm_page_faq/】【https://nacid.bg/en/foreign-diplomas-in-bulgaria-bachelor-master/bm_page_time/】
- For EU-issued diplomas (incl. Cyprus and Greece), Bulgaria’s official eGovernment guidance indicates **no legalisation/certification is required** for the recognition file; translation rules still apply.【https://egov.bg/wps/portal/egov/dostavchitsi+na+uslugi/ministerstva/ministerstvo+na+obrazovanieto+i+naukata/480】
- Public registers show issued decisions; monitoring can surface precedents for Cypriot and Greek universities.

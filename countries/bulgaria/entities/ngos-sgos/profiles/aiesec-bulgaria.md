# AIESEC Bulgaria

- **Focus:** Youth leadership; internships and volunteer exchanges
- **Reach:** Local committees at major Bulgarian universities (Sofia, Varna, Plovdiv, Ruse)
- **Website:** https://aiesec.bg

## Overview
AIESEC Bulgaria is the national chapter of the global youth-led organization that facilitates international internships and volunteer projects. It develops leadership skills through project delivery, cross-cultural teams, and exchanges, working closely with university student unions and corporate partners.

## Programs and reach
- **Global Volunteer:** 6–8 week volunteer projects abroad in education, social impact, and SDG-themed initiatives.
- **Global Talent / Global Teacher:** Professional internships (2–18 months) in business, tech, and education, often with partner companies.
- **Youth leadership pipeline:** Campus-based committees run recruitment, sales, and exchange operations, creating a pool of globally-minded student leaders.

## Target students
Undergraduate students and recent graduates seeking short-term volunteer or internship experiences abroad; student leaders engaged in campus chapters who influence peer decisions about international pathways.

## Partnership fit
- Promote **UNIC short programs, summer schools, and remote internships** to AIESEC returnees looking for academic progression after their exchange.
- Offer **career webinars and skill sprints** (CV, interview prep, portfolio building) co-branded with AIESEC chapters to seed awareness of UNIC degrees.
- Engage AIESEC alumni as **student ambassadors** to share mobility stories relevant to UNIC Athens/Nicosia pathways.

# America for Bulgaria Foundation (ABF)

- **Focus:** Scholarships; STEM and entrepreneurship philanthropy
- **Reach:** National programs with strong school and university partnerships
- **Website:** https://us4bg.org

## Overview
ABF is a major philanthropic foundation funding education, civic engagement, and economic development in Bulgaria. It invests in modernizing classrooms, supporting teacher training, and expanding access to STEM and entrepreneurship learning. ABF regularly co-funds scholarships and grants with universities and NGOs to scale high-impact programs.

## Programs and reach
- **STEM and tech education:** Grants for robotics labs, coding academies, and science fairs; partnerships with schools and universities to expand applied learning.
- **Entrepreneurship and leadership:** Support for accelerators, debate programs, and youth leadership camps that prepare students for global careers.
- **Scholarship partnerships:** Co-funded scholarships with institutions such as the American University in Bulgaria and targeted financial aid for high-need students in STEM/business tracks.

## Target students
High school and university students pursuing STEM, entrepreneurship, or civic leadership pathways; educators and program operators who influence student pipelines.

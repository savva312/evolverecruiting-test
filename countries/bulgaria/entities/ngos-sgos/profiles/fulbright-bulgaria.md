# Bulgarian-American Commission for Educational Exchange (Fulbright Bulgaria)

- **Focus:** Scholarships; study abroad advising (U.S.)
- **Reach:** National network via EducationUSA and Fulbright alumni
- **Website:** https://www.fulbright.bg

## Overview
The bilateral Fulbright Commission administers U.S. government-funded exchanges and operates EducationUSA Bulgaria. It provides merit-based scholarships for Bulgarian students and scholars to study, research, or teach in the United States and offers advising on U.S. admissions, testing, and financing. The organization maintains a large alumni community across disciplines that often mentors new applicants.

## Programs and reach
- **Fulbright Graduate Student Program:** Fully funded master’s/PhD study in the U.S.; typically 8–12 Bulgarian grantees annually, with strong STEM, policy, and education representation.
- **Fulbright Visiting Scholar/Teacher programs:** Short-term academic exchanges and professional development for Bulgarian faculty and educators.
- **EducationUSA Bulgaria advising:** Free advising, testing preparation workshops, and outreach to high schools/universities; organizes EducationUSA fairs and Opportunity Funds for high-need students.
- **Alumni network:** Active alumni chapters provide mentoring, mock interviews, and career panels.

## Target students
High-achieving Bulgarian undergraduates, recent graduates, and early-career professionals aiming for U.S. graduate degrees or academic exchanges; students seeking intensive advising and scholarship support.

# Human Resource Development Centre (HRDC) – Erasmus+ National Agency

- **Focus:** EU mobility funding; youth and education cooperation
- **Reach:** National authority for Erasmus+ and European Solidarity Corps
- **Website:** https://hrdc.bg

## Overview
HRDC is Bulgaria’s National Agency for Erasmus+ and the European Solidarity Corps, operating under the Ministry of Education and Science. It administers EU mobility and cooperation grants for schools, VET providers, higher education institutions, and youth organizations, and oversees recognition/credit transfer rules linked to Erasmus participation.

## Programs and reach
- **Erasmus+ KA1 mobility:** Funds student and staff exchanges; manages inter-institutional agreements and compliance with the European Charter for Higher Education.
- **Erasmus+ KA2 cooperation projects:** Supports partnerships, capacity building, and innovation pilots across education sectors.
- **European Solidarity Corps:** Coordinates volunteering and solidarity projects for youth, often in community or social impact domains.
- **National outreach:** Regular info days, compliance clinics, and guidance on credit transfer/learning agreements for Bulgarian institutions.

## Target students
University students and recent graduates seeking funded EU study, traineeships, or volunteering experiences; institutional coordinators needing reliable partner universities for mobility windows.

# Bulgarian Memory Foundation (Фондация Българска Памет)

- **Focus:** Scholarships; diaspora youth development
- **Reach:** Bulgaria plus Bulgarian communities in the Western Balkans
- **Website:** https://bgmf.eu

## Overview
Founded by Dr. Milen Vrabevski, the Bulgarian Memory Foundation promotes educational and cultural ties with Bulgarian communities in neighboring countries. It funds scholarships, organizes youth seminars on European integration, and supports STEM and innovation initiatives aimed at keeping talented students engaged with Bulgaria’s economy and universities.

## Programs and reach
- **Scholarships and stipends:** Financial aid for high-achieving students of Bulgarian origin from North Macedonia, Serbia, Albania, Moldova, and Ukraine to study in Bulgaria.
- **Youth leadership seminars:** "Together in Europe" and similar forums on EU citizenship, entrepreneurship, and innovation; often hosted in Varna, Sofia, and cross-border locations.
- **STEM/innovation projects:** Support for robotics, science fairs, and startup bootcamps in partnership with schools and tech NGOs.

## Target students
Bulgarian diaspora high school and university students seeking EU-aligned education; Bulgarian nationals interested in STEM, entrepreneurship, or policy who engage with the foundation’s seminars.

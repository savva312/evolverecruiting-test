# Roma Education Fund – Bulgaria programs

- **Focus:** Scholarships; inclusion
- **Reach:** National Roma student network with regional support programs
- **Website:** https://www.romaeducationfund.org

## Overview
The Roma Education Fund (REF) supports Roma students across Central and Eastern Europe through scholarships, tutoring, and mentoring. In Bulgaria it partners with universities and local NGOs to increase Roma participation in tertiary education and reduce drop-out rates, with an emphasis on professional degrees (health, education, social work, business, IT).

## Programs and reach
- **Roma Education Scholarship Program (RESP):** Tuition and stipend support for Roma students in accredited universities; includes academic mentoring and progress monitoring.
- **RomaVersitas / student support centers:** Study skills, language tutoring, and community-building activities delivered in cooperation with local NGOs.
- **Professional development micro-grants:** Small grants for certification exams, internships, or conferences to help Roma students secure employment.

## Target students
Roma undergraduate and graduate students, especially in professional majors; prospective Roma applicants needing guidance on admissions, financial planning, and persistence.

# Competitors

Track universities and providers competing for Bulgarian students. Summaries should cover positioning, pricing, scholarships, partnerships, and marketing tactics.

## Scope and priorities (Dec 2025)
- Focus on **institutions marketing English-taught or internationally accredited degrees** that appeal to Bulgarian students considering UNIC Nicosia or UNIC Athens.
- Prioritise players with **scholarships/aid, Erasmus-style mobility, or regional campuses** that can out-compete UNIC on convenience or price.
- Capture **program overlaps** (business/finance, computer science/data/engineering, medicine/health, and liberal arts) and any **Athens- or Sofia-based delivery** that could intercept our funnel.

## Structure
- `profiles/` – competitor snapshots with differentiation notes and observed messaging.
  - Each file should include `last_verified: YYYY-MM-DD`, target segments, overlap with UNIC/UNIC Athens offerings, commercial levers (pricing/aid), channels/partnerships, and a **Sources** list with working URLs.
  - Keep content text-first; link to `/UNIC/**` materials instead of duplicating programme descriptions.

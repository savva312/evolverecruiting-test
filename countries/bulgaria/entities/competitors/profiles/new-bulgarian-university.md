# New Bulgarian University (NBU)
- **last_verified:** 2025-12-22
- **location:** Sofia (with additional sites and centres)
- **website:** https://www.nbu.bg/
- **summary:** Private university in Sofia with a broad program portfolio and an active international-student admissions track, positioning itself as a “stay in Sofia” alternative for business/IT/social sciences applicants who might otherwise consider study abroad. [Sources](https://www.nbu.bg/en/prospective-students/admission-to-undergraduate-programs/bachelor-s-programs) · [International students hub](https://www.nbu.bg/en/prospective-students/admissions-and-academics)

## Tuition & admissions snapshot (recruiter-usable)
- **Bachelor tuition (international, indicative ranges as published):**
  - **EU/EEA applicants:** 2,500–3,150 BGN per semester (also published in EUR equivalent on the same page). [Source](https://www.nbu.bg/en/prospective-students/admission-to-undergraduate-programs/fees)
  - **Non‑EU applicants:** 10,200–13,200 BGN per year (also published in EUR equivalent on the same page). [Source](https://www.nbu.bg/en/prospective-students/admission-to-undergraduate-programs/fees)
- **Deadlines/intake:** NBU publishes rolling “key dates and deadlines” that differ for EU/EEA vs Non‑EU applicants; use the live page for the current cycle. [Source](https://www.nbu.bg/en/prospective-students/admission-to-undergraduate-programs/key-dates-and-deadlines)
- **How applicants apply:** NBU publishes step-by-step requirements and required documents for international bachelor applicants (application form, secondary education documents, ID/passport details, photos, and language certificates where relevant). [Source](https://www.nbu.bg/en/prospective-students/admission-to-undergraduate-programs/how-to-apply-bp)

## Target segments and commercial posture
- **Career-oriented applicants who want Sofia-based delivery** (and want to avoid the cost/logistics of relocating to Cyprus/Greece).
- **Program breadth shoppers:** NBU’s publicly listed bachelor portfolio enables high-intent applicants to “find a match” quickly, especially across business/IT/social sciences clusters. [Source](https://www.nbu.bg/en/prospective-students/admission-to-undergraduate-programs/bachelor-s-programs)
- **Price-sensitive families (relative to private English-medium competitors):** NBU’s published tuition ranges (EU/EEA vs Non‑EU) make it easy to frame value at the offer stage. [Source](https://www.nbu.bg/en/prospective-students/admission-to-undergraduate-programs/fees)

## Overlap with UNIC/UNIC Athens
- **Business/management and computing-adjacent degrees:** NBU’s bachelor portfolio can intercept candidates comparing UNIC Athens vs “stay in Sofia”. [Source](https://www.nbu.bg/en/prospective-students/admission-to-undergraduate-programs/bachelor-s-programs)
- **Offer-stage price anchoring:** NBU’s clear published fee ranges can be used by families as a counteroffer benchmark versus Cyprus/Greece total cost of attendance. [Source](https://www.nbu.bg/en/prospective-students/admission-to-undergraduate-programs/fees)
- **International-student enablement:** A dedicated “International students” admissions hub and process lowers friction for non-Bulgarian applicants in the same way UNIC’s international office does. [Source](https://www.nbu.bg/en/prospective-students/admissions-and-academics)

## Channels, partnerships, and tactics
- **International-student content funnel:** NBU centralizes international admissions content (program lists, fees, deadlines, how-to-apply) on a single subtree, supporting SEO and counselor sharing. [Source](https://www.nbu.bg/en/prospective-students/admissions-and-academics)
- **Program list as the top conversion asset:** The publicly navigable bachelor list is a “menu” that helps advisors/counselors direct-fit students without heavy consultation. [Source](https://www.nbu.bg/en/prospective-students/admission-to-undergraduate-programs/bachelor-s-programs)

## Risks and opportunities for UNIC
- **Risk:** Modern Sofia campus with international partnerships offers a stay-in-country alternative for business/IT students; arts infrastructure helps attract well-rounded applicants who might otherwise consider UNIC Athens.
- **Opportunity:** Promote UNIC’s **medical, nursing, and fintech/blockchain leadership**—verticals NBU lacks—and leverage Cyprus/Greece mobility plus English-first delivery for students wanting Mediterranean exposure rather than Sofia-based study.

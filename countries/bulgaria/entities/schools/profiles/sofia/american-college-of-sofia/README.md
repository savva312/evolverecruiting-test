**One-paragraph summary:**  
Founded in 1860 and regarded as the oldest American educational institution outside the United States, the American College of Sofia is a selective, English-medium high school offering an American high school diploma, Bulgarian diploma, and IB Diploma Programme for international students. Students pursue rigorous English-language coursework and typically target US, UK, and continental European universities, supported by a dedicated college counseling office. The school sits in the Dragalevtsi area of Sofia with a reputation for academic strength, debate/robotics excellence, and strong English proficiency, making it a priority feeder for UNIC and UNIC Athens.

**Top opportunities:**  
- Dedicated college counseling office open to university visits; reachable via collegecounseling@acsbg.org and +359 2 434 1008.  
- High English proficiency and international placement culture align with UNIC’s English-taught programs and scholarship hooks.  
- Established student clubs (debate, Model UN, robotics) create hooks for themed workshops and subject masterclasses.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- Very selective admissions and full event calendars; visit slots require advance coordination (6–8 weeks) and alignment with IB/Bulgarian exam windows.  
- Tuition is premium (two-tier local vs international pricing); scholarship messaging must be concrete to engage cost-sensitive families.  
- Competes with heavily-branded US/UK/EU universities; proof points on recognition and outcomes are essential.

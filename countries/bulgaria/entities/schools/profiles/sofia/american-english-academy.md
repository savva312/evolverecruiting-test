**One-paragraph summary:**  
American English Academy is a private, co-educational U.S.-style school located near Lozen on the outskirts of Sofia. It delivers a Common Core–aligned program from pre-K through Grade 12 and offers an optional dual-language track that meets Bulgarian Ministry requirements via the affiliated Abraham Lincoln School. The school holds MSA-CESS accreditation and grants an American high school diploma; academic calendar runs mid-September through end of June. Positioning should emphasize English-medium study, accreditation, and value; current affordability signals and university outcomes require confirmation.

**Top opportunities:**  
- U.S. curriculum with English-medium instruction positions students for international study without foundation requirements.  
- MSA-CESS accreditation and American High School Diploma provide quality/recognition proof points.  
- Dual Language Program engages Bulgarian families and could broaden reach for UNIC Athens messaging.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- Limited public data on university outcomes or counselor contacts; relationship building needed.  
- Campus is outside central Sofia (Lozen), which may limit on-site visit turnout.  
- Tuition/fee transparency is low online; affordability for UNIC pricing tiers is unverified.

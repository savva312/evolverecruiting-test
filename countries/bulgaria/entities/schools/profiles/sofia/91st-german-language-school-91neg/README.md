**One-paragraph summary:**  
Selective public language gymnasium founded in 1960 with a strong German focus and dual-diploma pathways (Bulgarian + German Section / DSD). Students complete intensive German (20+ hours in early years) and many obtain German-language certification enabling direct entry to German-speaking universities. Affordability is favorable (no tuition) but outbound intent skews to Germany and Austria, requiring targeted messaging and scholarship offers to redirect interest toward UNIC Nicosia/Athens programs taught in English.

**Top opportunities:**  
- Leverage EU campus positioning and affordability vs. German universities for non-medical programs.  
- Target German Section/DSD students interested in international business, CS, and health sciences where English-taught options matter.  
- Offer joint sessions with German-speaking UNIC faculty/alumni to validate quality for parents focused on German standards.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- Strong Germany/Austria pull; high-achieving students already have clear pathways.  
- Limited visibility into counselors/contacts; need verified gatekeepers.  
- English proficiency for some cohorts may lag German proficiency; admissions testing expectations must be explicit.

# Bulgaria playbook — Business, Finance, Accounting, and Marketing

Use this playbook to brief outreach teams, agents, and digital squads on how to win Bulgarian business-cluster students for both UNIC campuses. Tie every activity back to the market evidence in `../cluster.md`, [outbound mobility](../../market/outbound-mobility.md), the [premium feeder report](../../reports/2025-12-20-bulgaria-premium-high-school-feeders.md), and existing go-to-market skills.

## Channel-specific plays
### Schools
1. **Counselor immersion:** 30-minute counselor briefing + 30-minute student session. Focus on scholarship grid, recognition, internship map.
2. **Student conversion:** Capture QR leads with segment tags (Business, Marketing, Finance, Accounting) and route to admissions within 24 hours; send Bulgarian-language follow-up pack inside 48 hours.
3. **Parent loops:** Within one week of visit, run Bulgarian-language parent webinar focusing on total cost scenarios and recognition, co-hosted with an alumnus parent.

### Agents
1. **Launch package:** Provide digital toolkit (Bulgarian LP links, ACCA exemption sheet, Medicine timeline) + dedicated Slack/WhatsApp channel.
2. **SLA checkpoints:** Weekly pipeline tracker (inquiries, submitted apps, offers, deposits). Agents falling below 80% of SLA target for two consecutive weeks trigger remedial coaching.
3. **Co-branded activations:** Quarterly webinars split by cluster (Business/Finance vs. Medicine) to keep messaging compliant and segment-specific.

### Digital
1. **TOF creative:** Bulgarian Reels/TikToks featuring “Why study business 55 minutes from Sofia?” and “ACCA-ready in Cyprus with Mediterranean internships.” Use subtitles and CTA to scholarship calculator.
2. **MOF nurture:** Carousel ads with tuition vs. Western Europe comparisons, linking to Bulgarian landing pages built per `digital-playbook/landing-pages.md`.
3. **BOF remarketing:** Lead-gen forms replicating counselor call-to-actions; auto-trigger Calendly for 1:1 consults within 12 hours. Score leads using `measurement-utms.md` taxonomy.

## Conversion governance
- **Lead routing:** All business-cluster leads tagged with `market:bg` + `cluster:business` in CRM. Schools/agents get response within 24 hours; digital leads within 12 hours.
- **Scholarship integrity:** Publish scholarship tiers publicly; agents must disclose net tuition exactly as in offer. Any agent discounting outside policy leads to SLA suspension.
- **Measurement:** Weekly review of CPL/CPO vs. guardrails in the digital playbook. Track offer-to-deposit speed by channel; target ≤21 days for school/agent leads and ≤30 days for digital leads.

## Risks and mitigations
1. **Low-cost domestic alternatives (UNWE/FEBA/NBU):** Mitigate by leading with program depth, internships, and licensure recognition rather than price. Keep Bulgarian-language parent webinars ready to address “why pay €10k+ abroad?”
2. **Scholarship over-extension:** Cap Bronze tier at 10%, Silver at 20%, Gold at 30%, Platinum at 40% per city to protect budget; require VP approval for exceptions.
3. **Agent compliance:** Run quarterly secret shopper calls; revoke scholarship allocation if agents promise unpublished discounts.
4. **Data gaps:** Field-of-study dataset and employer roster still missing (see `../cluster.md`). Open follow-up tickets if dependencies block messaging.

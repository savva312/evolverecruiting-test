# Health, Pharmacy, and Allied Health competitors (Bulgaria)

Last updated: **2025-12-22**  
Sources last verified: **2025-12-22**  

Purpose: a **Bulgaria-relevant** competitive set for Nursing / Physiotherapy / Pharmacy used by recruiters and counselors. This is not meant to be exhaustive; it’s meant to match what Bulgarian families actually compare at decision time.

## Snapshot (how Bulgarians compare)
- **Default competitor = Bulgaria public medical universities** (price + “licensure feels straightforward” + family proximity).
- **Regional competitor = Greece** for Greek-speaking candidates (or those committed to Greek), especially for Pharmacy.
- **Cyprus private competitor = European University Cyprus** (similar geography + private-university sales model).
- Biggest conversion risk for UNIC/UNIC Athens: families assume “EU degree = automatic right-to-practice in Bulgaria”. Use `../../market/recognition-and-licensure.md` to keep claims compliant.

## Competitor entries (≥8)

### 1) Medical University of Sofia (Bulgaria)
- **Why it competes in Bulgaria:** domestic public option; perceived simplest Bulgaria right-to-practice; strong brand.
- **Relevant programmes:** Pharmacy; nursing/public health tracks (verify faculty/college specifics per year).
- **Admissions signal (typical):** Bulgarian/EU routes vary; international admissions published on university pages.
- **Notes vs UNIC:** price anchor + “stay in Bulgaria” advantage; UNIC must win on pathway support, student services, and mobility narratives.
- **Primary sources:**
  - Faculty of Pharmacy (official site): https://pharmfac.mu-sofia.bg/?page_id=2914&lang=en
  - Admissions (Faculty of Pharmacy): https://pharmfac.mu-sofia.bg/?page_id=4247&lang=en

## How to use this list in live counseling (practical)
1) Identify the student’s intended practice country (Bulgaria vs Greece vs Cyprus vs other EU).
2) If Bulgaria + regulated title: send `../../market/recognition-and-licensure.md` immediately and avoid “guarantee” language.
3) Compare on **clinical training structure + language reality + support**, not on “EU degree” alone.

# Bulgaria playbook — Design and Creative Media

This playbook is designed to help the Bulgaria team generate **high-intent design leads** and convert them via a **portfolio-first funnel**.

## The winning funnel (Bulgaria)
1. **Lead capture** (IG/TikTok/YouTube + school counsellors + partner academies)
2. **Portfolio review booking** (the conversion lever)
3. **Portfolio plan** (4–6 week checklist + deadlines)
4. **Application submission + fast decision**
5. **Offerholder yield** (student-work showcase + parent reassurance + financing clarity)

## Messaging angles to test (by segment)
### Art high schools / applied arts tracks (UG)
- “Portfolio review + a clear portfolio rubric (no guessing).”
- “Studio culture + critique cadence (what you build each semester).”
- “English-taught pathway in an EU campus environment.”

### NATFA/NAA near-miss candidates (UG)
- “Don’t lose a year — portfolio-based admissions pathway.”
- “Still a craft-first environment, with clearer programme structure.”

### Career switchers into UX/UI (PG / second-degree)
- “Portfolio outcomes, not promises: projects, critique, iteration.”
- “Structured pathway from fundamentals → specialisation.”

## Channel actions (do these, not “general marketing”)

### 1) Schools & counsellors (high-intent UG pipeline)
**Who to target**
- Specialised art high schools and applied arts tracks in **Sofia + major cities** (start with 10–15, then expand).
- Career counsellors in general gymnasiums with strong English + arts clubs.

**Offer**
- Free “**Portfolio Review Day**” (on-site or online): 30-minute 1:1 slots + a short group session (“What a competitive portfolio looks like”).
- Teacher/counsellor kit: one-page programme summary + portfolio rubric + timeline + contacts.

**Cadence**
- Sep–Nov: awareness + first portfolio reviews (Grade 11–12)
- Jan–Mar: second wave + application push
- Apr–Jun: offerholder/yield + “last-mile” portfolio help

**Operational notes**
- CRM tags: `BG-DESIGN-ARTHS`, `BG-DESIGN-NATFA`, `BG-DESIGN-CAREERSWITCH`, `BG-DESIGN-EU-BOUND`.
- Always offer a “next appointment” at the end of the review: (a) portfolio plan call, (b) admissions checklist call.

### 2) Portfolio prep centres & academies (feeder + co-marketing)
These organisations already aggregate the exact audience we need.

**Priority partner types**
- Creative short-course providers (UX/UI, branding, motion).
- Portfolio/game art academies (3D, concept art, animation).

**Named examples to start conversations**
- SoftUni Creative: https://creative.softuni.bg/
- ARC Academy (Sofia): https://arc.academy/

**Partnership offer (value exchange)**
- UNIC provides: portfolio rubric, guest critique panels, “how to apply” sessions, and priority review slots.
- Partner provides: access to cohorts + newsletter/social amplification + co-hosted events.

**Guardrail**
- Do not promise credit transfer. If asked, route to admissions with a “case-by-case” framing.

### 3) Fairs & events (lead volume + parent trust)
**Education fairs (high leverage for parents)**
- World Education Fair (Integral): https://www.integral.bg/events/world-education-fair

**On-site conversion tactic**
- Don’t just “collect leads”: run a mini-activation booth:
  - “Portfolio quick scan” (5 minutes)
  - QR to book full portfolio review
  - 3 example portfolios (permissioned) + rubric

### 4) Digital (always-on) — what to run
**Creative format rules**
- 70% student work / process / critique clips (UGC-style)
- 20% “portfolio education” (what to include, common mistakes, timelines)
- 10% “campus + studios” (only if you have strong visuals)

**Paid social structure**
- TOF: interests (design, illustration, UX, Adobe tools) + lookalikes from past applicants (if available).
- MOF: retarget video viewers + site visitors with “Book a portfolio review”.
- BOF: retarget portfolio-review attendees with “application checklist + deadline reminders”.

**Landing page / CTA**
- One primary CTA for this cluster: **Book a portfolio review**.
- Secondary CTA: download portfolio checklist (email capture).

## Offerholder/yield plays (Bulgaria-specific)
- “Student work showcase night” (online) every 3–4 weeks during peak offer months.
- Parent reassurance call script: tuition/payment clarity, student support, housing basics (no visa guarantees).
- “Peer proof”: connect offers to current students/alumni for short Q&A sessions (moderated; no outcome promises).

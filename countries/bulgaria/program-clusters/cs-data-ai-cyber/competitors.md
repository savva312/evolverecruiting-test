# CS, Data, AI, and Cyber competitors

Reference cards for the programs that keep showing up in Bulgarian CS/data/AI comparisons. Each card lists the offer, tuition/delivery, internship or employer hooks, and the perceived edge versus UNIC so recruiters can position quickly.

## Bulgaria

### Sofia University “St. Kliment Ohridski” (Sofia)
- **Offer:** Faculty of Mathematics & Informatics covers Computer Science, Software Engineering, Data Science, Cybersecurity and applied maths in Bulgarian or English, four-year degrees, and the university regularly partners with Sofia-based employers through shared labs and industry events.  
- **Tuition & delivery:** $2,800 per year for English-taught CS/Software Engineering; low cost plus state subsidies make it a baseline comparison for price-sensitive families (Standyou program brief, 2025).  
- **Employer hooks:** Participates in AmCham Bulgaria Hackathon 2025 alongside IBM, Paysafe, Sensata and TU-Sofia, giving students case-study exposure and mentor networks without leaving Sofia (AmCham Bulgaria release, 2025).  
- **Edge vs. UNIC:** Brand familiarity + ultra-low tuition. **UNIC counter:** highlight dual-campus (Nicosia + Athens) mobility, English-instruction from day one, modern labs, and the ability to plug into HHG hospitals or Ellinikon smart-city datasets for capstones.

### Technical University of Sofia (Sofia & Plovdiv)
- **Offer:** English-language Computer and Software Engineering, Cybersecurity, Information Technology in Industry; heavy emphasis on hardware+software stacks.  
- **Tuition & delivery:** €4,000/year for Computer Science & Engineering (non-EU); Bulgarian/EU citizens pay closer to €2,000 (UniversityInBulgaria.com profile, 2025).  
- **Employer hooks:** Hosts the AmCham Hackathon alongside TU professors and companies like IBM, Kamenitza, Paysafe and Sensata, ensuring real business cases and mentorship pipelines (AmCham Bulgaria release, 2025).  
- **Edge vs. UNIC:** Deep STEM faculty bench and direct pipeline into Bulgarian manufacturing and telecom firms. **UNIC counter:** newer AI/data curriculum, access to Greece’s Ellinikon living-lab infrastructure, and pan-EU networking vs. largely domestic placements.

### SoftUni (Software University & SoftUni BUDITEL High School)
- **Offer:** Non-formal education ecosystem with Software Engineering, AI, Digital Marketing, Creative, Kids, and high-school tracks; 500k+ cumulative learners, 155k paying students, 145+ partner companies (SoftUni 12-year retrospective, 2024).  
- **Delivery & pricing:** Modular bootcamps (online/on-site) with free introductory courses plus pay-per-module specialisations; omnichannel community of meetups, SoftUni Digital Fest, and SoftUniada competitions for middle and high-school talent (SoftUni impact report, 2024; SoftUniada Teens 2024 recap).  
- **Employer hooks:** Massive DEV community ties, partner companies embedded in coursework, SoftUniada Teens attracting ~150 budding coders/designers/entrepreneurs from 5th–7th grade nationwide—meaning SoftUni owns the top of the funnel long before university (SoftUniada Teens 2024 recap).  
- **Edge vs. UNIC:** Speed and affordability for up/reskilling; strong emotional pull with parents seeing coding camps as the safest pathway. **UNIC counter:** stack SoftUni certificates for credit recognition, pitch blended pathways (SoftUni short course + UNIC degree) and emphasise accredited EU degrees, research labs, and scholarships.

## Regional (Greece)

### The American College of Greece (Deree, Athens)
- **Offer:** US-accredited BSc in Computer Science, Data Science minors, business analytics and entrepreneurship tracks on an Athens campus with liberal-arts pedagogy.  
- **Tuition & aid:** €384 per credit for 15+ credit loads (≈€5,760/semester) plus lab and international-student fees; grants provide 5–35% tuition reduction for need-based applicants filing taxes in Greece (American College of Greece tuition & financial aid pages, 2025).  
- **Employer hooks:** Established internship office with pipelines into Attica-based multinationals, ALBA Graduate Business School pathways, and long-running work-study programs.  
- **Edge vs. UNIC:** Legacy brand for Greek-American education, robust merit/need-based aid, easy commuting for Athens families. **UNIC counter:** emphasise UNIC Athens’ brand-new 12,500 m² campus at The Ellinikon with 43 specialised labs, 19 OSCE rooms, and HHG hospital access for interdisciplinary CS/health or AI-in-clinics projects; highlight blockchain/AI research leadership (UNIC Athens campus reveal, 2024; HHG partnership brief, 2024; UNIC blockchain programme releases, 2024).  

## Western EU

### Technical University of Munich (Germany)
- **Offer:** Global top-25 technical university with elite Computer Science, Informatics, AI, Robotics, and Entrepreneurship centers; co-op style projects with BMW, Siemens, SAP, etc.  
- **Tuition & fees:** From Winter 2024/25, non-EU students pay €2,000–3,000 per semester for bachelor’s CS programs and €4,000–6,000 per semester for master’s, on top of semester contributions—ending the “free Germany” narrative for many Bulgarian families (TUM tuition announcement, Apr 2024).  
- **Employer hooks:** Bavaria’s Industry 4.0 ecosystem plus UnternehmerTUM incubator make it a magnet for start-up minded engineers.  
- **Edge vs. UNIC:** Brand prestige + Germany’s labour market. **UNIC counter:** zero visa friction for EU citizens, faster admission timelines, smaller cohorts, English-medium teaching from year one, and guaranteed access to cutting-edge blockchain/AI labs without relocating to Munich.

### University of Twente – Technical Computer Science (Enschede, Netherlands)
- **Offer:** English-taught BSc Technical Computer Science built around the Twente Education Model (12 project-based modules covering software, hardware, networks, human-computer interaction, and entrepreneurship); 40% international cohort (University of Twente programme overview, 2025).  
- **Tuition & cost:** Statutory fee for EU/EEA students set at €2,601 in 2025/26 (vs. €13,000 institutional fee for non-EU); Dutch cost-of-living and housing waitlists remain major hurdles (University of Twente tuition page, 2025).  
- **Employer hooks:** High placement into Dutch deep-tech firms, mandatory team projects with real companies, and double-degree options with Applied Mathematics.  
- **Edge vs. UNIC:** Perceived as the gold standard for project-based engineering plus access to NL internships. **UNIC counter:** comparable project pedagogy via Ellinikon living-lab data, warmer climate + lower living costs, and quicker onboarding through merit+need scholarships for blockchain/data programs.

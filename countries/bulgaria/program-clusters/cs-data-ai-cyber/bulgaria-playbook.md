# Bulgaria playbook — CS, Data, AI, and Cyber

## Messaging pillars (2025–2026)
1. **Dual-campus innovation loop (Athens + Nicosia):** Position UNIC Athens’ new 12,500 m² Ellinikon campus (43 specialised labs, 19 OSCE rooms, HHG clinical partnership) as the closest “living lab” for Bulgarian students, while UNIC Nicosia brings 15+ years of blockchain, AI, and cybersecurity leadership (world’s first MSc in Blockchain & Digital Currency, €300k+ annual scholarships, EU Blockchain Observatory role). Sources: UNIC Athens campus reveal (2024), HHG partnership brief (2024), UNIC blockchain programme releases (2024).  
2. **Career velocity in Bulgaria’s fastest-growing export sector:** Tie every outreach to BASSCOM’s 11.5% software revenue growth (BGN 11 b forecast) and the DEV.BG platform’s 30k professionals / 1,200 employers / 200k applications, proving that UNIC curricula map directly to the top hiring hubs. Sources: BASSCOM Barometer 2024 (BTA) and DEV.BG All-in-One 2025 fact sheet.  
3. **Inclusive affordability + women-in-tech focus:** Bulgaria already leads the EU with 27% female ICT specialists, yet only 35.5% of citizens have basic digital skills. Wrap UNIC scholarships, flexible payment plans, and women-in-blockchain narratives around that delta to win counselors pushing for gender balance. Sources: Eurostat Women in ICT release (2024), Digital Skills & Jobs Coalition snapshot (2024), UNIC scholarship announcements (2024).

## Channel plan

### 1. DEV.BG job board + All-in-One summit (Sofia, Oct each year)
- **Why now:** Largest aggregation of Bulgarian tech talent (30k pros, 1.2k employers, 1k events) and 2025 summit already marketing AI + cyber keynote slots (DEV.BG platform stats; DEV.BG All-in-One 2025 event site).  
- **Play:** Sponsor AI/cyber stage, run live “Athens living-lab” demos, push guaranteed interview pipelines with UNIC partners.  
- **Assets & SLA:** Bulgarian-language landing page + case study, 72-hour response SLA for leads captured onsite, follow-up dev community newsletter.

### 2. SoftUni ecosystem (SoftUni Digital Fest, SoftUniada Teens, SoftUni BUDITEL)
- **Why now:** 500k+ learners and 145 partner companies already trust SoftUni; SoftUniada Teens draws 150 middle-school coders/designers, making it the earliest STEM feeder nationwide (SoftUni 12-year retrospective, 2024; SoftUniada Teens 2024 recap).  
- **Play:** Co-create “SoftUni x UNIC” micro-credential that articulates into Nicosia/Athens credits, award two UNIC scholarships at SoftUniada, and film Bulgarian-language lab tours for SoftUni Digital Fest.  
- **Assets & SLA:** Credit-recognition one-pager, signed articulation agreement draft, scholarship guidelines before September recruitment cycle.

### 3. Integral World Education Fair + Study Abroad Insight Days
- **Why now:** Integral’s report shows 27% of Bulgarian outbound students picking the Netherlands, 12% Austria, 11% Spain—families go here to compare EU CS options (Integral Education “Study Abroad 2024” report).  
- **Play:** Run comparative workshops “Athens vs. Netherlands vs. Germany: total cost for CS/AI” using UNIC Athens’ housing data and highlight Cyprus climate + safety.  
- **Assets & SLA:** Tuition/Cost calculator, Bulgarian parent FAQ, dedicated counselor WhatsApp line with <24h turnaround.

### 4. Tech Career Days (Sofia & Plovdiv, Spring/Fall)
- **Why now:** 4,000+ participants, 60+ companies, and live technical interviews focus on emerging skills such as software, QA, AI, cyber (Tech Career Days organiser data, 2025).  
- **Play:** Sponsor cyber range challenge staffed by UNIC Athens faculty, invite top performers to Athens residency week, collect employer intent for co-branded scholarships.  
- **Assets & SLA:** Cyber challenge brief, faculty travel schedule, CRM tagging for employer partners (response SLA 3 business days).

### 5. ETHSofia / Web3 festivals + AmCham Hackathon
- **Why now:** ETHSofia positions Sofia as a European Web3 node every September; AmCham Hackathon already convenes IBM, Paysafe, Sensata, TU-Sofia, SU—perfect venues to showcase UNIC’s blockchain leadership (ETHSofia 2025 site; AmCham Bulgaria Hackathon 2025 release).  
- **Play:** Offer “UNIC Cyprus Blockchain Residency” prize, invite HHG partners to judge AI-in-health pitches, and schedule Athens campus tours immediately after the events.  
- **Assets & SLA:** Prize T&Cs, travel stipends, event-to-offer nurture sequence with 48-hour handoff from events team to admissions.

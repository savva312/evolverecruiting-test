# Visit templates

Standardize in-school sessions for Nigeria visits:
- 30–45 minute format: intro, program highlights, recognition/licensure, scholarships, Q&A.
- Bring QR codes/short links for lead capture; test them before arrival.
- Collect counselor feedback and student questions; log in the tracker within 24 hours.
- Travel planning: cluster multiple schools per city trip and confirm AV needs ahead of time.

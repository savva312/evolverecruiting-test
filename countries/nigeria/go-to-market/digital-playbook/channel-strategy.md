# Digital channel strategy

High-level guardrails for Nigeria digital campaigns. Replace placeholders with ticket-specific numbers before launch.

## Priorities for Nigeria + diaspora
- Meta and TikTok for prospecting and storytelling (English + key Nigerian languages where appropriate).
- Google Search for high-intent brand/program terms and "study in Cyprus/Greece" queries.
- YouTube for awareness and retargeting with campus/student content.
- LinkedIn for parents, counselors, and postgraduate audiences.

## Compliance, consent, and data handling
- Include privacy/consent language on all forms and landing pages.
- Cap retargeting windows for minors; refresh suppression lists regularly.
- Store UTMs and source parameters in CRM; log consent timestamp and channel.

## Cadence and optimization
- Weekly: refresh creative, audit CPC/CPL against guardrails, pause outliers.
- Monthly: reallocate budget toward channels with strongest qualified lead rate; verify pixel/GA4 events.

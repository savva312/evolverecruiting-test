# Exams and calendar

This page summarizes the secondary-exam landscape and seasonal calendar inflection points that drive Nigerian student recruiting for UNIC and UNIC Athens. Dates are month-level windows; exact timetables should be re-confirmed each cycle.

## Core exams (what matters for offers and scholarship timing)

| Exam | Admin body | Who takes it / eligibility | Usual sittings & results | Relevance to UNIC/UNIC Athens | Source |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| WAEC WASSCE (school candidates) | WAEC | SS3 students in approved schools | Papers run May–June; results typically release ~August | Primary school-leaver credential; grades drive direct admissions and scholarship bands | [WAEC/Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_African_Senior_School_Certificate_Examination) |
| WAEC WASSCE GCE (private) | WAEC | Out-of-school/private candidates | First series: Jan/Feb (results ~March); Second series: Oct/Nov (results ~Dec) | Re-sit path for students improving grades after SS3 | [WAEC/Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_African_Senior_School_Certificate_Examination) |
| NECO SSCE (internal) | NECO | SS3 students (public + private) | Written June–July; results ~Sept (45–60 days after last paper) | Widely accepted Nigerian credential; often used alongside/ instead of WAEC | NECO timetables (recurring June–July window; re-verify each year) |
| NECO SSCE (external/GCE) | NECO | Private candidates | Papers Nov–Dec; results Jan/Feb | Second-chance upgrade route before next admissions round | NECO timetables (recurring Nov–Dec window; re-verify each year) |
| JAMB UTME | Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board | Required for Nigerian university admission; useful signal for academic strength | Registration mid-Jan–late Feb; mock in March; main CBT exam mid/late April; results released within 48h; CAPS admissions Jun–Dec | UTME score is not mandatory for UNIC but strong scores help counselor credibility; timing overlaps with WAEC/NECO results | [JAMB/Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joint_Admissions_and_Matriculation_Board) |

## Month-by-month calendar (current & next cycle guide)

- **January–February**
  - WAEC GCE first series papers (Jan/Feb) with results ~March.
  - JAMB UTME registration mid-Jan–late Feb; counseling demand is high.
  - SS3 students resume 2nd term (early Jan) after Dec break; visitation feasible late Jan onward.
- **March**
  - JAMB mock UTME (early March).
  - WAEC GCE first-series results (March) → upgrade transcripts for applicants.
  - Schools in 2nd-term teaching; brief mid-term breaks vary by state.
- **April**
  - JAMB UTME main exam window (mid/late April).
  - Some private schools run WAEC practicals/orals toward late April.
  - Easter/Ramadan/Eid breaks can compress outreach weeks; keep digital nurturing live.
- **May**
  - WAEC WASSCE (school) papers in full swing (May–June).
  - 3rd term starts for many states after May Day; travel to schools limited by exam timetables.
- **June**
  - WAEC continues; NECO SSCE internal begins (June–July).
  - University post-UTME adverts start appearing; parents seek backup options.
- **July**
  - NECO SSCE internal ongoing; some WAEC papers finish early July in spillover schedules.
  - Schools close late July for long break; prime window for fairs/parent events.
- **August**
  - WAEC May/June results typically released (~August).
  - Long vacation continues; host digital webinars and agent trainings.
  - CAPS (JAMB admissions) begins for federal/state universities—students evaluate local vs. abroad.
- **September**
  - NECO internal results often released (~Sept); schools resume 1st term (early Sept).
  - Candidates assemble complete result sets; good month for offer pushes before post-UTME lists.
- **October**
  - Post-UTME screenings peak (Oct); WAEC GCE second-series registration closes and exams start.
  - Public holidays (Independence Day; possible Maulud) shorten school weeks; prioritize virtual touchpoints.
- **November**
  - WAEC GCE second-series papers; NECO external (Nov–Dec).
  - 1st-term exams for schools; limited counselor availability mid-late month.
- **December**
  - Results for WAEC GCE second series often out by December; schools on Christmas break from mid-Dec.
  - University merit/second lists still rolling; opportunity to convert wait-and-see students.
- **January (next year)**
  - NECO external results Jan/Feb; SS3 students resume 2nd term.
  - New UTME registration opens; fresh SS3 pipeline enters counseling.

## Timing implications for recruiting

- **Offer sequencing:** Target conditional offers before WAEC/NECO results (Mar–Apr) and firm offers after WAEC release (Aug) when transcripts update.
- **Campaign spikes:** Run high-intent digital bursts during long vacation (Aug) and just after result releases (Aug/Sept, Dec/Jan).
- **School outreach:** Avoid heavy travel during WAEC/NECO sittings (May–July); prioritize Sept–Oct and Jan–March for in-person counselor sessions.
- **Agent enablement:** Provide quick guides on result upload/verification in Aug–Sept and Dec–Jan when parents are re-evaluating options.
- **Visa/application pacing:** Encourage Fall intake submissions before UTME/Post-UTME results create local pull (aim Jul–Sept); Spring intake fits NECO external candidates awaiting Jan/Feb results.

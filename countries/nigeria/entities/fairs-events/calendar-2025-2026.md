## Execution guardrails

- Confirm exact dates and booth packages at least 3–4 months ahead; Lagos venues book fast.
- Align staffing with city strengths: Lagos = volume + media coverage; Abuja = government families + international schools; Port Harcourt = oil/gas families and STEM interest.
- Capture counselor contacts separately from student leads; route to segmented follow-up (Nicosia vs Athens value props, scholarship details, visa guidance).
- Track cost-per-lead and enrollment yield by event to refine 2026 budgets.

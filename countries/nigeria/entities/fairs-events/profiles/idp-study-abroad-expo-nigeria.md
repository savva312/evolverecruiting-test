# IDP Study Abroad Expo – Nigeria (Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt)

## Overview
Large multi-city fair organized by IDP Nigeria targeting outbound students for the UK, Australia, Canada, Ireland, and select EU destinations. Strong marketing spend and established brand; good mix of undergraduate and postgraduate prospects.

## Timing and format
- **Typical months:** February/March (spring) and September (fall), with a virtual follow-up day per season.
- **Cities:** Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt; virtual Nigeria-wide session.
- **Format:** Booth/table + short presentations; IDP manages registrations and lead scans.

## Audience and expected reach
- 5,000–7,000 total visitors per season across cities; Lagos generates the bulk of volume.
- Mix of undergrad (A-level/WAEC/IGCSE) and taught master’s prospects; families attend in Lagos/Abuja.

## Pricing (indicative)
- USD 2.5k–3.5k per city booth; virtual day ~USD 1k. Based on recent IDP Africa rate cards and peer institution quotes—confirm with local IDP team.
- Shipping and on-ground travel are additional.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** Lead with English-medium programs, affordability vs UK/Canada, and professional accreditation (ABET/EPAS/ACCA). Highlight visa approval stability.
- **UNIC Athens:** Stress proximity to Nigeria, Schengen mobility, and EU lifestyle; position as alternative to UK with lower living costs.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: book spring and fall slots early; request booth near counseling area; co-design landing pages with IDP tracking links; ship collateral to Lagos hub.
- During: run QR/scan + short interest form; offer 1:1 consult slots for high-intent leads; capture counselor details for school visits.
- Post: 24–48 hour follow-up segmented by campus and intake; invite leads to webinar + connect them with IDP counselors trained on UNIC programs.

## Risks and notes
- High booth demand—late booking may push to secondary locations.
- Lead quality varies by city; Port Harcourt skewed to oil/gas families and STEM programs.
- Ensure messaging aligns with IDP counseling scripts to avoid mis-selling.

# Nigeria Immigration Service (NIS)

Mandate: Issues Nigerian e-passports, manages visa/residence permits for foreign nationals, and controls entry/exit at borders.

Relevance to UNIC/UNIC Athens: Nigerian students need valid e-passports for visa applications and travel; NIS processes renewals, data corrections, and re-issuance. Understanding appointment availability and required validity (6+ months) helps de-risk late-stage visa refusals.

Key processes and timelines:
- Passport applications and renewals are initiated online via the NIS passport portal (`https://passport.immigration.gov.ng/`), where applicants complete forms, pay fees, and book biometric capture appointments.
- Contact channels published by NIS include service/complaints (nis.servicom@nigeriaimmigration.gov.ng) and visa enquiries (oa@nigeriaimmigration.gov.ng; cis-evisa@immigration.gov.ng).
- Processing timelines vary by passport office and are not formally published; schedule capture slots as early as possible and plan several weeks of buffer before visa submission (assumption based on typical centre queues).

# Government and regulators

Summaries of Nigerian agencies that influence outbound study, visas, and degree recognition. Note mandate, contact channels, and relevance to UNIC/UNIC Athens.

Coverage (last_checked: 2025-12-20):
- National Universities Commission — university licensing and programme accreditation; use accreditation results to validate Nigerian degrees.
- Federal Ministry of Foreign Affairs — legalisation of public documents for use abroad via online portal + appointments.
- Nigeria Immigration Service — e-passport issuance and travel document control; key for visa readiness timelines.

Open gaps to add next:
- Federal Ministry of Education (certificate authentication flows and scholarship secretariat).
- WAEC/NECO certificate verification (secondary exam authentication).
- Destination embassy/consular queues relevant to Cyprus/Greece student visas.

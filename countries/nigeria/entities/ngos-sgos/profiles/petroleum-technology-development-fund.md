# Petroleum Technology Development Fund (PTDF)

- **Type:** Semi-government scholarship agency (Federal Government of Nigeria)
- **Website:** https://ptdf.gov.ng/
- **Status in pipeline:** Priority target

## Overview
PTDF is the federal funder that builds Nigeria’s oil, gas, and energy talent pipeline. Its flagship Overseas Scholarship Scheme (OSS) sends high-performing Nigerians to partner universities abroad for MSc and PhD programs in engineering, geosciences, computing, environmental science, and energy systems.

## Programs and funding mechanisms
- **Overseas Scholarship Scheme (OSS):** Annual call that sponsors tuition, living stipend, flights, health insurance, and thesis/bench fees for approved MSc/PhD programs (recent host countries include the UK, Germany, France, and Malaysia).
- **Local Scholarship Scheme (LSS):** Funds MSc/PhD within Nigeria; useful for joint/dual pathways or short courses that de-risk OSS applications.
- **Industry alignment:** Priority fields mirror Nigeria’s energy transition agenda (oil & gas value chain, renewables, environmental protection, AI/data for energy).

## Target students and reach
- Nigerian citizens with at least a 2:1 (or strong 2:2 with industry experience) and NYSC completion.
- National reach with concentrated demand from oil-producing states; strong interest from early-career engineers and geoscientists seeking international credentials.
- Application cycles typically open Q4–Q1 with interviews run via zonal centers.

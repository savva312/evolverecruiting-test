# Nigeria LNG (NLNG) Postgraduate Scholarship Scheme

- **Type:** Corporate social investment scholarship (energy major)
- **Website:** https://www.nigerialng.com/
- **Status in pipeline:** Priority

## Overview
Nigeria LNG Limited runs a competitive postgraduate scholarship that sends Nigerian students to the UK for master’s study in energy-aligned fields. The scheme is part of NLNG’s education CSR portfolio and targets high-achieving candidates who can contribute to Nigeria’s gas and power sectors.

## Programs and funding mechanisms
- **UK-taught master’s scholarship:** Typically awards ~15 places annually, covering tuition, living stipend, return flights, and support for visa/health insurance. Historic host universities include top UK engineering schools and energy-focused programs.
- **Undergraduate/local scholarships:** NLNG also funds secondary/tertiary scholarships in Nigeria—useful for early pipeline visibility, though the outbound postgraduate track is the primary mobility lever.

## Target students and reach
- Nigerian citizens with a minimum 2:1, strong GRE/IELTS where required, and demonstrated leadership/community impact.
- National reach, with notable demand from gas-producing states (Rivers, Bayelsa, Delta) and engineering faculties across Nigeria.
- Application windows are usually announced mid-year with shortlisting tests/interviews.

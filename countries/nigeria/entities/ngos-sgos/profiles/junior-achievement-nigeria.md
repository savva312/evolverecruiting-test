# Junior Achievement Nigeria (JAN)

- **Type:** Non-profit youth development and career readiness NGO (member of JA Worldwide)
- **Website:** https://ja-nigeria.org/
- **Status in pipeline:** Prospect

## Overview
JAN delivers entrepreneurship, financial literacy, and work-readiness programs for secondary and tertiary students across Nigeria. Through experiential learning and leadership camps, it builds employability skills and exposes students to global education pathways.

## Programs and funding mechanisms
- **Company Program & JA Career Success:** Hands-on entrepreneurship and employability curriculum for secondary school students nationwide.
- **LEAD Camp for Girls:** Annual leadership program targeting high-achieving teenage girls, co-run with corporate sponsors.
- **Venture in Management Program (ViMP):** One-week mini-MBA for university students/graduates delivered with Lagos Business School and corporate partners.
- **Innovation challenges & incubators:** Themed competitions (financial inclusion, climate, digital skills) sponsored by industry partners.

## Target students and reach
- Secondary school students in major hubs (Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Kano) plus an expanding digital footprint to other states.
- University finalists and recent graduates for ViMP and innovation challenges, with strong alumni networks in business and tech.

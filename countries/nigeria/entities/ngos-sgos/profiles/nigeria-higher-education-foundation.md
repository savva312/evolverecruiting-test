# Nigeria Higher Education Foundation (NHEF) Scholars Program

- **Type:** Non-profit talent pipeline and career readiness foundation
- **Website:** https://www.thenhef.org/
- **Status in pipeline:** Prospect

## Overview
NHEF is a U.S.-registered non-profit that partners with leading Nigerian universities to provide intensive career readiness, internships, and graduate study preparation for high-achieving students. Its Scholars Program builds leadership capacity and connects students to global opportunities across finance, consulting, technology, and social impact.

## Programs and funding mechanisms
- **NHEF Scholars Program:** Competitive cohort model that delivers training bootcamps, mentorship, and internship placements with corporate partners. Includes support for standardized tests and graduate school readiness.
- **Campus Ambassadors & Career Fairs:** Student leaders and recruitment events on partner campuses (e.g., University of Lagos, University of Ibadan, University of Nigeria Nsukka, Ahmadu Bello University, University of Benin, University of Port Harcourt, Bayero University Kano).
- **Scholarship/Graduate funds:** Occasional scholarships and seed funding for standout Scholars pursuing further study or ventures.

## Target students and reach
- Penultimate and final-year undergraduates at partner institutions with GPAs in the top decile and strong leadership track records.
- Presence across southern and northern hubs via campus partners; alumni often pursue graduate study abroad after internships.

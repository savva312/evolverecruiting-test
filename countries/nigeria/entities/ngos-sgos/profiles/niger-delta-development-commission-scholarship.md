# Niger Delta Development Commission (NDDC) Foreign Postgraduate Scholarship

- **Type:** Regional development commission scholarship program (SGO)
- **Website:** https://nddc.gov.ng/
- **Status in pipeline:** Prospect

## Overview
The NDDC Foreign Postgraduate Scholarship funds master’s and PhD study abroad for indigenes of the nine Niger Delta states, aiming to build technical capacity for regional infrastructure, energy, environmental remediation, and public health.

## Programs and funding mechanisms
- **Foreign Postgraduate Scholarship:** Covers tuition, living allowance, flights, and insurance for approved MSc/PhD programs at universities outside Nigeria. Disciplines include engineering (civil, mechanical, petroleum), geosciences, environmental science, ICT, and select health sciences.
- **Community development lens:** Scholars are expected to return to serve in projects that advance the Niger Delta (pipeline integrity, remediation, maritime logistics, climate resilience).

## Target students and reach
- Applicants must hail from NDDC states (Abia, Akwa Ibom, Bayelsa, Cross River, Delta, Edo, Imo, Ondo, Rivers) with minimum 2:1 or relevant professional experience.
- Recruitment often peaks in Port Harcourt and state capitals with active civil society/parent forums that influence uptake.
- Annual intakes fluctuate with budget approvals; selection includes computer-based tests and interviews.

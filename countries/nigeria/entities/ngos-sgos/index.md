# Nigeria NGO/SGO landscape — scholarships and career readiness

This index highlights non-government and semi-government organizations that influence Nigerian students’ access to scholarships, international study, and employability. Use it to prioritize outreach and link to full profiles.

## Quick priorities
- **Immediate pitch:** PTDF and NLNG postgraduate scholarships—both fund energy/engineering talent and seek cost-effective placements abroad.
- **Regional depth:** NDDC scholarship targets Niger Delta indigenes; align with environmental, maritime, and public health programs.
- **Career funnels:** NHEF and JAN provide employability pipelines and campus/event access for high-achieving undergraduates.

## How to use
1. Start with **PTDF** and **NLNG** pitches; map UNIC programs and prepare co-branded webinars before their next call windows.
2. Engage **NDDC** through state liaison offices to secure visibility before application tests/interviews.
3. Leverage **NHEF** bootcamps and **JAN** events to run graduate-study and scholarship readiness sessions that seed future cohorts.
4. Keep the CSV (`../../data/entities/ngos-sgos.csv`) in sync with status changes and add new organizations as research expands.

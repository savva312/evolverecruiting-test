**One-paragraph summary:**  
AISL is a long-standing (est. 1964) American international day school on Victoria Island serving preschool through Grade 12, with Grades 11–12 offering the IB Diploma alongside the U.S. high school diploma. Hard-currency tuition (~USD 28k–32k reported for secondary) places families well above the UNIC affordability threshold. The school markets competitive college counseling that places students into selective universities worldwide, making it a prime Tier1 feeder for UNIC Nicosia and UNIC Athens.

**Top opportunities:**  
- Hard-currency USD tuition signals strong ability to pay for UNIC programs.  
- Dual pathway (American diploma + IB DP) positions students for Medicine, Business, and CS/Data/AI.  
- Dedicated international school college counselor highlighted on the high school page enables structured outreach.  

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- Published tuition sheet not public; must verify current amounts and mandatory charges directly.  
- Day-only model; no boarding, so travel timing and parent engagement need careful planning.  
- Security/access on Victoria Island likely requires pre-scheduled visits and ID; confirm visitor protocol.

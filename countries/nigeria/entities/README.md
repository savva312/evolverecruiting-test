# Nigeria entities directory

Centralize profiles for organizations that influence Nigerian student recruitment for UNIC and UNIC Athens. Each subfolder holds brief summaries, contact notes, and sourcing details.

- `schools/` – high schools and higher-ed feeders with counselor relationships and visit history.
- `agents/` – education agents and counseling firms active in Nigeria, including performance and compliance notes.
- `government-regulators/` – ministries and agencies touching outbound study, visas, and recognition.
- `ngos-sgos/` – non-government or semi-government organizations supporting mobility or scholarships.
- `fairs-events/` – recruitment fairs, school tours, and local events worth attending.
- `competitors/` – other universities and providers recruiting in Nigeria.

Add short, sourced entries and link to relevant go-to-market tactics when available.

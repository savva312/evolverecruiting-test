# Nile University of Nigeria (NILE)

- **Location:** Abuja, Nigeria
- **Website:** https://www.nileuniversity.edu.ng/
- **Ownership/network:** Part of the Honoris United Universities network after acquisition by Actis (2020). ([Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nile_University_of_Nigeria))
- **Last verified:** 2025-12-20
- **Sources:** Guardian scholarship coverage; NILE program list (Engineering, Law, Business, Medicine) on Wikipedia. ([Guardian scholarships](https://guardian.ng/features/nile-university-of-nigeria-promotes-academic-and-sporting-excellence-among-students-with-attractive-scholarships/); [Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nile_University_of_Nigeria))

## Positioning
- Private university marketed as premium, with 8 faculties spanning engineering, business, law, and health sciences, enabling one-stop progression from undergraduate to postgraduate study. ([Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nile_University_of_Nigeria))
- Membership in the Honoris network signals regional quality benchmarks and cross-border articulation—an appeal point for families seeking recognizable brands without leaving Nigeria. ([Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nile_University_of_Nigeria))

## Program overlaps with UNIC/UNIC Athens
- **Medicine and Surgery (MBBS)** competes directly with UNIC’s health sciences pipeline for Nigerian applicants. ([Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nile_University_of_Nigeria#College_of_Health_Sciences))
- **Engineering (Computer, Mechanical, Civil, Petroleum, Mechatronics)** overlaps UNIC’s STEM and computing draws. ([Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nile_University_of_Nigeria#Faculty_of_Engineering))
- **Business and MBA tracks** mirror UNIC business school offerings and scholarship-driven messaging. ([Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nile_University_of_Nigeria#Faculty_of_Management_Sciences))
- **Law (LLB–PhD)** offers a domestic alternative to UNIC’s legal studies, reducing outbound demand. ([Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nile_University_of_Nigeria#Faculty_of_Law))

## Pricing and scholarships
- Runs merit and sports scholarships ranging from **10%–100%** based on WAEC/JAMB scores and athletic performance—these discounts can undercut UNIC tuition even without price transparency. ([Guardian](https://guardian.ng/features/nile-university-of-nigeria-promotes-academic-and-sporting-excellence-among-students-with-attractive-scholarships/))

## Relevance to Nigerian students
- Abuja location plus Honoris branding reassure parents on safety and quality while keeping students close to home.
- Scholarship depth enables middle-class families to keep costs below typical outbound study budgets, especially for medicine and engineering.

# National Open University of Nigeria (NOUN)

- **Location:** Multiple study centres nationwide; HQ in Abuja.
- **Website:** https://noun.edu.ng/
- **Model:** Open and distance learning with course materials released after tuition payment; fully accredited by the National Universities Commission. ([Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Open_University_of_Nigeria))
- **Last verified:** 2025-12-20
- **Sources:** NOUN overview and tuition/payment model; scholarship examples awarded to high-visibility students. ([Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Open_University_of_Nigeria))

## Positioning
- Public, distributed delivery that lowers accommodation/travel costs and lets working adults study without leaving employment. ([Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Open_University_of_Nigeria))
- National accreditation and exam centres reassure parents about credential recognition for professional licensure.

## Program overlaps with UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Offers **law**, **business/management**, **health sciences**, and **education** programs through distance mode—direct overlaps with UNIC’s online and on-campus offerings.
- Flexible pacing plus rolling intake windows compete with UNIC’s online degrees for adult upskilling segments.

## Pricing and scholarships
- Tuition is pay-per-course; students receive materials only after fee payment, keeping upfront costs modular for price-sensitive households. ([Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Open_University_of_Nigeria))
- Scholarships have been used as marketing (e.g., awards to Nollywood ambassadors), signalling occasional fee relief for high-visibility or high-performing candidates. ([Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Open_University_of_Nigeria#cite_note-51))

## Relevance to Nigerian students
- Enables regional students to stay local while earning accredited degrees, reducing the perceived need for outbound study if affordability is the main constraint.
- Weak on campus experience and international exposure, creating a gap UNIC can exploit with mobility, practicum, and exchange messaging.

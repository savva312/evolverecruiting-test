# Competitors

Track universities and providers competing for Nigerian students. Summaries should cover positioning, pricing cues, recognition, and why Nigerian students choose them. Prioritize:

- **Health/medicine, business, and engineering** programs that overlap with UNIC or UNIC Athens.
- **Online or hybrid providers** offering low-cost alternatives that divert digitally savvy students.
- **Scholarship-heavy players** whose discounts reshape affordability perceptions.

Current profiles:
- [Nile University of Nigeria](profiles/nile-university-of-nigeria.md) — private Abuja campus with engineering, law, business, and MBBS programs plus aggressive merit/sports scholarships (last verified: 2025-12-20).
- [National Open University of Nigeria](profiles/national-open-university-of-nigeria.md) — open and distance model with pay-per-course tuition and national accreditation; notable scholarships to boost visibility (last verified: 2025-12-20).
- [University of the People](profiles/university-of-the-people.md) — global tuition-free online degrees with small assessment fees that appeal to cost-sensitive Nigerians (last verified: 2025-12-20).

# Nigeria competitor capture — 2025-12-20

## Summary
- Added three priority competitor profiles (Nile University of Nigeria, National Open University of Nigeria, University of the People) covering onshore private premium, national distance learning, and global tuition-free online options.
- Documented scholarship/pricing hooks that directly challenge UNIC and UNIC Athens on affordability and convenience.

## Highlights
- **Nile University of Nigeria:** Multi-faculty private university in Abuja with Honoris backing; offers MBBS, engineering, business, and law plus **10%–100% scholarships** tied to WAEC/JAMB and athletics ([profile](../entities/competitors/profiles/nile-university-of-nigeria.md)).
- **National Open University of Nigeria:** Open and distance model with modular pay-per-course tuition and national accreditation; uses scholarships for visibility and keeps students in-country ([profile](../entities/competitors/profiles/national-open-university-of-nigeria.md)).
- **University of the People:** Tuition-free, online-only degrees with low assessment fees and scholarships; undercuts total cost for business, CS, health, and education pathways ([profile](../entities/competitors/profiles/university-of-the-people.md)).

## Recommendations / next steps

- Track Nigerian enrollment or recruitment partners for University of the People to size threat to UNIC online programs.
- Use Honoris network and NOUN licensure recognition as proof points in UNIC messaging where UNIC has EU mobility/recognition advantages.

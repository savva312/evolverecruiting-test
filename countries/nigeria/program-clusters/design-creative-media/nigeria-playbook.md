# Nigeria playbook — Design and Creative Media

## Messaging angles to test
- Career and employability outcomes tied to this cluster.
- Scholarship/value story for Nigerian students.
- Campus experience and safety for Nigerians in Cyprus/Greece.

## Channels to prioritize
- School visits and counselor briefings that align to the cluster.
- Agents with a track record in this subject area.
- Digital campaigns and webinars targeted to Nigerian audiences.

## Local partners or feeders
- Nigeria-based schools, associations, or employers that can amplify this cluster.
- Alumni or current Nigerian students who can co-host events.

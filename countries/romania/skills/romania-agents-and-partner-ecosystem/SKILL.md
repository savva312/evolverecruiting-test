# Romania agents and partner ecosystem (addendum)

Use `/skills/agents-and-partner-ecosystem/SKILL.md` as the **primary** procedure for partner mapping. Apply this addendum only for Romania-specific nuances and paths.

## How to use

- Follow the global workflow for taxonomy, CSV schema, templates, and privacy rules.
- Layer in the Romania-specific emphases below when executing tickets scoped to `countries/romania/**`.

## Romania-specific partner emphases

- **Partner types to prioritize:** generalist agencies with Bucharest/Cluj/Iasi coverage; medicine-specialist agencies that also support interview prep; fair organizers with national and regional tours; counseling hubs inside top bilingual/high-performing schools.
- **City tags:** start with `Bucharest`, `Cluj-Napoca`, `Iasi`, `Timisoara`, `Constanta`; add others when partners show evidence of coverage.
- **Program-fit tags:** default to `medicine`, `cs-data-ai`, `business`, `psychology`; add `law` or `architecture` if the partner markets those pathways.
- **Engagement cues:** capture whether partners can support both UNIC Nicosia and UNIC Athens positioning, their Romanian-language collateral needs, and preferred timelines around Baccalaureate exam periods.
- **Compliance reminders:** confirm GDPR adherence and transparency of service/commission fees; flag if partners promote rebate-style incentives.

## Recommended Romania file paths

Use these when permitted by the ticket:

- `countries/romania/market/partners/index.md`
- `countries/romania/market/partners/profiles/<partner_slug>.md`
- `/countries/romania/data/partners/romania-partners.csv`

## Quick CSV and profile notes

- Keep `partner_id` slugs consistent with `city-keyword` patterns (e.g., `bucharest-education-fair`, `cluj-premed-agency`).
- In profiles, include whether the partner covers Romanian diaspora students elsewhere in the EU and note any translation/credential evaluation support.

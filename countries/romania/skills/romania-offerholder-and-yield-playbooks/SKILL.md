# Romania addendum: offerholder & yield playbooks

Use `/skills/offerholder-and-yield-playbooks/SKILL.md` for cadences and data capture. Apply these Romania-specific notes.

## Channel and language norms
- Default to Romanian-language updates; provide English versions for international/IB schools.
- Email and phone are standard; WhatsApp is acceptable with explicit opt-in and should be logged with source/time.

## Timing anchors
- Lighten outreach during Baccalaureate exams and results weeks; resume higher-intensity cadences once scores are known.
- Highlight housing and travel planning early for Athens-bound students; for Nicosia-bound students, clarify EU ID/passport travel expectations without over-promising processing timelines.

## Compliance and documentation
- Do not store national ID numbers, passports, or financial documents in the repo. Track application IDs, contact info, and checklist status only.
- Record every touch with channel, language, and owner in the country data model; capture consent for parents added to messaging groups.

## Objection handling to localize
- Cost: keep pricing in EUR; if RON is requested, share estimates with FX date and avoid implying fixed amounts.
- Recognition/licensure: reference the Romania recognition and licensure skill (`countries/romania/skills/romania-recognition-and-licensure/`) for sourced answers before making commitments.

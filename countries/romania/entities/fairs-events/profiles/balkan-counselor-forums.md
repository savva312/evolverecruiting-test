# Balkan / CEE counselor forums and virtual fairs

## Overview
Periodic counselor-focused forums and virtual fairs covering the Balkans or Central and Eastern Europe. Organizers vary (e.g., BMI, CIS/EAC, regional counselor networks). Useful for relationship-building and digital lead capture, but volumes are smaller than major public fairs.

## Timing and format
- **Typical cadence:** 1–2 events per year touching Romania; formats include half-day virtual fairs or 1–2 day in-person forums in Bucharest or nearby hubs (Bucharest/Belgrade/Athens).
- **Format:** Counselor roundtables, short presentations, and student-facing virtual sessions.

## Audience and expected reach
- 150–400 counselors/students depending on organizer; strongest value from direct counselor meetings rather than raw student counts.

## Pricing (indicative)
- Virtual: €0–€700 depending on organizer and sponsorship.
- In-person forums: €800–€1,500 plus travel/hotel.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** Counselor-facing toolkit on English-medium accredited programs, medicine, and digital economy degrees; emphasize structured onboarding and quick decisions.
- **UNIC Athens:** Emphasize proximity for school group visits, EU campus option, and business/psychology pathways; easy travel for Romanian counselors and families.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: monitor announcements via agent/counselor networks; prepare short deck and counselor FAQ; line up digital brochures and scholarship sheet.
- During: book 1:1 meetings with top counselors; run short demo of application portal; log follow-ups per school.
- Post: send recap email within 24 hours; invite counselors to campus-specific webinar; schedule school visits during next Romania trip.

## Risks and notes
- Event quality varies widely; vet organizer attendee lists before paying.
- Student-facing segments can be low volume; prioritize counselor relationship outcomes.

# RIUF – Romanian International University Fair (Educativa)

## Overview
RIUF (by Educativa) is the largest study-abroad fair in Romania with spring and fall editions across multiple cities. Strong mix of high-school seniors, undergraduates, parents, and counselors; high English proficiency and strong interest in EU/UK/US options.

## Timing and format
- **Typical months:** March (spring edition) and October (fall edition), with dates released ~3–4 months in advance; expect repeat editions in 2026.
- **Cities:** Bucharest, Cluj-Napoca, Iași, Timișoara; occasional virtual days in between.
- **Format:** In-person booths, seminar slots, and digital promotion packages; 1–2 days per city (Bucharest often the largest).

## Audience and expected reach
- 8,000–12,000 visitors per season across all cities; Bucharest drives the highest volume.
- Expect 600–1,000 qualified scans per top city with proactive staffing and localized messaging.
- Strong counselor presence in Bucharest and Cluj; good parent turnout in Iași and Timișoara.

## Pricing (indicative)
- €2,200–€3,800 per city depending on booth size/placement; bundled rates for multi-city routes.
- Add-ons: seminar slot, digital ads, email blasts, and lead packages; on-the-day printing/shipping extra.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** English-medium accredited programs; strong draws in medicine, CS/cyber, business/fintech; affordability vs UK; visa clarity.
- **UNIC Athens:** Proximity and fast travel from Romania; EU city experience; business/psychology/CS programs with Schengen lifestyle hook.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: book early (slots sell quickly); lock seminar topic per campus; set Romanian landing pages with UTMs; coordinate giveaways/light collateral per city.
- During: Romanian-speaking staff; aggressive QR + short-form capture; offer on-the-spot eligibility checks; invite counselors to briefings while in-city.
- Post: 24–48 hour follow-up sequences by intake/program; send webinar invites; segment leads by city and campus fit; circulate debrief to Educativa reps.

## Risks and notes
- High competition from UK/NL schools; differentiation on affordability and proximity is critical.
- Logistics: back-to-back city routing; plan travel buffers and backup collateral in each city.
- Dates can shift; monitor riuf.ro announcements and confirm invoice/payment deadlines.

# Integral Virtual Education Fair

## Overview
Online fair operated by Integral, usually positioned between the spring and fall physical tours. Delivers Romanian and regional leads with lower cost per lead and flexible staffing.

## Timing and format
- **Typical months:** May/June and November (1–2 days each).
- **Format:** Virtual booths with chat, video calls, webinar slots, and downloadable materials.

## Audience and expected reach
- 500–1,000 registrants; 150–250 engaged chats depending on staffing and webinar attendance.
- Strong participation from students outside Bucharest who may not attend in-person stops.

## Pricing (indicative)
- €500–€1,000 per virtual day; lead bundles sometimes included.
- Optional add-ons: featured placement, dedicated email blast, webinar promotion.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** Promote online info sessions and program taster videos; highlight merit scholarships and English-medium delivery.
- **UNIC Athens:** Emphasize fast travel, EU city appeal, and options for students seeking mainland EU but with English-taught degrees.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: set up Romanian-language booth copy; upload campus photos/videos; schedule live staff coverage for peak evening hours; prepare 20–30 minute webinar per campus.
- During: proactively invite visitors to chat; route technical questions to admissions; tag leads by interest (medicine, CS, business, psychology).
- Post: export chat logs and registrations; send immediate thank-you email with next-step booking link; retarget via social/Google to virtual attendees.

## Risks and notes
- Engagement drops without proactive outreach; staff should use chat triggers and scheduled webinars.
- Lead quality varies; use qualifying questions early (intake, GPA, language scores, budget).

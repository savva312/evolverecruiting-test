# ARACIS (Romanian Agency for Quality Assurance in Higher Education)

- last_checked: 2025-12-20
- website: https://www.aracis.ro/
- contacts: secretariat@aracis.ro; +40 21 206 76 00

## Mandate

Independent quality assurance agency responsible for external evaluation and accreditation of Romanian universities and study programs. Member of ENQA and EQAR, ARACIS sets standards, conducts periodic institutional reviews, and issues accreditation decisions published in the Official Gazette.

## Relevance to UNIC / UNIC Athens

- CNRED relies on ARACIS standards when benchmarking foreign degrees against Romanian cycles and credit expectations.  
- Romanian partner institutions considering joint programs with UNIC will need ARACIS approvals for program authorization and accreditation.

## Key processes and timelines

- Accreditation cycles typically run on a five-year schedule, with institutional and program-level evaluations that include self-evaluation reports, expert visits, and Council decisions.  
- Authorization for new joint or franchise programs requires a separate evaluation before student recruitment; processing can span multiple months depending on dossier completeness and scheduling of expert visits.

## Contact and engagement channels

- Secretariat: secretariat@aracis.ro for scheduling, procedural questions, and document routing.  
- Public information on standards and procedures is published on the ARACIS site (Quality Assurance Methodology section).  
- Institutions submit self-evaluation reports and supporting documents through ARACIS’s dedicated portals when applying for authorization or accreditation.

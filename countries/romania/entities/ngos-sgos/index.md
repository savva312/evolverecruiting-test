# Romania NGO/SGO landscape

This index highlights priority Romanian organizations that shape outbound mobility, scholarships, or advising for students considering international study (including UNIC/UNIC Athens). Profiles capture partnership fit and contacts; data is mirrored in `../../data/entities/ngos-sgos.csv`.

## Quick view

| Organization | Focus | Reach | Priority | Partnership status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [Liga Studenților Români din Străinătate (LSRS)](profiles/lsrs.md) | Scholarships, study abroad mentoring | National diaspora chapters (US, UK, EU hubs) | High | Warm network via diaspora mentors; outreach pending |
| [EducationUSA Romania](profiles/educationusa-romania.md) | Study-abroad advising (US) | Bucharest center; virtual national reach | Medium | No contact yet; joint info sessions under evaluation |
| [AIESEC Romania](profiles/aiesec-romania.md) | Youth development, internships abroad | 10+ university chapters nationwide | High | Strong existing mobility infrastructure; partner outreach recommended |
| [AFS Intercultural Programs Romania](profiles/afs-romania.md) | High-school exchanges, scholarships | National; strong in major cities | Medium | Contact form available; explore co-branded scholarship pilots |
| [Erasmus Student Network (ESN) Romania](profiles/esn-romania.md) | Peer support for Erasmus+ mobility | 15+ local ESN sections | Medium | Potential ambassadors/peer mentors; no outreach yet |
| [National Agency for Community Programs in Education and Vocational Training (ANPCDEFP)](profiles/anpcdefp-erasmus.md) | Erasmus+/European Solidarity Corps funding | National program agency | Medium | Formal SGO contact needed; align on credit transfer/short programs |

## How to use

1. Start with the profiles to understand mission, reach, and partnership hooks.
2. Use the CSV for quick filtering and to track contact status across runs.
3. Update `index.md` after each outreach cycle to keep prioritization current.

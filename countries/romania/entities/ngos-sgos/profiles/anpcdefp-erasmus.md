# National Agency for Community Programs in Education and Vocational Training (ANPCDEFP)

- **Type:** SGO (Romanian Erasmus+/European Solidarity Corps national agency)
- **Scope:** National

## Overview
ANPCDEFP administers Erasmus+ and European Solidarity Corps funding in Romania, setting mobility priorities, approving projects, and training institutional coordinators. It shapes grant availability for short-term mobility and can influence whether partner universities like UNIC/Athens are highlighted in Romanian HEI project pipelines.

## Programs and reach
- Manages Erasmus+ Key Action 1 (learning mobility) calls and quality assurance for Romanian HEIs and schools.
- Runs training and info sessions for international relations offices and school inspectors.
- Publishes guidance on scholarship stipends, recognition, and inclusion measures.

## Target students
University and vocational students benefiting from Erasmus+ mobility grants; secondary students participating in short-term mobility through school consortia.

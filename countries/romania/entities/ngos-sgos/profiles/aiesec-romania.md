# AIESEC Romania

- **Type:** NGO (student-run leadership and exchange network)
- **Scope:** 10+ university chapters (Bucharest, Cluj-Napoca, Iași, Timișoara, Brașov, Constanța, etc.)

## Overview
AIESEC runs global volunteer and internship exchanges that prepare Romanian students for international mobility. Chapters maintain corporate and NGO partnerships, coach students on applications, and provide pre-departure orientation.

## Programs and reach
- Global Volunteer and Global Talent exchanges sending Romanian students to short- and mid-term roles abroad.
- Career fairs and leadership conferences (e.g., National Congress) that aggregate mobility-minded students.
- Alumni network that mentors participants on scholarships and graduate study pathways.

## Target students
Romanian undergraduate students (18–24) with strong English proficiency seeking international internships or volunteer experiences and open to full-degree study abroad.

## Partnership fit (UNIC/UNIC Athens)
- Sponsor tracks at AIESEC national conferences focused on graduate study or 3+1 pathways at UNIC/UNIC Athens.
- Create a referral pathway from Global Talent returnees to UNIC master’s scholarships and fast-track applications.
- Recruit campus ambassadors from AIESEC local committee leaders to run info booths and webinars.

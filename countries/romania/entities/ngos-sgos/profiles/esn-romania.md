# Erasmus Student Network (ESN) Romania

- **Type:** NGO (student association supporting Erasmus+ mobility)
- **Scope:** 15+ local sections across Romanian universities

## Overview
ESN Romania provides peer support, buddy programs, and events for Erasmus+ inbound and outbound students. Local sections influence how students perceive study-abroad options and can amplify short-term mobility opportunities that funnel into degree decisions.

## Programs and reach
- Buddy and mentoring programs for outbound Erasmus+ nominees.
- Orientation events and fairs with university international offices.
- Social media campaigns highlighting scholarship and credit-transfer experiences.

## Target students
Undergraduate students selected for Erasmus+ exchanges and their peer networks who may consider full-degree study abroad after an exchange.

## Partnership fit (UNIC/UNIC Athens)
- Use ESN sections as micro-ambassadors for UNIC short programs and summer schools.
- Co-sponsor “study in Cyprus/Greece” info nights with international offices and ESN marketing channels.
- Offer ESN volunteers application fee waivers or travel grants to visit UNIC open days.

# AFS Intercultural Programs Romania

- **Type:** NGO (youth exchange charity)
- **Scope:** National network with strong presence in Bucharest, Cluj-Napoca, Iași, and Timișoara

## Overview
AFS Romania coordinates intercultural high-school exchanges and scholarships, preparing students for academic mobility. It provides selection, pre-departure training, host family placement, and returnee alumni engagement that often channels students into later university study abroad.

## Programs and reach
- Yearlong and semester exchanges funded through AFS scholarships and partner sponsorships.
- Pre-departure orientation on academic expectations, safety, and cultural adaptation.
- Alumni network (returnees) that volunteers as peer mentors and mobility advocates in schools.

## Target students
High-school students (15–18) seeking scholarship-backed semester/year exchanges; alumni considering international undergraduate degrees.

## Partnership fit (UNIC/UNIC Athens)
- Position UNIC/UNIC Athens as a follow-on pathway for AFS alumni who already have mobility experience.
- Co-develop scholarships that recognize AFS alumni leadership/service.

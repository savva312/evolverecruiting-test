# Liga Studenților Români din Străinătate (LSRS)

- **Type:** NGO (diaspora student network)
- **Scope:** National chapters plus diaspora hubs (US, UK, Western Europe)

## Overview
LSRS connects Romanian students studying abroad through mentorship, scholarship guidance, and advocacy on affordability and recognition of international degrees. It operates student-led chapters and runs annual events that spotlight scholarship pathways and support services.

## Programs and reach
- Annual LSRS Gala and mentoring program linking prospective outbound students with alumni already abroad.
- Country chapters (e.g., UK, US, France, Germany) host webinars on applications, scholarships, and visa processes.
- Publishes guides and webinars on funding sources, often featuring institutional partners.

## Target students
Romanian high school and university students exploring full-degree or exchange study abroad, especially scholarship-dependent candidates seeking peer mentoring.

## Partnership fit (UNIC/UNIC Athens)
- Position UNIC/UNIC Athens as affordable EU options in LSRS webinars and mentorship tracks focused on STEM, business, and health sciences.
- Source student ambassadors from existing Romanian cohorts to co-present with LSRS mentors.
- Offer co-branded scholarship micro-grants or travel stipends for LSRS mentees to attend virtual/physical open days.

# EducationUSA Romania

- **Type:** SGO (U.S. Department of State advising network, hosted by the Romanian-U.S. Fulbright Commission)
- **Scope:** Bucharest advising center with national virtual reach

## Overview
EducationUSA provides free, accredited advising on U.S. study options, scholarships, testing, and visa preparation. The Bucharest center reaches nationally through webinars and fairs, shaping the counseling landscape for internationally-minded Romanian students.

## Programs and reach
- One-on-one and group advising on scholarships, admissions timelines, and standardized testing.
- Annual EducationUSA fairs and “Study in the USA” webinars attracting scholarship-seeking students and school counselors.
- Collaborative sessions with American Corners and high-performing high schools.

## Target students
Romanian high school and undergraduate students seeking scholarships or financial aid for full-degree study abroad, plus counselors influencing high achievers in Bucharest, Cluj-Napoca, Iași, and Timișoara.

## Partnership fit (UNIC/UNIC Athens)

**One-paragraph summary:**  
BBIS is an independent international school in Bucharest’s District 2 offering an IB pathway (PYP authorized 2021, DP authorized 2017, MYP in candidacy) alongside a Lebanese national track. The school reports roughly 400 students (co-ed, ages 2–18) and English is the primary language of instruction. Fees are not published publicly, but the IB continuum positioning plus international branding place it in the premium Bucharest segment that signals ability-to-pay for UNIC programs. Contact channels are centralized via admissions@bbischool.ro and the main line (+40 744 309 199).

**Top opportunities:**

- Clear admissions and secretary mailboxes enable rapid counselor outreach; onsite visit windows published (08:30–15:10 Mon–Thu, shorter Fridays).  
- Lebanese programme + international mix suggests diaspora/expat families receptive to Cyprus/Greece proximity and accreditation messaging.

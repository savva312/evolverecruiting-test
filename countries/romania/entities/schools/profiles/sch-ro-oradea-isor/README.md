**One-paragraph summary:**  
ISOr is a non-profit British-curriculum school in Oradea delivering Key Stage 3–4 with Cambridge Lower Secondary and Cambridge IGCSE (First Language English 0500 plus sciences, maths, history, Spanish). 2025/26 fees for secondary sit in the €9,300–11,400 band plus €500 registration and €300 resources, indicating households able to fund €10k–€20k/year university options. The school markets a seamless progression to GCSEs/A Levels, but onsite Key Stage 5 delivery is not yet confirmed, so the current verified pipeline ends at Year 11. English-medium instruction and a published day schedule (08:15–14:50) support recruitment, but university outcomes and counselor contacts are not published and require direct confirmation.

**Top opportunities:**  
- Cambridge IGCSE track with First Language English positions students well for English-medium university entry.

- Non-profit positioning may welcome scholarship narratives and community-impact programming.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- KS5/A Level delivery not evidenced; senior students may transfer elsewhere for Years 12–13.

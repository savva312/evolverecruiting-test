**One-paragraph summary:**  
Established in 1994 by Lumina Instituții de Învățământ, ICHB runs two Bucharest liceu campuses (Colentina and Pallady) with math-informatics specialization, strong computer science/olympiad branding, and Romanian national curriculum delivered with significant English exposure. Published 2025–2026 tuition of €9,400 plus a €700 enrollment fee signals a premium payer base aligned to UNIC tuition bands. Dual campuses and Lumina network ties (Spectrum schools) create multiple entry points for outreach, but counselor contacts are not yet public and should be confirmed directly.

**Top opportunities:**  
- Published premium liceu fees (€9,400 + €700 enrollment) indicate strong ability-to-pay for UNIC programs, including Medicine.  
- Math-informatics orientation and olympiad culture match UNIC CS/Data/AI, cybersecurity, and engineering pitches; STEM clubs enable targeted workshops.  
- Lumina network reach (Spectrum schools + shared administration) can open warm introductions across campuses and regions.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- Counselor/decision-maker not visible online; access may require routing via general inboxes or Lumina central admin.  
- Romanian-curriculum focus means some families prioritize Romanian/EU public options; UNIC value proof (clinical placements, post-study pathways) must be explicit.  
- Two-campus structure (Colentina vs. Pallady) risks mixed messaging; outreach should be campus-specific on dates, logistics, and contacts.

**One-paragraph summary:**
Avenor College is a private, non-profit international high school in northern Bucharest delivering the Cambridge IGCSE and A Level pathway under dual Romanian and British accreditation. The school reports ~190 high-school students, 15 IGCSE subjects, and 15 A Level options, supported by a career guidance program with internships through 45 partner companies. Premium, grade-based 2025–2026 fees are published on the school’s site (exact amounts still to capture), signaling strong ability to pay for UNIC and UNIC Athens.

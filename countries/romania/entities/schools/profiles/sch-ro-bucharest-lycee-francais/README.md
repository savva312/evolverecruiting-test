**One-paragraph summary:**  
AEFE-accredited French lycée serving preschool through Terminale on a modern Sector 1 campus. The school follows the French national curriculum leading to the Baccalauréat, offers multilingual pathways (French medium with strong English, German, Spanish), and publishes premium upper-secondary fees (€10,150–€11,290 for 2025–2026). High outbound orientation toward France and wider Europe makes it a priority feeder for English-taught programs that balance reputation, EU recognition, and value.

**Top opportunities:**  
- Affluent, internationally mobile families already budgeting €10k+ annually for secondary tuition.  
- AEFE network credibility and French Bac alignment enable positioning UNIC/UNIC Athens as EU-recognized, Mediterranean options.  
- English proficiency reinforced through American International Section and multilingual offer.

**Top risks or constraints:**  
- Competitive pull from French universities and Grandes Écoles, plus UK/Netherlands.  
- Counselor bandwidth may prioritize long-standing partners; requires tailored, bilingual materials.  
- Fee transparency exists but visits likely gated to scheduled windows and AEFE protocols.

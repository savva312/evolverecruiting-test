# Competitors

Track universities and providers competing for Romanian students. Summaries should cover positioning, pricing, scholarships, partnerships, and marketing tactics.

**Scope and priorities (Romania)**

- Focus on medicine, dentistry, nursing, and business programs offered in English that are realistic alternatives to UNIC/UNIC Athens (Romania, Bulgaria, Hungary, wider EU).
- Capture clear price signals (annual tuition, entrance/application fees) and whether merit or need-based discounts are visible.
- Note admissions filters that shape conversion (language proficiency, science subject tests/interviews, GPA cut-offs).
- Prefer official university sources (fee schedules, methodology documents) over agency pages; include working links and `last_verified`.

- `profiles/` – competitor snapshots with differentiation notes and observed messaging.

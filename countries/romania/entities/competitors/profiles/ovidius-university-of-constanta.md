# Ovidius University of Constanța – Faculty of Medicine

- **Type:** Public university (coastal Romania)
- **Website:** <https://www.univ-ovidius.ro/>
- **Last verified:** 2025-12-22
- **Source:** [Bachelor tuition & admission criteria 2025–2026](https://admitere.univ-ovidius.ro/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/Licenta-Taxe-si-criterii-Admitere-2025-2026-Re-3.pdf).

## Positioning and overlap with UNIC/UNIC Athens

- Offers 6-year English-taught Medicine and Dentistry degrees; lower-priced alternative to Bucharest/Cluj schools and to UNIC’s English MD track.
- Regional draw for Dobruja/Black Sea students who want to study in English without leaving Romania; location and Romanian-language support can reduce perceived risk for parents.

## Pricing, scholarships, and cost signals

- Tuition (2025–2026 bachelor guide): **€7,500/year** for English-taught Medicine and **€7,500/year** for English-taught Dentistry (fee table lists “Medicină (în limba engleză)” and “Medicină dentară (în limba engleză)” at €7,500).
- Romanian-language Medicine shows higher local-currency amounts in the same table; no explicit scholarships or early-payment discounts noted in the published guide.

## Recruitment and conversion levers

- Admission is file-based with a **language competence test in English** for non-EU candidates (pass/fail) plus dossier scoring per the faculty methodology (same guide).
- Uses a centralized PDF guide for fees, criteria, and timelines; no visible reliance on agency networks in the published materials.

## Implications for UNIC/UNIC Athens

- Price anchor at €7.5k/year for English Medicine/Dentistry undercuts both UNIC campuses; compelling for cost-sensitive Romanian applicants who still want English delivery.
- UNIC can counter with international clinical placements, Mediterranean campus experience, and clearer scholarship/financial aid messaging to justify higher price points.

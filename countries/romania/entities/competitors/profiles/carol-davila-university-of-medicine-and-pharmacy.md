# "Carol Davila" University of Medicine and Pharmacy (Bucharest)

- **Type:** Public medical university (Romania)
- **Website:** <https://umfcd.ro/en/>
- **Last verified:** 2025-12-22
- **Sources:** [Tuition fees 2025–2026 (English/French programs)](https://umfcd.ro/en/wp-content/uploads/2025/INTERNATIONAL_STUDENTS/ADMISSION_2025/DOCUMENTS/Taxe_UMFCD_2025-2026_studenti_internationali_EN.pdf), [Entrance exam methodology 2025–2026](https://umfcd.ro/en/wp-content/uploads/2025/INTERNATIONAL_STUDENTS/ADMISSION_2025/DOCUMENTS/METHODOLOGY-FOR-THE-ENTRANCE-EXAMINATION-BACHELORS-DEGREE-ENGLISH-TAUGHT-PROGRAMS-ACADEMIC-YEAR-2025-2026.pdf).

## Positioning and overlap with UNIC/UNIC Athens

- Flagship Romanian medical school with long-standing brand recognition; competes for students seeking EU-recognized MD/DDS degrees delivered in English or French.
- English-taught Medicine and Dentistry programs (6-year) directly overlap UNIC’s English medical pathway and UNIC Athens’ health clusters; Pharmacy offered in French.
- Admission requires proof of English proficiency at **minimum B2** level plus science background per the 2025–2026 entrance methodology, creating a filter on non-native English speakers.

## Pricing, scholarships, and cost signals

- Tuition (international students, 2025–2026): **€10,000/year** for English-taught Medicine, **€10,000/year** for English-taught Dentistry, and **€8,500/year** for French-taught Pharmacy (fee circular, 2025–2026).
- Language test fee for admission: **€500** (same fee circular).
- No institutional scholarships advertised in the fee circular for international applicants; pricing parity between EU and non-EU candidates (paid in EUR or RON equivalent).

## Recruitment and conversion levers

- Uses an entrance examination plus file review; methodology requires English B2 proof and evaluates science subjects (biology/chemistry/physics) when selecting candidates.
- Published annual fee tables and methodology PDFs are the main information channels; no evidence of agent partnerships in the 2025–2026 documentation.

## Implications for UNIC/UNIC Athens

- Competes head-to-head on English-language Medicine and Dentistry with clear, stable tuition signaling; high brand equity inside Romania may anchor students domestically unless UNIC differentiates on international exposure, clinical placements in Cyprus/Greece, or student life abroad.
- The €500 language test fee and B2 requirement create friction that UNIC can counter with streamlined English-language admissions and early pre-screening support.

# Romania agent feeder candidates

Tiered roster of Romanian agents/counseling firms prioritized for UNIC Nicosia and UNIC Athens recruiting.

## Tiering approach
- **Tier 1 – Ready to activate**: Established credibility, multi-city reach, and clear EU-friendly messaging; align immediately with campus webinars and offer-holder handling.
- **Tier 2 – High potential**: Good reach or niche fit; requires proof of volumes, compliance review, or training before launch.
- **Tier 3 – Watchlist/validate**: Useful coverage or niche lane but higher compliance/quality risk; run pilots with tight guardrails.

## Next actions
- **Enable Tier 1**: Schedule UNIC Nicosia and Athens webinars, provide Romanian-language program sheets (medicine, CS, business/design), and share scholarship/tuition calculator.
- **Train Tier 2**: Run 60–90 minute counselor bootcamps with campus differentiation, admissions timelines, and advertising do/don’t guidance; pilot 10–20 leads per agent before scaling.
- **Control Tier 3**: Require compliance review, script approvals, and data-processing addenda before campaigns; start with small, monitored cohorts (≤10 leads) and exit criteria.

## Compliance watchpoints
- Align all agents on Romanian advertising rules for scholarship/funding claims; avoid implied guarantees.
- Require explicit GDPR consent for sharing leads between agents and UNIC; collect proof for fair-based leads.
- Demand sub-agent disclosure, especially around fair season and medicine entrance prep partners.

## 1. Romania Market Snapshot (Why Athens matters)

- **Demand signals.** Romania produces ~65,000 Bacalaureat graduates annually, with ~8–10% actively looking at overseas English-medium universities. Premium private/international liceu enrollment, particularly across Bucharest/Ilfov (~15,000 students), Cluj, Iași, and Timișoara, correlates strongly with ability to afford €9k–€15k tuition. See `countries/romania/reports/20251220-Romania High-School Feeder Map.md` for the tiered city map.
- **UNIC Athens positioning.** Appeals to students who want: (a) mainland EU capital lifestyle, (b) English-taught degrees in business, CS/data, psychology/design, and (c) proximity (1.5h flight) plus Schengen travel convenience. Pair with UNIC Nicosia for medicine/allied health and blockchain/fintech differentiators.
- **Conversion truths.** Families expect a €10–€13k total cost of attendance (tuition + living) and faster offer/deposit cycles than UK/Netherlands choices. Counselors and agents emphasize proof of recognition (HQA, DOATAP equivalence, EU Blue Card mobility) and structured arrival support.

## 6. Offerholder & Yield Workflow (Romania specific)

### Timeline (aligned to Bac calendar)
1. **Offer issued (Day 0).** Attach bilingual offer pack, deposit link, and campus comparison slide. Tag record as `segment=RO_Athens` or `RO_Nicosia` in CRM.
2. **Reminder Day 7.** Country recruiter WhatsApp + email; highlight seat caps (Medicine/allied health) and housing queue position. Reference deposit-deadline SOP.
3. **Standard deposit deadline Day 14 (Day 10 Medicine).** Admissions Ops sends automated reminder; escalate to recruiter for at-risk flag.
4. **Reminder Day 18.** Recruiter call parents + student; log notes.
5. **Escalation Day 21 (Day 14 Medicine).** Admissions Manager email + SMS; mention seat release.
6. **Seat release Day 24 (Day 17 Medicine).** Director sign-off; allow 72-hour reinstatement with proof of payment.

**Yield levers.**
- Quarterly webinars per `offerholder-and-yield/webinars.md` (welcome, visa/housing, program spotlights, parents/finance). Track attendance vs deposit conversion.
- Housing allocation triggered within 48h of deposit; share Athens neighborhood guide and arrival windows (`housing-and-arrival.md`).
- Pre-departure checklists localized to Romanian timeline (Bac diploma release mid-July, CNRED certification windows).
- Offerholder WhatsApp groups separated by campus; weekly FAQ digest.

## Creative hygiene and localization
- Always publish Romanian + English variants; Romanian first for TOF, English acceptable for international parents and masters.  
- Add captions to all video assets; prioritize vertical (9:16) 15–30s, plus 6-second cutdowns for YouTube retargeting.  
- On-screen text: tuition in EUR, scholarship % if available, and explicit campus (Athens/Nicosia).  
- Accessibility: keep safe zones for TikTok/Meta UI; contrast ratios ≥4.5:1.  
- Compliance: include GDPR consent text on forms and privacy link in captions; avoid claiming job placement guarantees.  
- Measurement: embed UTMs by channel/campaign/creative; use unique short links for influencer/Spark Ads to track lift.

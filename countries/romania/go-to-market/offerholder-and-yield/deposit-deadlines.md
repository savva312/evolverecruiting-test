# Deposit deadlines

- **Last updated:** 2025-12-20  
- **Owners:** Admissions Ops (UNIC Nicosia); Admissions Ops (UNIC Athens) — accountable; Country Recruiter Romania — support

## Romania-specific principles

- Align to Romanian secondary exams (May–June) and diploma release (mid–July); allow families ~2 weeks post-results to lock seats.  
- Medicine/limited seats require faster deposits to signal scarcity; business/CS allow slightly longer.  
- Deposit confirms eligibility to issue final acceptance/visa support; outstanding documents pause visa kits.

## Standard fall intake timeline (September starts)

| Step | Timing from offer | Owner | Notes/CTAs |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Offer issued | Day 0 | Admissions | Include deposit link (standard amount per program), bilingual (EN/RO) one-pager on next steps. |
| Reminder 1 | Day 7 | Country Recruiter | SMS + email; reiterate seat cap for Medicine/Nursing/Aviation; share tuition payment methods. |
| Standard deposit deadline | Day 14 (Medicine: Day 10) | Admissions Ops | Non-payment triggers “at-risk” flag; send pre-filled payment slip; offer call slots. |
| Reminder 2 | Day 18 | Country Recruiter | Call + WhatsApp; ask for intent (“will pay by ___”); log in CRM. |
| Escalation | Day 21 | Admissions Manager | If no proof of payment, call parent/guardian; warn of seat release date. For Medicine, escalate Day 14. |
| Seat release | Day 24 (Medicine: Day 17) | Admissions Director | Email seat release notice; allow 72h reinstatement if payment proof sent. |

## January/spring intake cadence

- Shorten by ~3–5 days because students already graduated.  
- Target Day 10 deadline for all programs; single escalation on Day 14 before seat release on Day 17.

## Visa/arrival dependencies (assumptions)

- Romanian/EU passport holders: **no entry visa** for Cyprus or Greece; still need enrollment letter + insurance proof for residence registration after arrival.  
- Non-EU residents in Romania: deposit and unconditional acceptance required to issue visa support letters; consulate booking may take 3–4 weeks.  
- Proof of deposit is required before issuing: final acceptance letter, accommodation confirmation, and arrival welcome pack.

## Escalation rules and channels

- **Risk triggers:** no payment proof by Day 14 (Day 10 Medicine) or missed WhatsApp read for 72h.  
- **Tier 1:** Country Recruiter call/WhatsApp + parent call.  
- **Tier 2:** Admissions Manager email from official inbox + SMS.  
- **Tier 3:** Director note warning of seat release; copy agent (if applicable).  
- Track all touches in CRM with timestamps; only Director can approve extensions beyond seat release window.

## Monitoring and reporting

- Weekly dashboard: deposits by program, % within SLA, at-risk list with owner + next touch date.  
- Run “Romanian high schools” filter to ensure post-exam cohorts receive the Day 7 reminder only after diploma results publish.  
- Add “Assumption check” column to confirm deposit amount and banking rails per campus each cycle.

## Counselor conversation checklist
- **Pre-call prep:** pull the school’s profile from `entities/schools/profiles/*` to confirm curriculum, fees, and counselor names; align persona to campus (Athens urban vs Nicosia residential).  
- **Campus fit:** use the [UNIC Nicosia programme guide](../../../../../UNIC/unicnicosia/programs/README.md) and [Athens catalogue](../../../../../UNIC/unicathens/programs.md) to map Medicine, CS/Data, Business, Law, or Design interest lanes.  
- **Affordability & financing:** walk families through tuition + living costs (Parent FAQ §1) and show scholarship levers via [scholarship folders](../../../../../UNIC/unicnicosia/scholarships/README.md).  
- **Recognition & visa:** reinforce CNRED recognition steps plus visa assumptions from the Parent FAQ; reference [housing & arrival](../offerholder-and-yield/housing-and-arrival.md) and the campus visa guides for non-EU passports.  
- **Timeline:** anchor on the [deposit workflow](../offerholder-and-yield/deposit-deadlines.md); set expectations for 48h document checks, 7-day offer SLAs, and 10–14 day deposits (10 for Medicine).  
- **Assets to leave behind:** Parent FAQ, counselor intro email, programme decks, scholarship sheet, and creative clips/QR from the [digital library](../digital-playbook/creative-library.md).

## Objection handling (Romanian context)
- **Cost:** highlight scholarship math, instalment options, and total cost savings vs UK/NL; reference Parent FAQ §1 and scholarship folders.  
- **Safety:** point to 24/7 security + orientation steps documented in [housing & arrival](../offerholder-and-yield/housing-and-arrival.md) and [student-life hubs](../../../../../UNIC/unicnicosia/student-life/README.md), [Athens student life](../../../../../UNIC/unicathens/student-life/README.md).  
- **Recognition:** outline CNRED recognition (30–60 days) and regulated pathways (Medicine, Nursing, Law) using Parent FAQ §5; offer to loop in Academic Recognition.  
- **Distance:** position <3h flights from Bucharest to Larnaca/Athens, flexible onboarding windows, and Romanian-speaking recruiter support.  
- **Visa/residence for non-EU families:** leverage [Cyprus visa guide](../../../../../UNIC/unicnicosia/visas/README.md) and [Greece visa guide](../../../../../UNIC/unicathens/visas/README.md), emphasizing document coaching + booking support.

## Process aids
- **Application steps:** Account creation → document upload → interview (if needed) → offer (≤7 days) → deposit (10–14 days) → visa/housing kit (48h) → onboarding; mirror the [deposit SOP](../offerholder-and-yield/deposit-deadlines.md).  
- **Parent communications:** send the [Parent FAQ](templates/parent-faq-ro-en.md) + [counselor intro email template](templates/counselor-intro-email-ro-en.md) within 24h of every visit, webinar, or WhatsApp Q&A.  
- **Interest tracking:** capture students in a shared CSV (name, campus interest, program, scholarship need, application month) and sync weekly with romania@unic.example; upload template to the school’s preferred drive.  
- **Creative follow-up:** embed testimonial clips, COA slides, and scholarship cards from the [creative library](../digital-playbook/creative-library.md#tested-angles-and-formats-seed-set); ensure Romanian captions/subtitles on video cuts.

# Romania playbook — Psychology, Counselling, and Behavioral

## Messaging angles to test
- Practical training hours and supervised placements.
- International recognition of counselling credentials.
- Access to research labs or community clinics.

## Channels to prioritize
- Partnerships with mental health NGOs and university counselling centers.
- Online communities focused on psychology and wellbeing in Romania.
- Webinars featuring faculty and alumni practitioners.

## Local partners or feeders
Track agencies, high-school counsellors, and clinical partners that can support placements or guest lectures.

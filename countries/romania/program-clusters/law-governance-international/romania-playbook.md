# Romania playbook — Law, Governance, and International

## Messaging angles to test
- EU law expertise and pathways to practice across member states.
- Diplomacy and international relations projects with experiential learning.
- Dual-campus mobility between Cyprus and Athens for regional exposure.

## Channels to prioritize
- Partnerships with law student associations and debate clubs.
- Webinars with alumni in legal practice, diplomacy, or EU institutions.
- Collaboration with NGOs and think tanks for guest lectures and case studies.

## Local partners or feeders
Map agencies, law prep schools, and civic organizations that can support recruiting events or internships.

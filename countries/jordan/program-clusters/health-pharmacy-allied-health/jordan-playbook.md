# Jordan playbook — Health, Pharmacy, and Allied Health

## Messaging angles to test
- Licensing pathways and equivalency for Jordanian practice.
- Clinical placement sites and mentorship.
- Small-cohort, high-contact teaching environments.

## Channels to prioritize
- Healthcare professional associations and forums.
- Webinars with practicing pharmacists, nurses, and therapists.
- Partnerships with vocational schools and science-focused high schools.

## Local partners or feeders
Note agencies with healthcare focus, hospital education offices, and secondary schools that supply health-track students.

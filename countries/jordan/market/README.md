# Jordan market overview

This folder centralizes market research for recruiting Jordanian students to UNIC and UNIC Athens. Use concise, sourced notes with links to data where possible.

Contents:
- `outbound-mobility.md` – student flows, destinations, and program preferences.
- `affordability-and-economics.md` – tuition affordability, household income signals, financing options.
- `education-system.md` – secondary pathways, grading, and curriculum structures.
- `exams-and-calendar.md` – national exams, application timelines, and school calendars.
- `recognition-and-licensure.md` – degree recognition and professional licensure considerations.

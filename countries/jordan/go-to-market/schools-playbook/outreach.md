# School outreach playbook (Jordan)
Last updated: 2025-12-22 | Owner: School outreach lead (TBD)

## What this is
Operational steps to (1) win counselor relationships, (2) deliver high-attendance school sessions, and (3) convert applicants for both **UNIC Nicosia** and **UNIC Athens**.

## Targeting priorities (where to start)
- **Tier 1: West Amman premium international schools** — highest ability-to-pay; strongest Medicine pipeline; prioritize counselor 1:1s + parent evenings.
- **Tier 1 adjunct: Madaba (King’s Academy)** — treat as “Amman-market” premium feeder; schedule early in the year before May/June exam congestion.
- **Tier 2: Irbid (north hub)** — bilingual/STEM/private schools; run 1–2 anchor events per term plus virtual clinics.
- **Tier 3: Zarqa (large population; mixed ability-to-pay)** — focus on English-track/private cohorts with international pathways; pre-qualify leads with counselors.
- **Tier 4: Aqaba (opportunistic coastal)** — bundle with fairs/partner trips; targeted sessions when already on-ground.

## Channel mix (Jordan norms)
- **Email first (always)** — formal intro + assets + 2 proposed dates; keeps a clean record for the counselor and the school.
- **WhatsApp follow-up (same day / next day)** — short text + optional 30–45s voice note; ask for a 10-minute call; avoid repeated pings.
- **Phone call** — confirm cohort fit (Tawjihi / IB / A-Levels / US diploma), audience mix (Medicine vs non-Medicine), and format (student session + optional parent evening).
- **In-person** — best for West Amman + first-time relationships; add a 15-minute counselor 1:1 before/after the student session.
- **Virtual** — webinars during exam-heavy weeks; monthly “Counselor Hour” to maintain the network.

## Per-school outreach sequence (21–28 days)
1. **Day 0–1: Intro email** — dual-campus positioning + 2 date options (student session + optional parent evening); attach one-pagers (avoid external links unless approved).
2. **Day 1–3: WhatsApp** — confirm receipt; request a 10-minute call; send a short voice note if appropriate.
3. **Day 3–7: Call** — align on:
   - target grades (G11–G12) and curriculum (Tawjihi / IB / A-Levels)
   - session type (Medicine-focused vs general programs) and language preferences
   - the best week to visit (avoid exam windows; see timing anchor below)
4. **Week 2: Confirm + promo kit** — poster + registration link; ask the counselor to push via school WhatsApp broadcast groups (opt-in compliant).
5. **Week 3: Deliver session** (45–60 mins + Q&A) — capture opt-ins and book follow-up clinics (virtual 1:1s for students/parents).
6. **Week 4: Conversion push** — recap + application checklist + deadlines; ask counselor for feedback and lock the next touchpoint.

## Messaging angles (tailor per city/school type)
- **Academic outcomes** — EU-recognized degrees; employability stats; internship pipelines in Cyprus/Greece.  
- **Affordability** — outline tuition vs. UK/EU comparables; highlight scholarships/discounts for Jordanian students (placeholder link).  
- **Mobility & safety** — 1-hour flight from Amman to Larnaca/Athens; campus safety; housing support.  
- **Program fit** — Medicine/MD, CS/AI, Business with English delivery; Athens options for students preferring mainland EU context.  
- **Process clarity** — rolling admissions, document list, English language requirements; emphasize counselor support and fast offer timelines.

## Timing anchor (Tawjihi + IB/A-Level)
Use: [Exams and academic calendar](../../market/exams-and-calendar.md)

- **Best counselor engagement windows:** Sep–Nov and Feb–Mar (lighter school load; easier to secure visits).
- **Avoid heavy-visit periods:** Ramadan/Eid weeks; **May (IB exams)** and **May/June (A-Level/IGCSE papers)**; **mid-June–early July (Tawjihi main session)**.
- **Peak conversion windows (prepare fast offers + scholarship decisions):**
  - **Tawjihi results (mid-July typical)** and **supplementary/resit results (late Aug typical)**
  - **IB May results (~6 July)**
  - **A-Level/IGCSE results (mid-August)**

## Travel cadence & coverage
- **Amman + Madaba** — weekly half-days; cluster 3–4 schools per trip; prioritize Tier 1 relationships.  
- **Irbid** — 1x per month; bundle a northern hub event with 1–2 school visits.  
- **Zarqa** — every 6–8 weeks; focus on pre-qualified English-track cohorts.  
- **Aqaba** — quarterly (or when already on-ground for fairs/partners).

## Minimum tracking (do not skip)
- Log every touch in CRM: date, channel, decision-maker, decision/status, next step, and agreed visit date.
- After each visit: attendance, opt-ins, top objections, and follow-up appointments booked.

# Digital channel strategy

Ticket: T-010 — Digital channel benchmark pack (Jordan)

## Priorities for Jordan + diaspora
- **Meta (Facebook/Instagram)** — primary volume driver for 17–24 with Jordanian + English splits; fast creative testing and lowest CPC/CPL.
- **Google Search** — captures high-intent traffic on brand/program and “study abroad Cyprus/Greece” terms; protects brand SERP in Jordanian and English.
- **TikTok** — efficient prospecting and social proof for STEM/medicine/business themes; strong video completions, especially before exam seasons.
- **YouTube** — awareness and consideration with campus/tour content; retarget for lower CPLs.
- **LinkedIn** — niche but effective for parents, counselors, and postgraduate audiences; keep tight budgets and use lead forms.

## Channel plays (actionable settings)
- **Meta (FB/IG)**  
  - Objectives: Traffic to LP for qualification, then Lead Gen forms for efficient CPL.  
  - Targeting: 17–24 in Jordan; add diaspora Greeks/Spaniards/Germans with Jordanian language and expats interest. Use Advantage+ placements; exclude current students.  
  - Creative: Carousels for tuition/fees vs Western Europe; Reels with student voiceovers; test Jordanian + English variants.  
  - Guardrails: Cap frequency to 3/week prospecting; retarget 7/14/30-day site visitors with heavier proof points.
- **Google Search**  
  - Campaigns: Brand (Jordanian + English), Program groups (medicine, business, CS, fintech), “study in Cyprus/Greece” intent.  
  - Bidding: tCPA using CPL guardrails; start with Maximize Conversions if volume <30/week.  
  - Ad assets: Jordanian headlines, sitelinks to campuses/fees/scholarships, callout on EU recognition. Add location extension for Nicosia/Athens.  
  - Negatives: filter K-12/primary keywords; exclude “free courses” if CPL spikes.
- **Google Display**  
  - Use for remarketing only until >150 conversions/month.  
  - Creative: responsive display with campus photos, Jordanian headline + English CTA.  
  - Frequency: cap at 5/day; exclude mobile app placements that drive low-quality clicks.
- **YouTube**  
  - Campaigns: In-Stream for reach; Video Action for retargeting site visitors and YouTube engagers.  
  - Targeting: custom segments around “study abroad Jordan/Cyprus”, “medicine degree Europe”, and visitors to competitor domains.  
  - Creative: 6–15s cutdowns with Jordanian captions; pin CTA to virtual tour or inquiry form.
- **TikTok**  
  - Objectives: Traffic for pixel learnings; Lead Gen forms once CTR >0.8%.  
  - Targeting: interests (STEM, Erasmus, scholarships, English learning), hashtags (#طلاب_الأردن, #الدراسة_في_اليونان, #studyabroadjo); whitelist education creators for Spark Ads.  
  - Creative: native-looking UGC; subtitles in Jordanian Arabic; show tuition + career outcome in first 3 seconds.
- **LinkedIn**  
  - Audiences: Parents (age 35–55, seniority mid+), school counselors, alumni in Jordan.  
  - Formats: Document ads (brochures in Jordanian/English), Lead Gen Forms with GDPR checkbox.  
  - Guardrails: small daily caps; monitor CPC/CPL weekly—keep only if downstream conversion is ≥2x Meta.

## Language + geography guidance
- **Split**: start 60% Jordanian / 40% English for undergrad; flip to 70% English for masters and diaspora.  
- **Geos**: Jordan nationwide with Amman/Irbid bid multipliers; diaspora clusters in Greece, Germany, Spain, UK with Jordanian language preference.  
- **Localization**: tuition, scholarships, and recognition claims must be in Jordanian for top-of-funnel; English acceptable for program depth pages and international parents.  
- **Landing pages**: duplicate Jordanian LPs per program; ensure <2s load and GDPR-compliant consent copy.

## Compliance, consent, and data handling
- Include GDPR consent language on all lead forms (Meta/TikTok/LinkedIn) and LP forms; link to privacy notice.  
- No retargeting windows beyond 180 days for minors; prefer 90-day membership.  
- Keep suppression lists for enrolled/opt-out leads; refresh weekly.  
- Store UTM and source parameters in CRM; log consent timestamp and channel.

## Cadence and optimization
- **Weekly**: refresh creative (at least 2 new Reels/TikToks), audit CPC/CPL vs guardrails, pause any ad set >120% CPL target.  
- **Bi-weekly**: rotate program angles (medicine, business, CS) based on intake calendars; expand negatives in Search.  
- **Monthly**: reallocate +/–15% budget toward top-two channels by qualified lead rate; verify pixel/GA4 events still firing after LP edits.

# Offerholder webinars

- **Last updated:** 2025-12-20  
- **Owner:** Country Recruiter Jordan (delivery) with Admissions Ops (logistics) and Faculty/Student Ambassadors (content)

## Sequence overview (Jordan cohorts)

| Touch | Timing | Topic | Speakers | CTA/Follow-up |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1. Welcome + next steps | Within 3 days of offer | Offer walkthrough, deposit process, Jordanian calendar fit, Q&A | Country Recruiter + Admissions Ops | Send slides + deposit how-to; 1:1 call booking link; WhatsApp group invite. |
| 2. Visa, docs, housing | 7–10 days pre-deposit reminder (or post-offer) | EU entry/residence assumptions, housing options (UNIC dorms/Athens private), insurance, arrival windows | Admissions Ops + Housing | Checklist PDF + “upload proof” form; set personal housing consultation slot. |
| 3. Program spotlight (clusters) | Week 3 after offer (or 1 week post-deposit) | Breakouts for Medicine, CS/Engineering, Business; faculty positioning vs Amman/Irbid options | Faculty lead + Jordanian student ambassador | Push recording + FAQ; collect “intent to enroll” poll; invite to labs/club Discords. |
| 4. Parents & finance clinic | Mid-July (Fall) / Mid-Nov (Spring) | Fees, payment rails, scholarships/early-bird incentives, safety/healthcare | Finance rep + Parent alumnus | Send tuition statement template; schedule tuition call if needed; confirm guardian contact. |

Assumptions: majority are EU passport holders; visa content focuses on residence registration + insurance, with a short segment for non-EU residents in Jordan.

## Operating notes

- **Cadence:** Send invites 72h before each session; same-day SMS reminder in Jordanian; post-event drip (T+24h and T+5d) with recording + CTA.  
- **Segmentation:** Run separate Q&A rooms for Medicine vs other programs; add Athens-specific breakouts when session includes both campuses.  
- **Language:** Slides bilingual (EN primary, Jordan secondary). Live Q&A can mix; provide translated FAQ doc.  
- **Platform:** Zoom with registration form that tags “Jordanian Offerholder,” program interest, and agent (if any). Enable auto-reminders + attendance export to CRM.  
- **KPIs:** Attendance rate >60% of registrants; deposit conversion uplift tracked vs non-attendees; time-to-deposit reduction measured per cohort.

## Risks and mitigations

- **Low attendance in July/August:** stack sessions early evening Jordan time (18:30–19:30) and post recordings within 12h.  
- **Medicine seat anxiety:** include “seat caps + deposit timelines” slide; live escalation path for payment proof.  
- **Athens vs Nicosia confusion:** open with comparison slide (tuition, housing, arrival docs) and provide campus-specific contact emails; route follow-ups within 24h.

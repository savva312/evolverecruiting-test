# Accreditation and Quality Assurance Commission for Higher Education Institutions (AQACHEI)

**Mandate**: Independent commission setting standards, accrediting Jordanian higher education institutions/programs, and evaluating quality benchmarks used by MoHESR for recognition of foreign providers and cross-border program delivery.

**Relevance to UNIC/UNIC Athens**: AQACHEI standards inform MoHESR decisions on recognizing foreign universities and distance programs. Demonstrating alignment with AQACHEI expectations (e.g., credit loads, on-campus delivery, faculty qualifications) strengthens equivalency outcomes for returning graduates and supports articulation or joint-program discussions.

**Key processes and timelines**
- Publishes accreditation criteria, institutional/programme accreditation decisions, and quality assurance manuals at `http://aqachei.jo`.
- Periodic reviews (usually 5-year accreditation cycles) guide which foreign institutions Jordan considers comparable; MoHESR may reference these when assessing foreign degrees.
- For cross-border partnerships, prior no-objection from AQACHEI/MoHESR is typically required before marketing in Jordan; review timelines run several months depending on documentation completeness.

**Contacts**
- Public enquiries via the AQACHEI website contact page (http://aqachei.jo) and in-person at the Amman headquarters.
- Official gazette publications of accreditation decisions provide additional contact points for specific programs.

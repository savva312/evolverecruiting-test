# Civil Status and Passports Department (CSPD) – Ministry of Interior

**Mandate**: Issues Jordanian passports, national IDs, and civil status documents. Controls passport renewals, emergency travel documents, and documentation needed for foreign visa applications.

**Relevance to UNIC/UNIC Athens**: Jordanian students require valid passports for Cyprus/Greece visa issuance. CSPD processing times and biometric appointment availability affect travel start dates; lost or expiring passports can delay embassy appointments.

**Key processes and timelines**
- Passport issuance/renewal applications submitted through CSPD service centers or eServices on `https://www.cspd.gov.jo`; standard processing typically 3–10 business days, with express channels available for urgent travel.
- Requirements include biometric enrollment, national ID, photos, and fee payment; minors need guardian consent.
- CSPD also authenticates civil status documents (birth certificates, family books) that may need subsequent Ministry of Foreign Affairs legalization for study abroad.

**Contacts**
- Service center locator and enquiry channels available through the CSPD portal: https://www.cspd.gov.jo
- General enquiries via the CSPD call center listed on the portal; escalation through Ministry of Interior public service desks.

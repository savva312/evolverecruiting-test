# Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research (MoHESR)

**Mandate**: National authority for higher education policy, scholarship management, and recognition/equivalency of non-Jordanian degrees via the Recognition & Equivalency Directorate (RCE). Oversees lists of recognized foreign institutions and sets rules for distance learning acceptance.

**Relevance to UNIC/UNIC Athens**: Jordanian applicants often need MoHESR equivalency of Cypriot/Greek degrees for public sector hiring or regulated professions. Admission teams should align offer letters and transcripts with RCE documentation requirements and steer students toward enrolling in recognized on-campus delivery to avoid distance-learning rejection.

**Key processes and timelines**
- RCE portal (`https://rce.mohe.gov.jo`) handles submissions for recognition and degree equivalency; applicants upload legalized credentials, study mode evidence, and pay fees online.
- Recognized universities/programs list published by MoHESR guides which foreign institutions are eligible; updates typically each academic year.
- Equivalency decisions generally issued within 10–20 working days after complete submission; delays common when needing additional proof of on-campus attendance or program duration.

**Contacts**

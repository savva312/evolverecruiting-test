# Middle East University (Amman, Jordan)

- **last_verified:** 2025-12-20  
- **sources:**

## Snapshot and target segments
- Private Amman-based university with broad undergraduate portfolio (business, pharmacy, allied health, engineering/design, IT, media, law). Hosts joint UK degrees and emphasises campus services near QAIA.
- Targets Jordanian and regional students wanting local private options with English/Arabic delivery; overlaps with UNIC/UNIC Athens for business, computing, pharmacy, and nursing seekers.

## Pricing and fee signals (undergraduate)
- One-time fees: **75 JD (≈110 USD)** application and **75 JD** refundable deposit.  
- Recurring per semester: **500 JD (≈705 USD)** registration (300 JD / 430 USD for summer), **30 JD (45 USD)** student service fee for non-Jordanians; health insurance currently **free**; placement tests **25 JD (35 USD)** each.  
- Transportation listed as free; tuition-per-credit/hour not published on the page—requires confirmation with admissions for full cost comparisons.

## Positioning vs UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Competes on local convenience, lower headline fees, and partnerships with UK degree providers; UNIC/UNIC Athens (see [programme catalogue](../../../../UNIC/UNIC%20Athens/programs.md)) should emphasise EU degree portability, English-medium clinical/business pathways, and international campus experience.  
- MEU’s allied health and pharmacy presence makes it a local alternative for students considering UNIC’s health offerings; transparent non-tuition fees help frame total cost-of-attendance discussions.

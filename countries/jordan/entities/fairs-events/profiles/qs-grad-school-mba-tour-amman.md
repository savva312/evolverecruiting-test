# QS World Grad School & MBA Tour – Amman

## Overview
QS runs combined master’s and MBA recruitment events in Amman with pre-scheduled meetings, school presentations, and admissions workshops. Attendees are pre-registered and screened for postgraduate interest, giving strong quality for both campuses’ graduate portfolios.

## Timing and format
- **Typical months:** October–November; mid-week evening events (4–9pm).
- **Location:** Central Amman hotels (Le Royal, InterContinental, or similar).
- **Format:** Table booths plus 20-minute info sessions, one-to-one meetings, and panel/Q&A slots.

## Audience and expected reach
- 500–900 pre-registered candidates; 250–450 check-ins on site.
- Mix of final-year undergraduates, young professionals (0–5 years experience), and returning alumni; high English proficiency.
- Lead quality: strong for postgraduate and scholarship-driven prospects.

## Pricing (indicative)
- €2,200–€3,500 per event depending on booth size and data package.
- Add-ons: lead downloads, email blasts, panel sponsorship, and post-event webinar slots.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** Stress affordable master’s with English-medium delivery, fintech/blockchain/cybersecurity strengths, and research and PhD pathways.
- **UNIC Athens:** Highlight proximity for weekend travel, EU business hub positioning, and Athens-based MBA/MSc programs for professionals seeking mainland EU exposure.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: request 2025 Amman slot and confirm if combined Grad School + MBA format; prepare scholarship/GMAT-waiver messaging; set booking link for 1:1 consults.
- During: prioritize scheduled meetings; capture work experience and intake preferences; invite candidates to campus-specific webinars within one week.
- Post: send same-day follow-up with tailored program sheets; segment leads (MBA vs MSc/MA/CS); arrange counselor/agent briefings for high-potential candidates.

## Risks and notes
- Evening timing compresses conversations—keep scripts concise.
- Competition from UK/EU MBAs can be intense; differentiate on tuition, ROI, and quick travel.

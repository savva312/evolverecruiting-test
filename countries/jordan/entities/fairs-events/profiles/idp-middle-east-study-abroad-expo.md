# IDP Middle East Study Abroad Expo – Amman (with virtual follow-up)

## Overview
IDP’s regional expo includes an Amman stop plus a virtual fair follow-up. Events are heavily marketed to IDP’s counseling database, producing a mix of school-leaver and undergraduate transfer leads with strong digital retargeting.

## Timing and format
- **Typical months:** November for Amman in-person; December virtual follow-up. Spring mini-fairs occasionally added based on demand.
- **Location/format:** Amman hotel ballroom (table-top booths, on-the-spot counseling) plus a 1–2 day virtual fair with live chat/video.

## Audience and expected reach
- 800–1,200 attendees in Amman; virtual fair adds 400–700 registrants.
- Strong pipeline of students considering UK/Australia but open to EU options with lower tuition; high digital engagement via IDP CRM.
- Lead quality: good top-of-funnel; needs close alignment with IDP counselors for conversion.

## Pricing (indicative)
- €1,500–€2,500 per in-person booth; €600–€1,200 virtual add-on with lead bundle.
- Sponsorships/email pushes priced separately; early-booking discounts common.

## UNIC vs UNIC Athens positioning
- **UNIC Nicosia:** Position as EU alternative with English-medium study and lower total cost than UK/Australia; emphasize recognition and clear visa steps.
- **UNIC Athens:** Lead with proximity, Schengen mobility, and English-taught business/tech options in a mainland EU city.

## Pre/during/post actions
- Pre: book Amman + virtual bundle; brief IDP counselors on admissions, scholarship bands, and Athens vs Nicosia routing; supply Arabic/English landing pages and QR codes.
- During: use short eligibility checks; push campus-specific webinars; tag leads by campus preference in IDP CRM export.
- Post: same-day follow-up with consult-booking links; request IDP retargeting pixels and nurture emails to Jordanian leads; schedule counselor refresher webinar within 2 weeks.

## Risks and notes
- Competition from UK/Australia providers can overshadow EU options; sharpen ROI and scholarship messaging.
- Lead exports may lag; set clear SLA with IDP team for daily files.

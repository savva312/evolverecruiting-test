# Education for Employment – Jordan (EFE-Jordan)

## Overview
- Local affiliate of the regional Education for Employment network.
- Mission: equip youth with demand-driven skills and connect them to employers through training and placement services.
- Relevance: strong bridge between recent graduates and employers; can channel employable candidates into UNIC upskilling pathways or postgraduate programs.

## Programs and reach
- **Job Training & Placement (JTP)** — soft skills, English for the workplace, and sector-specific training (retail, services, ICT) tied to employer vacancies.
- **Tech and digital programs** — coding, data, and digital marketing bootcamps often co-delivered with corporates.
- **Entrepreneurship support** — short programs on business planning and access to mentorship.
- Reach: national with heavy Amman footprint; works with refugee and vulnerable Jordanian youth.

## Target students
- Unemployed or under-employed youth (18–30), including university graduates needing employability polish.
- Refugees and vulnerable Jordanians seeking pathways to formal employment.

## Partnership fit for UNIC/UNIC Athens
- **Bridge programs**: bundle EFE soft-skills training with UNIC micro-credentials or online certificates, then **convert top performers** into degree applicants.
- **Employer-linked scholarships**: co-market UNIC degrees tied to corporate sponsors in the EFE network.
- **Career events**: guest lectures and virtual career days featuring UNIC faculty/employers.

# Queen Rania Foundation / Edraak

## Overview
- Queen Rania Foundation (QRF) is a non-profit focused on education quality and innovation; Edraak is its Arabic MOOC and blended-learning platform.
- Mission: improve learning outcomes and access through digital content, teacher development, and college/career readiness resources.
- Relevance: broad reach into schools and self-learners; valuable for top-of-funnel awareness, counselor enablement, and pre-arrival academic prep.

## Programs and reach
- **Edraak K-12 and skills MOOCs** — free online courses in STEM, English, soft skills, and test prep; millions of learners across the Arab world, with high uptake in Jordan.
- **College and career readiness content** — modules on study skills, CV/interviewing, and career exploration.
- **Teacher and counselor PD** — QRF initiatives that train educators on digital pedagogy and guidance.
- **Assessment and research pilots** — QRF-led evidence-based pilots in Jordanian public schools.

## Target students
- High school students and young adults using Edraak for English/STEM upskilling and college prep.
- Teachers/counselors who influence study-abroad decisions and can become advocates.

## Partnership fit for UNIC/UNIC Athens
- **Content partnerships**: publish UNIC-branded micro-courses on Edraak (e.g., intro to data science, study-in-Cyprus prep).
- **Counselor training**: co-host webinars with QRF for school counselors highlighting scholarships and visa guidance.

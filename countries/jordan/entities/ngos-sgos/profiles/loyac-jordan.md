# LOYAC Jordan

## Overview
- Non-profit youth development organization (Jordan chapter of regional LOYAC network).
- Mission: empower young people through leadership, volunteering, arts, and employability programs.
- Relevance: active volunteer and internship placement base in Amman; strong channel for community-minded students and counselors.

## Programs and reach
- **Internship and workplace experience** — places students in summer internships with corporate partners; includes soft-skills workshops.
- **Community service and leadership** — volunteering projects that attract high school and university students.
- **Arts and entrepreneurship labs** — creative programs (film, design, social entrepreneurship) run with mentors.
- Reach: primarily Amman, with outreach projects in other governorates; mixed demographic (high school, university, early-career).

## Target students
- High school and university students seeking leadership, community engagement, and first work experience.
- Youth interested in creative industries, social entrepreneurship, or service-learning.

## Partnership fit for UNIC/UNIC Athens
- **Service-learning pipeline**: co-develop volunteering projects with UNIC branding and application workshops.
- **Summer intensives**: invite LOYAC cohorts to 1–2 week academic tasters (business, media, CS) with scholarship vouchers.
- **Counselor/mentor sessions**: train LOYAC coordinators on UNIC admissions and scholarships to drive referrals.

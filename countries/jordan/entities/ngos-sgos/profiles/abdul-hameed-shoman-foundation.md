# Abdul Hameed Shoman Foundation (AHSF)

## Overview
- Philanthropic arm of Arab Bank focused on knowledge, culture, and innovation.
- Runs national scholarship and research grant programs plus the Shoman Cultural Center.
- Relevance: offers funding that can offset tuition/living costs and hosts innovation events where UNIC can position programs.

## Programs and reach
- **Shoman Scholarships** — tuition support for Jordanian undergraduates and postgraduates in priority fields (STEM, business, culture/arts); periodic calls for applicants.
- **Research and Innovation Grants** — funding for applied research, startups, and maker/innovation challenges.
- **Cultural and youth programs** — reading initiatives, film/culture events that draw school counselors and youth groups.
- Geographic reach is national with strong Amman presence; application volume typically exceeds award slots, signaling unmet demand.

## Target students
- Jordanian students with strong academics seeking funding for university (domestic or regional).
- Early-career researchers/innovators who need scholarships or co-funding for graduate studies.

## Partnership fit for UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Co-design a **joint scholarship track** for bachelor’s/master’s applicants in business, CS/data, and health.
- Sponsor **innovation challenges** with prizes that include partial scholarships or summer school seats at UNIC.
- Host **information sessions** at Shoman Cultural Center to access counselors and scholarship seekers.

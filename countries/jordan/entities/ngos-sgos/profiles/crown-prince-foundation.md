# Crown Prince Foundation (CPF)

## Overview
- Semi-governmental youth foundation launched in 2015 under the patronage of HRH Crown Prince Al Hussein bin Abdullah II.
- Mission: empower Jordanian youth with skills, innovation opportunities, and civic engagement pathways.
- Relevance: runs national STEM/innovation programs and scholarships that can channel high-performing students toward UNIC/UNIC Athens, especially in engineering/tech.

## Programs and reach
- **Al Hussein Technical University (HTU) pathways** — applied engineering and technology degrees with industry labs; source of diploma/associate graduates seeking top-up or transfer options abroad.
- **FabLabs and STEM initiatives** — maker spaces in Amman, Irbid, Karak; robotics and space programs (incl. NASA internship pipeline).
- **Scholarship and excellence tracks** — merit awards and sponsorships for high-achieving students in STEM and vocational fields; occasional international exchange placements.
- **Volunteer and leadership programs** — youth civic engagement initiatives that build counselor/ambassador networks.

## Target students
- High-achieving STEM and vocational talent from across Jordan (HTU students, FabLab cohorts, NASA/robotics participants).
- Youth from governorates outside Amman seeking upward mobility and international exposure.

## Partnership fit for UNIC/UNIC Athens
- Offer **co-branded STEM scholarships** for top HTU diploma holders to complete bachelor’s at UNIC/UNIC Athens (credit transfer/top-up).
- Run **summer bootcamps or hackathons** hosted by CPF FabLabs with UNIC faculty mentors; include admissions counseling sessions.
- Create **ambassador/counselor training** with CPF’s youth leaders to generate steady referral flow.

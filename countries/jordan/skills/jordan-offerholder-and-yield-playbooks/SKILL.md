# Jordan addendum: offerholder & yield playbooks

Follow `/skills/offerholder-and-yield-playbooks/SKILL.md` for cadences and data capture. Layer these Jordan-specific notes.

## Channel and language norms
- Offer Arabic-first messaging for students/parents; provide English versions for international school cohorts.
- WhatsApp is common but requires opt-in; keep formal updates over email/phone and log consent for chat groups.

## Timing anchors
- Reduce intensity during Tawjihi exams and results weeks; resume heavier cadences once scores are released.
- Address housing and travel planning early, especially for Athens-bound students; for Nicosia-bound students, outline visa steps without promising expedited processing.

## Compliance and documentation
- Do not store passport scans or national IDs in the repo. Track application IDs, contact info, and checklist status only.
- Record every touch with channel, language, and owner in the country data model; capture consent source/time for parents added to group chats.

## Objection handling to localize
- Cost: keep pricing in EUR and avoid quoting JOD as fixed; include FX date if providing an estimate.
- Recognition/licensure: rely on the Jordan recognition and licensure skill (`countries/jordan/skills/jordan-recognition-and-licensure/`) before making claims.

## 2. Executive functional description (what the room is)

The space is a **hybrid culinary demonstration theater** combining:
1. A **tiered lecture amphitheater**: fixed rows of benches and seats optimized for sightlines to a front demonstration counter and projection screen.
2. A **commercial-grade demonstration kitchen line**: stainless steel work surfaces, a ventilated cooking suite under a Type I style canopy hood (inferred), and at least one high-capacity oven unit suitable for live production and teaching.
3. A **multi-modal instructional system**: projection, large-format display(s), instructor workstation, and likely camera capture for live magnification and/or recording (camera observed).

The layout prioritizes:
- **Visibility**: stepped seating and an elevated visual focal plane at the front counter.
- **Hygiene and robustness**: stainless steel kitchen envelope and cleanable floor finishes.
- **Instructional agility**: ability to alternate between live demo, projection-based theory, and Q&A, with learners seated at work surfaces suitable for note-taking and potentially tastings.
